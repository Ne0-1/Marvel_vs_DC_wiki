'''Sam Wilson''' is a [[character (arts)|character]] portrayed by [[Anthony Mackie]] in the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]] (MCU) [[film franchise]], based on the [[Marvel Comics]] character of the same name and known commonly by his [[alter ego]], '''[[Falcon (comics)|Falcon]]'''.
