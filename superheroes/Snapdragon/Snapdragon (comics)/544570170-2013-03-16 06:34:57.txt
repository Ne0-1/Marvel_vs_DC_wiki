'''Snapdragon''' ('''Sheoke Sanada''') is a [[Japan]]ese [[supervillain]] in the [[Marvel Comics Universe]].  She first appeared in ''[[Marvel Fanfare]] vol. 1'' #12 (1984) and was created by [[Ralph Macchio (comics)|Ralph Macchio]] and [[George Pérez]].

==Fictional character biography==
A skilled martial artist, Snapdragon was hired to defeat the [[Black Widow (Natalia Romanova)|Black Widow]] in [[Japan]].{{issue|date=December 2012}}  She succeeded on her mission, but Black Widow soon regained her composure and quickly dispatched Snapdragon.{{issue|date=December 2012}}  Later, Snapdragon joined [[Superia]]'s [[Femizons]] and became one of her lieutenants.{{issue|date=December 2012}}  There, she found long-time enemy [[Diamondback (comics)|Diamondback]].{{issue|date=December 2012}}  The two were trained by [[Anaconda (comics)|Anaconda]] in [[Taskmaster]]'s Academy,{{issue|date=December 2012}} but Diamondback had subsequently dishonored Snapdragon in some form.{{issue|date=December 2012}}  While on the S.S. ''Superia'' cruise ship, Snapdragon apprehended Diamondback and threw her off, leaving her to drown.{{issue|date=December 2012}}  Diamondback eventually recovered and received the super-soldier serum in one of her misadventures, and during their rematch, choked Snapdragon to death.{{issue|date=December 2012}}  Her body was returned to Japan and cremated by her brother, Kono.<ref>''Captain America'' #413</ref>

Later, the name Snapdragon was taken by [[Diamondback (comics)|Rachael Leighton]] after she began working for [[Superia]] as payment for finding a cure for the glitch in the super-soldier serum.<ref>''Avengers'' #387</ref>

A woman calling herself Sheoke Sanda appeared in ''Moon Knight''.<ref>''Moon Knight'' #2 2011</ref> If this is the original Snapdragon details of her resurrection are unknown.

==Powers and abilities==
Snapdragon possesses no superhuman abilities other than a protective suit and a helmet with infrared sensors. She is a master in hand-to-hand combat, and skilled with various forms of weaponry.
