'''Idunn''' is a [[fictional character]] appearing in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe]], based on the [[Iðunn]] of [[Norse mythology|Norse myth]]. [[Odin (Marvel Comics)|Odin]] appointed Idunn Keeper of the Golden Apples, whose mystical properties retard the aging process of the [[Asgardians (Marvel Comics)|Asgardians]].

==Publication history==
Idunn first appeared in ''[[Journey into Mystery]]'' #114 (March 1965), and was adapted from mythology by [[Stan Lee]] and [[Jack Kirby]].

The character subsequently appeared in ''Thor'' #294-295 (April–May 1980), 325-326 (November–December 1982), ''Marvel Fanfare'' #13 (March 1984), and ''Avengers: Domination Factor'' #3 (January 2000).

Idunn appeared as part of the "Asgardians" entry in the ''[[Official Handbook of the Marvel Universe|Official Handbook of the Marvel Universe Deluxe Edition]]'' #1.

==Fictional character biography==
According to ''[[Marvel Fanfare]]'' #13, Idunn is married to [[Bragi (Marvel Comics)|Bragi]].

Idunn appears at Thor's funeral at the end of Fear Itself alongside Freyja and [[Gaea (Marvel Comics)|Gaea]].<ref>''Fear Itself'' #7.2 : Thor</ref>
