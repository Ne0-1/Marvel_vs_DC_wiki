'''Tildie Soames''' is a [[fictional character]], in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]] of the [[X-Men]]. She first appeared and featured prominently in the first story arc of the monthly series ''[[Astonishing X-Men]]'' in 2004.

==Fictional character biography==
Tildie's powers manifested as she was asleep in her mother's bed. She was having a nightmare that came to life. She killed her mother who was sleeping beside her. Her father rushed in and was also killed. The cops were called and came to her house to see what was happening. Officer Hoyt was killed in the same way as her parents. Tildie was apprehended and taken to Benetech Labs, where she was used as a guinea pig for the "Cure" by Dr. [[Kavita Rao]]. Tildie lost her powers and stayed at the labs with Kavita.{{issue|date=November 2011}}

One night, [[Ord (comics)|Ord]] of the Breakworld stole her and used her as a hostage.{{issue|date=November 2011}} She was successfully taken by the [[X-Men]] and returned to Benetech.{{issue|date=November 2011}}

==Powers and abilities==
Her nightmares can manifest into real monsters. At the same time, Tildie is inside the monster when asleep.

==Other media==
===Television===
* Tildie Soames appears in the ''[[Wolverine and the X-Men (TV series)|Wolverine and the X-Men]]'' episode "Battle Lines" voiced by [[Danielle Judovits]]. She is shown locked up in a sealed box that was watched over by Kavita Rao at the Mutant Response Division's Central Facility. When [[Mystique (comics)|Mystique]] reported the problem, [[Magneto (comics)|Magneto]] sent [[Juggernaut (comics)|Juggernaut]] to break the box open and implant her with a device to make her lose control over her powers. A monster manifested from Tildie's nightmares, knocked Juggernaut into the city and then went on a rampage. During battle, [[Wolverine (comics)|Wolverine]] discovered her in the monster and [[Emma Frost]] was able to infiltrate her mind. [[Rogue (comics)|Rogue]] absorbs the abilities of [[Kitty Pryde|Shadowcat]] and Juggernaut and uses them to enter the monster. Once Rogue got into the monster, she removed the device as Tildie tells her that she can't stop the monster. Rogue then uses her power on Tildie which stops the monster. Tildie is currently living with the X-Men. In "Backlash," Shadowcat looked after Tildie so that she wouldn't go monster while the X-Men are away. She managed to keep the girl calm and hidden while she took care of an MRD raid. In "Foresight" Pt. 3, Tildie appeared with the rest of the X-Men and met with [[Professor X]].
