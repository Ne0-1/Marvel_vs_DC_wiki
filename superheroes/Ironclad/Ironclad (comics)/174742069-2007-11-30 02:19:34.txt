'''Ironclad''' ('''Michael "Mike" Steel''') is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Comics Universe]] whose first appearance was in ''[[Incredible Hulk]]'' vol. 2 #254. He is a member of the super-villain group, the [[U-Foes]].

==Fictional character biography==
Mike Steel was an engineer and pilot before gaining his superhuman powers.

Mike was identified as one of the 142 registered superheroes who appear on the cover of the comic book ''[[Avengers: The Initiative]]'' #1. <ref>[http://www.marvel.com/news/comicstories.947 ''Avengers: The Initiative'' #1 Character Map]</ref>

Though a registered hero, Ironclad is later seen as part of the [[Hood (comics)|Hood]]'s criminal organization who plan to take advantage of the split in the superhero community caused by the Superhuman Registration Act.

==Powers and abilities==
'''Ironclad''' has a metal form which gives him tremendous durability and resistance to most forms of injury, and also incredible superhuman strength.  He can also increase his own body's density to increase his strength and endurance.

==Other comics characters named Ironclad==
*In the [[Ultraverse]], [[Ironclad (Ultraverse)|Spencer Collins]] was known as ''Ironclad''.  He was a member of [[Ultraforce (comics)|Ultraforce]] and first appeared in ''Firearm'' #11.  He was killed by [[Rafferty (comics)|Rafferty]] in ''Ultraverse Premiere'' #5 and revived in ''Prototype'' #13.
