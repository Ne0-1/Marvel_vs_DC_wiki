'''Ironclad (Michael "Mike" Steel)''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Comics Universe]] whose first appearance was in ''[[Incredible Hulk]]'' (2nd series) #254. He is a member of the super-villain group, the [[U-Foes]].

==Powers==
'''Ironclad''' has a metal form which gives him tremendous durability and resistance to most forms of injury, and also incredible superhuman strength.  He can also increase his own body's density to increase his strength and endurance.

==Notes==
*In the [[Ultraverse]], [[Ironclad (Ultraverse)|Spencer Collins]] was known as ''Ironclad''.  He was a member of [[Ultraforce]] and first appeared in ''Firearm'' #11.  He was killed by [[Rafferty (comics)|Rafferty]] in ''Ultraverse Premiere'' #5 and revived in ''Prototype'' #13.
