'''Commander Kraken''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Universe]].

==Publication history==
Commander Kraken first appeared in ''[[Sub-Mariner]] #27'' (July 1970), and was created by [[Roy Thomas]] and [[Sal Buscema]].

The character subsequently appeared in ''The Cat'' #3 (April 1973), ''Iron Man'' #93 (December 1976), and was killed by the [[Scourge of the Underworld]] in ''Captain America'' #319 (July 1986).

Commander Kraken received an entry in the ''[[Official Handbook of the Marvel Universe|Official Handbook of the Marvel Universe Deluxe Edition]]'' #16.

==Fictional character biography==
Commander Kraken was a self-styled modern-day [[pirate]] who first encountered and fought [[Namor|Namor the Sub-Mariner]]. He later underwent plastic surgery which drastically altered his appearance, sold his services to [[HYDRA]] and became their Naval Action Division Leader.{{Issue|date=April 2009}} He fought Namor and [[Iron Man]] but lost against them.{{Issue|date=April 2009}}

Later, Commander Kraken was invited to a meeting organized by Gary Gilbert, who wanted to discuss counter-measures against the threat by the [[Scourge of the Underworld]] at the "Bar With No Name". Kraken attended and was shot dead along with all the other criminals present by the Scourge himself, who had infiltrated the meeting as the bartender.<ref>''Captain America'' #319 (July 1986)</ref>

Someone that looks like Commander Kraken is seen attending a meeting of the heads of HYDRA. This hasn't been confirmed if the original was brought back to life or if it's a new Commander Kraken.<ref>''Secret Warriors #2''</ref>

Commander Kraken is among the dead characters seen in Erebus when [[Hercules (Marvel Comics)|Hercules]] travels to the Underworld.<ref>''Incredible Hercules'' #129</ref>

==Powers, abilities, and equipment==
Commander Kraken had a hook on his left hand that was capable of emitting an electrical shock, but HYDRA later replaced this with a functional bionic hand. His left leg was also bionic, and allowed him to fly. His electro-sword was capable of firing electricity and could reflect force and energy attacks.

Commander Kraken also used squid-shaped submarines with tentacles.
