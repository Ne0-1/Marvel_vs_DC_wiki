'''Michelle Jones''' is a [[Character (arts)|character]] portrayed by [[Zendaya]] in the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]] (MCU) [[film franchise]], based on the [[Marvel Comics]] character [[Mary Jane Watson]], and is known commonly as '''MJ'''. She is a student at Midtown High, Peter Parker's school. She is one of Peter's best friends, and after [[Liz Toomes]] leaves Peter, Michelle and Peter start a romantic relationship. Michelle is snarky and coy, but also kind and friendly. She often acts as the voice of reason, and she provides the troubled Peter with much-needed comfort and advice. As of 2019, the character has appeared in 2 films of the series.
