'''''Cinder and Ashe''''' is a four issue [[comic book]] [[limited series|mini-series]] published by [[DC Comics]] in [[1988]]. The series was written by [[Gerry Conway]] and drawn by [[José Luis García-López]]. The story follows the two partners in a [[private investigator|private investigation]] firm. Ashe is a [[United States|U.S.]] [[Vietnam War]] veteran. Cinder is the child of an African-American soldier and a Vietnamese woman. The series is set in [[New Orleans, Louisiana]] with [[flashback (literary technique)|flashbacks]] to [[Vietnam]]. The series was labelled "[[Adult comics|Suggested for Mature Readers]]" to indicate that its content may be inappropriate for young children.
