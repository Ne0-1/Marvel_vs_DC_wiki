'''Ms. Gsptlsnz''' ({{pron-en|ɡɪzˈpɪtlɛznɛz}} {{respell|giz|PIT|lez-nez}}), sometimes called '''Gizbie''', is a [[fiction]]al [[character]] who appears in [[DC Comics]]' [[Superman: The Animated Series]]. 

She was created by [[Jerry Siegel]] and [[Joe Shuster]], and first appeared in ''[[Superman: The Animated Series]]'' in the 1990s as the girlfriend of Mr. [[Mxyzptlk]]. She only appeared in the episodes "Mxyzpixilated" and "Little Big Head Man", In the same scene where Mxy gives his name pronunciation and turns himself into a comicstrip character. The strip is credited to Siegel and Shuster.

Ms. Gsptlsnz is voiced by [[Sandra Bernhard]].
