'''Thog the Nether-Spawn, Overmaster of Sominus''' is a [[fictional character]] appearing in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]].  He is a [[Demons (Marvel Comics)|demon]] who has clashed with the [[Man-Thing]].  He first appeared in [[Adventure into Fear]] #11, [[Steve Gerber]]'s first work for Marvel, illustrated by [[Val Mayerik]].  Gerber himself, along with Man-Thing, were integral in defeating Thog in his first attempt to take over the universe.  [[Chris Claremont]], in the form of [[Man-Thing]], defeated him in his second.
