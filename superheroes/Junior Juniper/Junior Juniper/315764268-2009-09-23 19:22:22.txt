'''Jonathan "Junior" Juniper''' is a [[fictional character]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] Universe. His first appearance was in ''Sgt. Fury and the Howling Commandos vol. 1 #1''.

==Fictional character biography==
'''Jonathon "Junior" Juniper''' was a founding member of the original [[Howling Commandos]] and fought alongside the team during [[World War II]].  He was the youngest on the team as he was still attending an unnamed [[Ivy League]] college before he enlisted in the [[United States Air Force|Air Force]].  Juniper was later transferred from the Air Force to the Commandos because he had flown [[B-17 Flying Fortress]] in bombing raids as a tail gunner.  He was the grand-uncle of [[S.H.I.E.L.D.]] agent [[Roger Juniper]].  In ''Sgt. Fury and his Howling Commandos'' #4 he was killed.<ref>{{Comic book reference | writer=[[Stan Lee|Lee, Stan]] | penciller=[[Jack Kirby|Kirby, Jack]] | inker=[[George Bell|Bell, George]] | story=Lord Ha Ha's Last Laugh | title=[[Sgt.
