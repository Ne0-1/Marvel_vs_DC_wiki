The '''Lurking Unknown''' is a [[fictional character]] appearing in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]].  He is a [[Demons (Marvel Comics)|demon]], one of the [[Fear Lords (comics)|Fear Lords]], who has clashed with [[Doctor Strange]] and [[Thor (Marvel Comics)|Thor]].
