The '''Lurking Unknown''' is a [[fictional character]] appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]]. He is a [[Demons (Marvel Comics)|demon]], one of the [[Fear Lords (comics)|Fear Lords]], who has clashed with [[Doctor Strange]] and [[Thor (Marvel Comics)|Thor]]. The character first appeared in ''Thor'' #136 (Jan 1967).

==Fictional character biography==
[[Odin (comics)|Odin]] had his vizier summon the Lurking Unknown and locked [[Jane Foster (comics)|Jane Foster]] in a darkened chamber as part of a test of her worthiness to marry [[Thor (Marvel Comics)|Thor]]. Terrified, she called out for help from Thor, who burst into the room and defeated the Lurking Unknown. A rock troll struck the tuning fork which summoned the Lurking Unknown, and Thor fell under its fear power.  Thor was aided by [[Sif (comics)|Sif]] in defeating the Lurking Unknown, causing it to return to its realm.{{issue|date=February 2013}}

The Lurking Unknown was later summoned to the Halls of Fear to join the other Fear Lords in a plot to gain control of Earth. [[Doctor Strange]] was able to defeat the Lurking Unknown and imprison him within a magical shield.{{issue|date=February 2013}}

The Fear Lords later met to discuss the Serpent's attacks on Earth.{{issue|date=February 2013}} [[Loki (comics)|Loki]] tricked them into a meeting to tell them about [[Nightmare (Marvel Comics)|Nightmare]]'s scheme to harvest the fear brought by the Serpent to become the new King of Fear, leading to a battle that resulted in a stalemate.{{issue|date=February 2013}}
