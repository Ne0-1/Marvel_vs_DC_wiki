'''Tyrak''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Universe]]. He is an [[Homo mermanus|Atlantean]] warrior, serving in the army of the conqueror [[Attuma]]. He rivals Attuma's strength and is able to enhance it further by increasing his height to a maximum of 40 feet. He has served in a number of missions for Attuma, and has fought the [[Avengers (comics)|Avengers]] on more than one occasion.
