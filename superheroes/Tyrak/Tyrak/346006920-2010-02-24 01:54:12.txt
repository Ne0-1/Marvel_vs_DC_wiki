'''Tyrak''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Universe]].

==Fictional character biography==
He is an [[Homo mermanus|Atlantean]] warrior, serving in the army of the conqueror [[Attuma]]. He has served in a number of missions for Attuma, and has fought the [[Avengers (comics)|Avengers]] on more than one occasion.{{Issue|date=February 2010}}

==Powers and abilities==
He rivals Attuma's strength and is able to enhance it further by increasing his height to a maximum of {{convert|40|ft|m}}.
