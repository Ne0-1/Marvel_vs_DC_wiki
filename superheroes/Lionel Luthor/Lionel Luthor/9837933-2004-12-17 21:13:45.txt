'''Lionel Luthor''' is [[Lex Luthor]]'s father in [[Superman]] [[mythology]]. He is a recent addition to the franchise, first appearing in the ''[[Smallville (TV series)|Smallville]]'' TV series, in which he is played by [[John Glover (actor)|John Glover]].

In the ''Superman'' comics, Luthor and his wife were killed in a car accident perpetrated by their son. Lex had taken a large [[life insurance]] premium out on the couple which Lex used to start [[LexCorp]].
