'''Professor Power''' ('''Anthony Power''') is a [[fictional character]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] Universe. His first appearance was in ''Marvel Team-Up vol. 1 #117''.

==Fictional character biography==
Anthony Power was born in [[Norfolk, Virginia]].  A former criminal mastermind, he was also the former ally of [[Morgan MacNeil Hardy]] and [[August Masters]] as well as the founder of the [[Secret Empire]].  He transferred his mind into his son's body after his son went insane.

Among the superheroes Professor Power has battled with are [[Captain America]], [[Defenders (comics)|Defenders]], [[Spider-Man]], [[Darkhawk]], [[Iron Man]], [[X-Men]] and [[X-Factor (comics)|X-Factor]].
