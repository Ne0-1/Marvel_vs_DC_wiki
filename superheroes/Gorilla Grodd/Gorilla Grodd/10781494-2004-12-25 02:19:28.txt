'''Gorilla Grodd''' is a [[fictional character]] appearing in [[DC Comics]], primarily as an opponent of [[The Flash]].  He is a hyper-intelligent [[telepathy|telepathic]] [[supervillain]] with the power to [[mind control|control the minds]] of others.  He is also, incidentally, a [[gorilla]], hailing originally from a secret nation of sapient talking gorillas, Gorilla City, which is hidden somewhere in darkest [[Africa]].
