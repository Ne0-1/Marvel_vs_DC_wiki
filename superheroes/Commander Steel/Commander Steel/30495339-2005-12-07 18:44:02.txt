'''Commander Steel''' is a [[fictional character]] and a [[superhero]] who first appeared in ''Steel, The Indestructible Man'' #1 in 1978, published by [[DC Comics]]. Created by [[Gerry Conway]] and [[Don Heck]], the stories were set in [[World War II]]. He was originally called Steel, but was later renamed Commander Steel by [[Franklin D. Roosevelt|President Roosevelt]].

He was a member of the [[All-Star Squadron]].
