'''Yo-Yo Rodriguez''', also known as '''Slingshot''', is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Universe]].

==Fictional character biography==
Yo-Yo Rodriguez, is the Puerto-Rican daughter of the [[Griffin (Marvel Comics)|Griffin]]. She was recruited by [[Nick Fury]] to join his anti-[[Skrull]] task force.<ref>''Mighty Avengers'' #13</ref> This team became known as the [[Secret Warriors]].<ref>''Secret Invasion'' #3</ref> As a part of her training for the team, Nick Fury violently interrogated her disguised as [[Madame Hydra]], leader of [[HYDRA]]. He kept hurting her to see if she would betray her team mates, but she didn't, so she passed the test.{{issue|date=November 2010}} This experience traumatized her greatly.{{issue|date=November 2010}} She helped fight the Skrulls during the [[Secret Invasion]],{{issue|date=November 2010}} and they won with the help of the other heroes and villains.<ref>''Secret invasion'' #8</ref>

At one point she was injured severely, with both of her arms severed by the Gorgon, and was temporarily unable to remain active with the team.<ref>''Secret Warriors'' #3</ref> However, both arms have now been replaced with prosthetics<ref>''Secret Warriors'' #8</ref> and she has returned to active duty.<ref>''Secret Warriors'' #9</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
She can run at superhuman speed and bounces back to the point where she began running.
