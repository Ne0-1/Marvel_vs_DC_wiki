'''Yo-Yo Rodriguez''', also known as '''Slingshot''', is a fictional superhero appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]]. Yo-Yo Rodriguez first appeared in ''[[The Mighty Avengers]]'' #13 (July 2008) and was created by writer [[Brian Michael Bendis]] and artist [[Alex Maleev]]. She has a form of super-speed, when used she automatically returns to place where she started. She was a member of Nick Fury's [[Secret Warriors (Team White)|Secret Warriors]] team. She is the daughter of supervillian [[Griffin (Marvel Comics)|Johnny Horton]].

Yo-Yo Rodriguez is portrayed by [[Natalia Cordova-Buckley]], in the TV series ''[[Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.]]'' which is set in the [[Marvel Cinematic Universe]]. In this version she is reimagined as an [[Inhumans|Inhuman]].

==Fictional character biography==
Yo-Yo Rodriguez is the Puerto-Rican daughter of the [[Griffin (Marvel Comics)|Griffin]]. She is recruited by [[Nick Fury]] to join his anti-[[Skrull]] task force.<ref>''Mighty Avengers'' #13</ref> This team becomes known as the [[Secret Warriors (Team White)|Secret Warriors]].<ref>''Secret Invasion'' #3</ref>

Both of her arms are severed by [[Gorgon (Tomi Shishido)|the Gorgon]], and she is temporarily unable to remain active with the team.<ref>''Secret Warriors'' #3</ref> However, both arms are later replaced with prosthetics,<ref>''Secret Warriors'' #8</ref> and she returns to active duty.<ref>''Secret Warriors'' #9</ref>

Slingshot is believed to have been killed in a confrontation with the [[Wrecking Crew (comics)|Wrecking Crew]].<ref>''Secret Warriors'' #1 (2017)</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
She can run at superhuman speed, then bounces back to the point where she began.<ref>''[[The Mighty Avengers]]'' #13 (July 2008)</ref>
