'''Yo-Yo Rodriguez''', also known as '''Slingshot''', is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Universe]].

==Fictional character biography==
Yo-Yo Rodriguez, is the Puerto-Rican daughter of the [[Griffin (Marvel Comics)|Griffin]]. She is recruited by [[Nick Fury]] to join his anti-[[Skrull]] task force.<ref>''Mighty Avengers'' #13</ref> This team becomes known as the [[Secret Warriors]].<ref>''Secret Invasion'' #3</ref>

Both of her arms are severed by the Gorgon, and is temporarily unable to remain active with the team.<ref>''Secret Warriors'' #3</ref> However, both arms are replaced with prosthetics<ref>''Secret Warriors'' #8</ref> and she returns to active duty.<ref>''Secret Warriors'' #9</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
She can run at superhuman speed and bounces back to the point where she began running.
