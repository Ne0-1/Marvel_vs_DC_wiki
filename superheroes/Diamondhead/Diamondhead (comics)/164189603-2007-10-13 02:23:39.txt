'''Diamondhead''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Universe]].

==Fictional character biography==
Diamondhead was a criminal from Earth, and a member of the [[Terrible Trio (Nova)|Terrible Trio]], along with [[Condor (comics)|Condor]] and [[Powerhouse (comics)#Powerhouse I (Rieg Davan)|Powerhouse]].  Diamondhead was mutated by a diamond-powered laser while attempting to steal some precious gems.  He became a superstrong criminal with a hard diamond-like body.  He was an enemy of [[Nova (comics)|Nova]] and the [[Champions of Xandar]], and betrayed the Nova Corps to the [[Skrull]]s.

Later, Diamondead was seen as a prisoner on the [[Stranger (comics)|Stranger]]'s laboratory world.
