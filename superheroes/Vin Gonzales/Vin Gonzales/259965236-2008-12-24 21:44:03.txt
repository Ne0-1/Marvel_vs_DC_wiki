'''Vin Gonzales''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[supporting character]] of [[Spider-Man]] in [[Marvel Comics]]' main [[Shared universe|shared]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]]. He is the roommate of Peter Parker as well as Parker's first romantic rival since the [[retcon]]ing of his marriage to [[Mary Jane Watson]] in "[[Spider-Man: One More Day|One More Day]]."

== History ==

Vin Gonzales is the roommate of [[Peter Parker]] and an [[NYPD]] officer. He doesn't have faith that [[Spider-Man]] is a "Good Guy". Vin seems to have a crush on Carlie Cooper. To make things more complicated, it seems Carlie has a crush on Peter Parker. Vin and Peter recently had a argument because of Peter's problems at the [[Daily Bugle]]. Vin thought that Peter still had his job as a photographer and Vin was giving Peter news tips. Peter decided not to tell Vin until he had found a job. Vin thought that Peter was playing him and that made Vin very angry.
