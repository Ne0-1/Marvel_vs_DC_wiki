'''Shockwave''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Comics Universe]] whose first appearance was in ''[[Master of Kung Fu]]'' (1st series) #42.

==History==
'''Lancaster Sneed''' is a former agent of [[MI-6]] and after he stole an experimental exoskeleton he became '''Shockwave'''. As Shockwave he has been a member of the Oriental Expediters and the Golden Dagger Sect. He later became one of SHIELD Deltites.
He has been the ally of many masterminds and criminals including [[Zaran]], [[Razor Fist]], [[Fah Lo Suee]], Tarrant/Griswold, [[Fu Manchu]] and [[Brynocki]].

He is the nephew of Sir Denis Nayland Smith and before becoming an agent for MI6, he was a carnival performer.  

In ''Master of Kung Fu'' (1st series) #60, Shang-Chi thought he was fighting the real Shockwave but in fact it was a robot duplicate being controlled by [[Doctor Doom]].

==Notes==
*In the [[New Universe]], [[Kathy Ling]] is known as ''Shockwave'' and a member of [[Psi-Force]].  
*An experimental vehicle stolen from [[H.A.T.E.]] by [[Nextwave]] in ''Nextwave'' #1 was called the '''Shockwave Rider'''.
*Two members of the [[Neo (comics)|Neo]], Farahd and Gethrin, were known as the '''Shockwave Riders'''.  The rode skimmers controlled by thought and attacked the X-Men in Venice in ''[[Uncanny X-Men]]'' #381
