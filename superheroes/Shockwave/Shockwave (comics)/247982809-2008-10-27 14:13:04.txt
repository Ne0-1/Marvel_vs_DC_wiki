'''Shockwave''' ('''Lancaster Sneed''') is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Comics Universe]] whose first appearance was in ''[[Master of Kung Fu]]'' vol. 1 #42.

==Fictional character biography==
'''Lancaster Sneed''' is the nephew of Sir Denis Nayland Smith and before becoming an agent for [[MI-6]], he was a carnival performer. After he stole an experimental exoskeleton, he became '''Shockwave'''. As Shockwave he has been a member of the Oriental Expediters and the Golden Dagger Sect. He later became one of SHIELD Deltites.

He has been the ally of many masterminds and criminals including [[Zaran]], [[Razor Fist]], [[Fah Lo Suee]], Tarrant/Griswold, [[Fu Manchu]] and [[Brynocki]].

He has faced [[Shang-Chi]] on several occasions though once Shang-Chi thought he was fighting the real Shockwave but was in fact it was a robot duplicate being controlled by [[Doctor Doom]].<ref>''Master of Kung Fu'' (1st series) #60</ref>

Shockwave was later involved in a [[Advanced Idea Mechanics#Splinter groups|R.A.I.D]] terror plot in [[London]], only to be foiled by [[Union Jack (Joseph Chapman)|Union Jack]].

===Civil War===
During the height of the Civil War he was captured by Heroes for Hire. Lancaster has been identified as one of the 142 registered superheroes who have registered as part of Initiative<ref>''[[Avengers: The Initiative]]'' #1</ref>. <ref>[http://www.marvel.com/news/comicstories.947 ''Avengers: The Initiative'' #1 Character Map]</ref>

===Post-Civil War and Secret Invasion===
Someone that looks like Shockwave was among the members of [[Hood (comics)|Hood]]'s Crime Syndicate <ref>New Avengers #35 (2007)</ref>. He was defeated by the New Avengers, however, he escaped from jail. 

He's recently appeared in ''[[Spider-Man: Brand New Day|Brand New Day]]'' as one of the villains in the bar.

Shockwave is one of the villains incarcerated in the [[The Raft (comics)|The Raft]] when the [[Skrull]]s strike.<ref>''Secret Invasion'' #1</ref> He is one among many supervillains who rejoined The Hood's crime syndicate and attacked an invading Skrull force.<ref>''Secret Invasion'' #6</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
Lancaster has some proficiency in unarmed combat and wears a stolen exoskeleton suit giving him enhanced strength and the ability to generate electric shocks on contact.

The suit is later water-proofed to prevent shorting out on contact with water.<ref>''Union Jack'' (2006-07) #2</ref>
