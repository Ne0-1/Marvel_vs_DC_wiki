'''Tana Nile''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Universe]].

Tana Nile was a leading female member of the Colonizers of [[Rigellians (Marvel Comics)|Rigel]], and a frequent ally of [[Thor (Marvel Comics)|Thor]].  Tana Nile played a major role in finding a new home-world for the Rigellians after the destruction of their native world<ref>Thor #218</ref>, Rigel-3, by the [[List of Marvel Comics alien races#R|Rhunians]].

She framed [[Ronan the Accuser]] for the House of Fiyero in exchange for information on Ronan.

She later joined [[Gamora]]'s Graces.

Tana Nile was slain by the [[Annihilation (comics)|Annihilation Wave]].
