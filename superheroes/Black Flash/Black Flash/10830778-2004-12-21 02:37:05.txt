the '''Black Flash''' is a [[fictional]] [[comic book]] [[character]] from [[DC Comics]]. The Black Flash essentially fulfils the same role as [[Death (personification)| Death]] for those who possess super-speed in the DC Universe, returning them to the source of their powers the [[Speed Force]]

He should not be confused with the other Death's in the DCU: The [[Black Racer]] and [[Death (Sandman)]]
