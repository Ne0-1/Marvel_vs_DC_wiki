'''Grindhouse''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Comics Universe]] whose first appearance was in ''[[Heroes for Hire]]'' (2nd series) #1.

==History==
Little is known of '''Grindhouse''''s activities before she appeared in ''Heroes for Hire'' (2nd series) #1, however upon her first appearance she was tricked into a sting operation to seek a new identity from [[Vienna (comics)|Vienna]] and to escape the prosecution from the [[Superhero Registration Act]].  Because of this set-up she was captured by the [[Heroes for Hire]].
