'''Kiwi Black''' is a fictional [[mutant (fictional)|mutant]] character in the [[Marvel Comics]] Universe. His first appearance was in ''Uncanny X-Men #429''.

==History==
He was a mutant bred by [[Azazel (Marvel Comics)|Azazel]] to help him transport his army to [[Earth]] from the hellish dimension they had been trapped in. Kiwi rejected his father’s influence, and allied himself with the [[X-Men]] in order to eliminate Azazel’s army.
Kiwi Black lost his mutant powers after the [[Decimation (comics)|M-Day]] (powerloss confirmed in ''New Avengers #18'').

==Notes==
*Kiwi Black is the son of Azazel and the half-brother of [[Nightcrawler (comics)|Nightcrawler]] and [[Abyss (comics)|Abyss]].

==Powers==
Focuses energy through his body, increasing the potency of his attacks, increased strength, enhanced muscle mass.
