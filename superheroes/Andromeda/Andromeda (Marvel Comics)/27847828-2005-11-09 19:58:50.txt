'''Andromeda''' is a character in [[Marvel Comics]] fictional [[Marvel Universe]].

A member of the [[Homo mermanus]] race, Andromeda is the first born daughter of [[Attuma]] of [[Atlantis]]. An occasional [[super-villain]], she appeared frequently in the original [[Defenders (comics)|Defenders]] series in the [[1970s]] and [[1980s]].

In the [[1989]] ''[[Atlantis Attacks]]'' crossover she was one of the "Seven Brides of [[Set (Serpent God)|Set]]" kidnapped by the [[Deviants|Deviant]] priest [[Ghaur]].
