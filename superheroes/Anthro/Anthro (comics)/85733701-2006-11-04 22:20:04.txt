'''Anthro''' is a [[fictional character]], the first [[Cro-Magnon]] boy born in the [[DC Comics]] [[DC Universe|universe]]. Anthro was created by cartoonist [[Howard Post]].  After appearing in issue #74 of ''[[Showcase (comics)|Showcase]]'', he moved to his own title that lasted 6 issues (1968-69).  

Anthro was a teen-age caveman.  His father was the chief of his tribe, his mom a member of another tribe.
