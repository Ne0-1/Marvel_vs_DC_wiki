'''Stringfellow''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[Mutant (Marvel comics)|mutant]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] Universe. His first appearance was in ''[[X-Treme X-Men]]'' #35.

==Fictional character biography==
Stringfellow is a mutant with the ability to cause others' bodies to become super elastic. Serving as [[Sunspot (comics)|Sunspot]]â€™s personal bodyguard, Stringfellow used his abilities on the X-Man [[Rogue (comics)|Rogue]] when she crashed an [[X-Corporation]] party. Following the events of [[Decimation (comics)|M-Day]] where [[Scarlet Witch]] removed the mutant genome from over 90% of the world's mutant population, it is unknown whether Stringfellow retained his superhuman powers.

==Powers and abilities==
He mentally causes organic beings to lose all rigidity in their physical structure, making them collapse like loose, pliant bands of flesh .
