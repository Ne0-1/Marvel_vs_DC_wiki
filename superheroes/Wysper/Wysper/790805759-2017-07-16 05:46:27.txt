'''Wysper''' ('''Jennifer Stewart''') is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Comics]] universe. She first appeared in ''Venom: Sinner Takes All '' #1 and was created by [[Dan Slott]] and [[John Calimee]].

==Fictional character biography==
'''Jennifer "Jenny" Stewart''', also known as Wysper is a member of [[The Jury (comics)|the Jury]]. Jennifer and her husband Ryan were Guardsmen at the [[Vault (comics)|Vault]], a prison for superhuman criminals. Ryan was murdered during a prison riot by prisoner Luis Alvarez, the second [[Tarantula (Marvel Comics)#Luis Alvarez|Tarantula]]. After the murder of her husband Jennifer moved in with a roommate named Claire until receiving a notice for "jury duty".<ref>''[[Venom: Sinner Takes All]]'' #1 (August 1995)</ref>

The majority of the members of the Jury were also former Guardsmen at the vault. The Jury soon freed Luis Alvarez from a prison in Del Boca Dios. After their own version of a trial, they freed the Tarantula and gave him the option to kill Wysper to gain his freedom. To be inducted into the Jury, Wysper had to kill Tarantula. She refused to do so until the Jury informed her that Tarantula had killed her husband Ryan. Wysper chose to snap Tarantula's neck.<ref>''Venom: Sinner Takes All'' #2-4 (Septemberâ€“November 1995)</ref>

Wysper and the Jury set a trap for the symbiote [[Hybrid (Scott Washington)|Hybrid]], defeating and capturing him.<ref>''[[Venom (comic book)#Venom: The Hunted (1996)|Venom: The Hunted]]'' #1 (May 1996)</ref> The symbiote broke free and attacked, and the [[New Warriors]] attacked as the Jury were trying to get it back under control.<ref>''Venom: The Hunted'' #2 (June 1996)</ref> Wysper fought [[Vance Astrovik|Justice]] until she was defeated by [[Turbo (comics)|Turbo]].<ref>''Venom: The Hunted'' #3 (July 1996)</ref>

Wysper was with the team when they lost their funding and were forced to work for [[Edwin Cord]] with the [[U.S. Agent]] as the Jury's new leader. The Jury fought the [[Thunderbolts (comics)|Thunderbolts]] and lost.<ref>''Thunderbolts'' #23 (February 1999)</ref> Wysper was later seen training with her new equipment and wanted to go with U.S. Agent and the team to fight [[Graviton (comics)|Graviton]] but were told to stand down by Edwin Cord.<ref>''Thunderbolts'' #28 (July 1999)</ref> Later they helped the Thunderbolts defeat the second [[Secret Empire (organization)|Secret Empire]].<ref>''Thunderbolts'' #32 (November 1999)</ref>

===The Initiative===
Jenny was considered as a "potential recruit" for the [[Fifty State Initiative|Initiative]].<ref>''Civil War: Battle Damage Report''</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
Wysper has a [[Guardsman (comics)|Guardsman]]-based suit of armor that allows her to fly and fire energy blasts. She also has sonic weapons that can cause feedback or cause an opponent to experience a loss of balance.
