'''Kraglin''' is a fictional alien appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]]. Created by plotter [[Stan Lee]], writer [[Ernie Hart]] and artist [[Don Heck]], He first appeared in ''[[Tales to Astonish]]'' #46 (August 1963). He is a member of the Ravagers.

Kraglin appears in ''[[Guardians of the Galaxy (film)|Guardians of the Galaxy]]'' and ''[[Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2]]'' where the character is portrayed by [[Sean Gunn]].

==Publication history==
The character, created by plotter [[Stan Lee]], writer [[Ernie Hart]] and artist [[Don Heck]], debuted in ''[[Tales to Astonish]]'' #46 ([[cover-date]]d Aug. 1963). Kraglin is a A-Chilitarian, a alien humanoid and was introduced as an one-off villian that was defeated by [[Ant-Man]] and [[Wasp (comics)|the Wasp]].<ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.geekfeed.com/guardians-galaxy-2-post-credits/|title=All 5 Guardians of the Galaxy: Vol 2 Post-Credits Scenes Explained|last=Carlisle|first=William|website=Geekfeed|date= April 30, 2017|accessdate=September 3, 2019|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170606031822/http://www.geekfeed.com/guardians-galaxy-2-post-credits|archivedate=June 6, 2017|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=https://www.cheatsheet.com/entertainment/guardians-of-the-galaxy-vol-2-marvel-movie-characters-vs-the-comics.html/|title=What the 'Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2' Characters Are Supposed to Look Like|last=Bleznak|first=Becca|website=Cheatsheet|date=August 17, 2017|accessdate=September 3, 2019|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20170720213222/http://www.cheatsheet.com/entertainment/guardians-of-the-galaxy-vol-2-marvel-movie-characters-vs-the-comics.html/|archivedate=July 20, 2017|url-status=live}}</ref>

After that single appearance, Kraglin returned in ''All-New Guardians of the Galaxy Annual'' #1 (Aug. 2017), by Chad Bowers, Chris Sims and Danilo Beyruth.<ref>{{cite web|url=https://comicsverse.com/new-guardians-galaxy-annual-1-review/|title=ALL-NEW GUARDIANS OF THE GALAXY ANNUAL #1 Review: Return of the Ravagers|last=Berkowitz |first=Aaron|website=[[ComicsVerse]]|date=June 28, 2017|accessdate=September 3, 2019|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20190903112348/https://comicsverse.com/new-guardians-galaxy-annual-1-review/|archivedate=September 3, 2019|url-status=live}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.adventuresinpoortaste.com/2017/06/28/all-new-guardians-of-the-galaxy-annual-1-review/|title=All-New Guardians of the Galaxy Annual #1 Review|last=Brooke|first=David|website=Adventures in Poor Taste|date=June 28, 2017|accessdate=September 3, 2019|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20181224054943/http://www.adventuresinpoortaste.com/2017/06/28/all-new-guardians-of-the-galaxy-annual-1-review/|archivedate=December 24, 2019|url-status=live}}</ref>

==Fictional character biography==
Kraglin is an [[List of alien races in Marvel Comics#A|A-Chilitarian]] from the planet A-Chiltar III and has the appearance of a furry, purple being with mult-faceted eyes. At the time when they are in [[Greece]], [[Hank Pym|Ant-Man]] and [[Wasp (comics)|Wasp]] investigate a monster attacking boats in the ocean where they run into a [[Cyclops]] that is being used by Kraglin and his fellow A-Chilitarians into capturing people for their plot to study the captive fishermen and sailors and take over Earth.<ref name="tales">''Tales to Astonish'' #46. Marvel Comics.</ref>

During the ''[[Secret Empire (comics)|Secret Empire]]'' storyline, Kraglin resurfaces as a member of [[Yondu]]'s Ravagers.<ref>''All-New Guardians of the Galaxy Annual'' #1. Marvel Comics.</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
Kraglin's multi-faceted eyes enable him to see in multiple directions at once.<ref name="tales" />
