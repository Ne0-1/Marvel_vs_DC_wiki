'''Lady Mastermind''' ('''Regan Wyngarde''') is a [[fictional character]], who is a [[blonde]], [[supervillain]], who exist in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]]. She first appeared in ''[[X-Treme X-Men]]'' #6 ([[December 2001]]) and was created by [[Chris Claremont]] and [[Salvador Larroca]].

==Character biography==

Lady Mastermind is one of two daughters of [[Mastermind (comics)|Jason Wyngarde]], the original Mastermind. She has an intense hatred, for her half-sister. She was originally involved in a plot created by [[Sebastian Shaw (comics)|Sebastian Shaw]] to gain revenge against [[Sage (comics)|Tessa]]. Tessa was placed inside the [[Hellfire Club (comics)|Hellfire Club]], as a mole, for [[Professor X|Charles Xavier]].

Lady Mastermind placed Tessa is a series of [[Illusion|illusions]]. This illusions were to make Tessa work for Shaw again. However, Tessa recieved help from [[Lifeguard (comics)|Lifeguard]], to fight the illusions. Tessa was able to turn Lady Mastermind's powers against herself.

==Powers and abilites==
Lady Mastermind possess the same mutant powers as her half-sister. She can project sophisticated illusions that are indistinguishable from reality.
