'''Tyr''' is a [[fictional character]] appearing in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]], based loosely on the [[Tyr]] of [[Norse mythology|Norse myth]].

==Fictional character biography==
Tyr is the Asgardian god of war.  Tyr was renowned throughout [[Asgard (Marvel Comics)|Asgard]] as being the only god brave enough to place his hand in the mouth of the wolf [[Fenris Wolf (Marvel Comics)|Fenris]] during the latter's binding.  He has fought in the defense of Asgard numerous times against such threats as [[Surtur (Marvel Comics)|Surtur]] and the Egyptian death-god [[Seth (Marvel Comics)|Seth]].  He has several times rebelled against [[Odin (Marvel Comics) |Odin]] due to his love of battle and fought [[Thor (Marvel Comics)|Thor]] due to resentment of Thor's relationship to [[Sif (Marvel Comics)|Sif]].

==Powers and Abilities==
According to the Marvel website, Tyr possess the superhuman abilities possessed by all Gods Of Asgard. Tyr can lift over 35 tons. Tyr, being the god of war, is not only a master at unarmed combat but also in using all forms of Asgardian weaponry as well

==Other Media==
Tyr appears in ''[[Marvel: Ultimate Alliance]]''. He was seen defeated and was being guarded by the [[Mac Gargan|Scorpion]] and the [[Lizard (comics)|Lizard]] with him hanging over lava. To get him down after the battle, water must be obtained to cool down the lava, and he subsequently thanks the heroes, apologising for his past belief that humans are weak when it is they who now come to save Asgard.
