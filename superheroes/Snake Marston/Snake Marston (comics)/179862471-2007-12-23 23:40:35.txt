'''Sylvester "Snake" Marston''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Comics Universe]] whose first appearance was in ''[[Machine Man]]''  #16.  

==Fictional character biography==
Sylvester Marston was born in [[Ontario, Canada]].  He was a member of [[Baron Brimstone]]'s [[Satan Squad]] and the [[Enforcers (comics)|Enforcers]]. Snake has not been seen since, and was presumed dead, following his impersonation by the [[Eel (comics)|Eel]] as part of a plot involving [[Daredevil (Marvel Comics)|Daredevil]], [[Mister Hyde (comics)|Mister Hyde]], and the Enforcers, but he has lately resurfaced as a member of the Government-sponsored [[Thunderbolts (comics)|Thunderbolts]] team.
