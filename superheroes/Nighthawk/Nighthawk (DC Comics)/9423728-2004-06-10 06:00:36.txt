'''Nighthawk''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[cowboy]] in the [[DC Comics]] [[DC Universe|universe]].  His real name is Hannibal Hawkes, and he first appeared in ''[[Western Comics]]'' #5.  He died in the [[Crisis on Infinite Earths]].  Later comics revealed that he was a reincarnation of Prince Khufu (whose next incarnation would become [[Hawkman]]), and that he was the partner and lover of the Western heroine [[Cinnamon (comics)|Cinnamon]], who was the reincarnation of Khufu's wife Kheiera.
