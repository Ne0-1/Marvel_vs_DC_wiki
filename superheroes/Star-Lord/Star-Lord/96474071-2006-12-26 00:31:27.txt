'''Star-Lord''' is a [[fictional character]] published by [[Marvel Comics]]. He debuted in ''Marvel Preview'' #4, January ([[1975]]). He was created by writer [[Steve Englehart]] and artist Steve Gan.

==Fictional character biography==
Star-Lord is Peter Quill, a former astronaut who was the son of Meredith Quill, an Earth woman, and Jason, emperor of the Spartoi, an alien humanoid civilization. 

==Powers and abilities==
As Star-Lord, Quill possesses augmented strength and resiliency and controls an "element gun", a pistol capable of conjuring one of the four elements. He is also psychically linked to his starship, which is a living entity.
