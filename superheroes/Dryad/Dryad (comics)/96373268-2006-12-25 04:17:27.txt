'''Dryad''' is a fictional character in [[Marvel Comics]]' [[X-Men]] comic franchise.  She had the power control plant life and plant growth. According to the Xavier Institute for Higher Learning's Yearbook, Callie's favourite hobbies were the outdoors and spending time with her father. She was also a vegetarian She disliked greed and large corporations. She was also voted as having the 'Coolest Codename'. Dryad was a member of the Corsairs, along with [[Specter (comics)|Specter]], [[Quill (comics)|Quill]] and the [[Stepford Cuckoos]], who led the team. However, after [[Scarlet Witch]]'s actions of M-Day Dryad was depowered and boarded the bus to leave the Institute, only to be killed by William Stryker when the bus was blown up, killing the depowered students. 

==Powers==
Dryad had the mutant ability to manipulate plant life (Chlorokinesis), as well as communicating with plants. She could use her powers to make plants grow or follow her commands. Dryad also enjoyed the outdoors, which shows her connection to nature. She also had a slightly pinkish skin tone, which is assumed to be a part of her mutation. After Decimation, Dryad lost her powers.
