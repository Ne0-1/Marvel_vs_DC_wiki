'''Dryad''' ('''Callie Betto''') is a [[fictional character]] appearing in [[comic books]] published by [[Marvel Comics]]. Created by Nunzio DeFilippis and Christina Weir, she first appeared in ''[[New X-Men]]: Academy X'' #1. 

==Fictional character biography==
Dryad was a member of the former Corsairs Training Squad, along with [[Specter (comics)|Specter]], [[Quill (comics)|Quill]] and the [[Stepford Cuckoos]], who lead the team. She appears in New X-Men: Academy X 2 as background character,she also appears in the party. She appears in the cover with [[Anole (comics)|Anole]]. She appeared with the students that aren't fighting with the [[Blob]].

===Depowered and Death===
After [[Scarlet Witch]]'s actions of M-Day, Dryad is depowered and boards a bus with other depowered students leaving the [[X-Mansion|Xavier Institute]].  She is killed when William Stryker fires a missile at the bus, blowing it up, killing the depowered students aboard<ref>''New X-Men'' vol. 2, #23</ref>.

==Powers and abilities==
Dryad had the mutant ability to manipulate plant life, as well as communicating with plants. She could also use her powers to make plants grow or follow her commands. After [[Decimation (comics)|Decimation]], Dryad loses her powers.

==Alternate Versions==
===Too Much Information===
In the storyline "Too Much Information", Dryad appears as one of the future X-Men that is blown up during the mission to find the Hellions.
