'''Nicholette "Nikki" Gold''' is a fictional character, a woman in the [[Earth-691]] timeline of the fictional [[Marvel Universe]], genetically engineered to live on the planet [[Mercury (planet)|Mercury]].  As such, she has a superhuman ability to withstand heat and ultraviolet radiation, and can see clearly in very bright light. She is bald, although she expels body heat through her head in a low-temperature flame resembling hair in shape.  She was the sixth member of the [[Guardians of the Galaxy]], and is noted for her acrobatic and sharpshooting skill.  She first appeared in ''[[Marvel Presents]]'' #4, and was created by [[Steve Gerber]].
