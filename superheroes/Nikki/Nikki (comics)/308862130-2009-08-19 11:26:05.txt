'''Nicholette "Nikki" Gold''' is a fictional character, a woman in the [[Earth-691]] timeline of the fictional [[Marvel Universe]], genetically engineered to live on the planet [[Mercury (planet)|Mercury]].  As such, she has a superhuman ability to withstand heat and ultraviolet radiation, and can see clearly in very bright light. She is bald, although she expels body heat through her head in a low-temperature flame resembling hair in shape.  She was the sixth member of the [[Guardians of the Galaxy]], and is noted for her acrobatic and sharp-shooting skill.

==Publication history==
Nikki first appeared in ''[[Marvel Presents]]'' #4, and was created by [[Steve Gerber]] (who did not call her Nicholette Gold).

==Fictional character biography==
'''Nicholette "Nikki" Gold''' was born in the planet Mercury in the 31st Century. She was rescued from an abandoned spacecraft by the [[Guardians of the Galaxy]]. She joined the Guardians, seeking excitement and adventure after years of solitude aboard the abandoned spacecraft.<ref>''Marvel Presents'' #4</ref> She later joined with [[Vance Astro]] in a metaphysical union in a successful attempt to use the force of human love to destroy the Topographical Man.<ref>''Marvel Presents'' #7</ref>

Nikki traveled to the present with the other Guardians, and assisted the [[Avengers (comics)|Avengers]] in battle against [[Korvac]].<ref>''Avengers'' #172-177</ref> She traveled to the present again, and battled [[Hammer and Anvil (comics)|Hammer and Anvil]] alongside [[Spider-Man]].<ref>''Marvel Team-Up'' #86</ref>

Alongside the Guardians, Nikki later went on a quest to find the lost [[Captain America's shield|shield]] of [[Captain America]], and battled Taserface.<ref>''Guardians of the Galaxy'' #1</ref> She battled the [[Stark (species)|Stark]] alongside the other Guardians.<ref>''Guardians of the Galaxy'' #2-3</ref> She encountered [[Firelord]].<ref>''Guardians of the Galaxy'' #4</ref> She battled the super-humanoid team known as Force alongside the Guardians.<ref>''Guardians of the Galaxy'' #5-6</ref>

During her time on the team, Nikki had a romantic relationship with [[Charlie-27]], though they later amicably separated.{{Issue|date=July 2009}} She became attracted to later Guardian recruits [[Firelord]] and [[Talon (Marvel Comics)|Talon]] though both simply wished to remain friends.{{Issue|date=July 2009}}

==Powers and abilities==
Nikki is a member of a genetically engineered offshoot of humanity whose traits were designed for survival in the harsh conditions of the planet Mercury. As such, she has the ability to see in intense light, and has a high degree of resistance to heat and most types of radiation.

Nikki has achieved extensive proficiency in hand-to-hand combat and proficiency in gymnastics and sharp-shooting. She is self-taught from memory tapes.

Nikki is often armed with a neuronic frequency stun gun, a laser pistol, and various handgun-type weapons as needed.
