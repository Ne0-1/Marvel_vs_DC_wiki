'''Khonshu''' is a [[fictional character]] appearing in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]], based loosely on the [[Chons|Khonshu]] of [[Egyptian mythology]]. Khonshu first appeared in ''Moon Knight'' (1st series) #1 (November 1980).

He is the patron of the superhero [[Moon Knight]]. 

==In other media==
Khonshu appears as an alternate costume for Moon Knight in the video game ''[[Marvel: Ultimate Alliance]]''.
