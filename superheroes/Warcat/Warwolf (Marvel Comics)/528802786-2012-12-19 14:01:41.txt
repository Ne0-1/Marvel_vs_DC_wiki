'''Warwolf''' ('''Vince "Vic" Marcus''') is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Comics]] Universe who first appeared in ''Nick Fury’s Howling Commandos'' #1.

==Fictional character biography==
''Vince Marcus'' is a member of [[S.H.I.E.L.D.]]'s [[Howling Commandos]] Monster Force.  He is the field commander.  It is unknown yet when he joined SHIELD or why he is nicknamed Warwolf.{{issue|date=December 2012}}

He has fought with guns and also used his claws to inflitrate the "Lords of Lightning",  a doomsday cult and led the assault on Stonehedge when Merlin and faeries were bringing their world into ours.{{issue|date=December 2012}}

==Notes==
*On '''Earth-Deathlok''', there is a character known as '''Warwolf'''.  He first appeared in ''Astonishing Tales'' #26
*A member of [[Cybertek]] is known as ''Warwolf'' who first appeared in ''Deathlok'' vol. 2 #1.
*A group of extradimensional former agents of [[Mojo (comics)|Mojo]] are known as the [[Warwolves]] who first appeared in ''Excalibur Special Edition'' #1.
