The '''Arkon''' was a [[one-off]] [[England|English]] [[automobile]] manufactured by two determined students, [[Richard Moon]] and [[Neil Morgan]], between [[1971]] and [[1972]].  The 33-inch high car is an exotic-looking [[GT]] powered by a [[rear-mounted]] [[Imp]] engine; the whole is set on a [[Triumph Spitfire]] [[chassis]].
