'''Hermod''' (aka '''Hermod the Brave''') is a [[fictional character]] appearing in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]], based loosely on the [[Hermóðr]] of [[Norse mythology|Norse myth]]. 

==Fictional character biography==
Hermod is a young god who is shrouded in mystery. It is known, however, that [[Odin (Marvel Comics)|Odin]] will usually employ Hermod as a messenger because of his great speed. Hermod's greatest mission was when he traveled to Hel in order to ask the goddess [[Hela (Marvel Comics)|Hela]] for information on how to revive [[Balder (Marvel Comics)|Balder the Brave]]. Because of the events of Ragnarok, Hermod is believed to have suffered the same fate that befell the rest of the [[Asgardians (Marvel Comics)|Asgardians]].

== Powers and abilities ==
Hermod possesses normal strength, stamina, and durability for an Asgardian god.  He also can run at speeds far exceeding those of other Asgardian gods.

==In other media==
Hermod appears as a non-player character in the video game ''[[Marvel: Ultimate Alliance]]''. He has special dialogue with Thor where it is mentioned that Hermod evaded capture.
