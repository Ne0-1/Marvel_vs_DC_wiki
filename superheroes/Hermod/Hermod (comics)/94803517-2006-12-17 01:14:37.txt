'''Hermod''' (aka '''Hermod the Brave''') is a [[fictional character]] appearing in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]], based loosely on the [[Hermóðr]] of [[Norse mythology|Norse myth]]. Hermod is a young god that is shrouded in mystery. It is known, however, that [[Odin (comics)|Odin]] will usually employ Hermod as a messenger because of his great speed. Hermod’s greatest mission was when he traveled to Hel in order to ask the goddess [[Hela (comics)|Hela]] for information on how to revive [[Balder (comics)|Balder the Brave]]. Because of the events of Ragnarok, Hermod is believed to have suffered the same fate that befell the rest of the [[Asgardians]].

==In other media==
Hermod appears as an NPC in the video game [[Marvel: Ultimate Alliance]]
