'''Orbit''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[Mutant (Marvel comics)|mutant]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] Universe. His first appearance was in ''X-Statix #1''.

==Fictional character biography==
Orbit is a member of America's newest mutant superhero team, [[O-Force]]. Like all potential recruits, Orbit had to compete in a live television program, hosted by [[Solomon O'Sullivan]], to win a place on the O-Force team. It's unknown if Orbit lost his mutant powers after the [[Decimation (comics)|M-Day]].

==Powers and abilities==
He can fly and apparently project energy from his eyes that disrupts the equilibrium of others. Orbit had several small spheres constantly orbiting around his head.
