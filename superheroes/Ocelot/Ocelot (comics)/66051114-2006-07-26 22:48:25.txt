'''Ocelot''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[mutant (fictional)|mutant]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] Universe. His first appearance was in X-Statix #1.

Ocelot is a member of America's newest mutant superhero team, called [[O-Force]]. Like all potential recruits, Ocelot had to compete in a live [[television program]], hosted by [[Solomon O'Sullivan]], to win a place on the O-Force team. It's unknown if Ocelot lost/kept his mutant powers after the [[Decimation (comics)|M-Day]].

'''Note''': This Ocelot (II) should not be confused with Ocelot I of the Seraphim.

==Powers==
He has a very hairy appearance and he has above average agility.
