'''Tamara Rahn''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Universe]].

Tamara Rahn is an alien of the water-breathing extraterrestrial Banari race from the planet Laab.  She is a red-skinned humanoid with blonde hair.

==Fictional character biography==
Tamara stowed away on the spacecraft which brought the males of her race to earth, where all except Tamara were killed by the [[Atlanteans (Marvel Comics)|Atlanteans]], who mistook them for enemies.  The other females of the race apparently perished on their dying home world.

On Earth, she encountered [[Namor]].<ref>''Sub-Mariner'' #58</ref> Namor sought to rescue her.<ref>''Sub-Mariner'' #59</ref> Later, she helped Namor fight [[Orka (comics)|Orka]].<ref>''Sub-Mariner'' #66-67</ref>

However, she teamed up with [[Namorita]], [[Doctor Doom]], and the [[Hydro-Men]] against the [[Avengers (comics)|Avengers]] at [[Hydro-Base]], and defeated the [[Wasp (comics)|Wasp]].<ref>''Super-Villain Team-Up'' #9; ''Avengers'' #155-156</ref>

Tamara briefly appeared during the ''[[Contest of Champions]]''.<ref>''Contest of Champions'' #1-3</ref>
  
She was reunited with Namor.<ref>''Namor'' #35</ref> She helped Namor fight Suma-Ket and was rescued by [[Neptune (Marvel Comics)|Neptune]],<ref>''Namor'' #36</ref> and helped reclaim [[Atlantis (Marvel Comics)|Atlantis]] from the Unforgiven Dead. <ref>''Namor'' #38-40</ref> Tamara eventually forgave the Atlanteans and has since become a citizen of Atlantis.


Tamara took [[Tiger Shark (comics)|Tiger Shark]] as a lover for a time. She helped Namor, [[Stingray (comics)|Stingray]], [[Triton (comics)|Triton]], Tiger Shark, [[Lyja]], and the [[Fantastic Four]] battle the Starblasters. Xlym exterminated her remaining fellow Banari, and Tamara executed Xlym.<ref>''Namor'' #46-47; ''Fantastic Four'' #385-386</ref>

Tamara and Tiger Shark were investigated and beaten by [[Llyra]] and her Namor-clone [[Llyron]], and strung up outside the gates of Atlantis. They were rescued by Namor and taken for medical treatment.<ref>''Sub-Mariner'' #54-56</ref> She was also a member of his short-lived [[Deep Six (Marvel Comics)#Namor's Deep Six|Deep Six]] team.<ref>''Namor'' #58-59</ref> 

Tamara was used by the [[Radioactive Man (comics)|Radioactive Man]] to infect At'la'tique with a radiation plague.<ref>''New Thunderbolts'' #9</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
Tamara has superhuman strength on Earth and can breathe Earth air for short periods.
