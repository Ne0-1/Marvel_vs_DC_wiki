'''Geiger''' ('''Delilah "Dee Dee" Dearborn''') is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Universe]]. She first appeared in ''[[Doc Samson]] #1'' (Jan 1996), created by [[Dan Slott]].

==Fictional character biography==
Delilah "Dee Dee" Dearborn was a student at [[Empire State University]] who gains superhuman powers from an accident with a gamma accelerator at the school.<ref>''Doc Samson'' #1</ref> [[Doc Samson]] mistakes her for a murderer and they fight.<ref>''Doc Samson'' #2</ref> She later helps him track and confront the real killer.<ref>''Doc Samson'' #4</ref>

===Initiative===
Geiger is seen at the military base [[Camp Hammond (comics)|Camp Hammond]], as an official recruit for the [[Avengers: The Initiative|Initiative]], which is a super-human military draft. She is introduced to Camp Hammond along with [[Ant-Man (Eric O'Grady)|Ant-Man]], [[Crusader (Marvel Comics)#Crusader (Skrull)|Crusader]], [[Melee (comics)|Melee]], [[Geldoff]], [[Dragon Lord (comics)#Tako Shamara|Dragon Lord]], [[Red Nine]], and [[Diamondback (comics)|Diamondback]].<ref>''Avengers: The Initiative'' #8</ref>

She was one of the trainees that attacked [[Michael Van Patrick#KIA|KIA]] and were defeated.<ref>''Avengers: The Initiative'' #10</ref>

===Dark Reign===
Following the events of [[Secret Invasion]], Geiger graduated from the Initiative<ref>''Avengers: The Initiative'' #21</ref> and was assigned to New Mexico's Initiative team the Mavericks.<ref name="Christos Gage Author Comments">{{cite web |url=http://forums.comicbookresources.com/showpost.php?p=8321510&postcount=46 |title= Christos Gage (author) Comments|accessdate=2009-01-30 |work=comicbookresources.com forums }}</ref>

===Heroic Age===
Geiger appears at a meeting held by [[Prodigy (Ritchie Gilmore)|Prodigy]] regarding magical hammers that have crashed into the earth.<ref>''Fear Itself: Youth In Revolt'' #1</ref> She saves [[Gravity (comics)|Gravity]] from a grenade thrown by [[Crossbones (comics)|Crossbones]], but is injured when it explodes.<ref>''Fear Itself: Youth In Revolt'' #2</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
Due to a Gamma radiation accident, Geiger has the ability to mimic the powers of any gamma-powered beings in her immediate vicinity, potentially making her as strong as the Hulk, as intelligent as the Leader, etc.  However, she has little control over these powers, a downside exacerbated by the fact that she also takes on personality aspects of whoever she is mimicking as well.
