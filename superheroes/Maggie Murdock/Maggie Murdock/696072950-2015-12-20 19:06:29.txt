'''Maggie Murdock,(née Maggie Grace)''' is a fictional character appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]]. She is the ex-wife of [[Jack Murdock (comics)|Jack Murdock]] and the mother of Matt Murdock who grew up to become the superhero known as [[Daredevil (Marvel Comics)|Daredevil]].<ref>Daredevil vol 1, #230</ref> Maggie Murdock was created by [[Literary editor|writer-editor]] [[Frank Miller (comics)|Frank Miller]] and artist [[David Mazzucchelli]]. The character first appeared in ''Daredevil'' #229 (April 1986).<ref>Daredevil vol 1, #229</ref>

==Creation==
Sister is a nun and, ironically, the mother of the [[Daredevil (Marvel Comics)|Daredevil]], she was created by Frank Miller and David Mazzucchelli, first appearing in Daredevil #229<ref>Daredevil vol 1, #229</ref> in 1986.

==Fictional character biography==
Sister Maggie (Maggie Murdock, née Maggie Grace) is the Mother of  Matthew Murdock aka [[Daredevil (Marvel Comics)|Daredevil]].<ref>Daredevil vol 1, #229</ref> She left him and her husband [[Jack Murdock (comics)|Jack Murdock]], when Matt was still a baby to join Catholicism and become a nun.<ref>Daredevil vol 1, #230</ref><ref>Daredevil vol 1, #233</ref><ref>Daredevil vol 1, #267</ref> Matt found his mother in the  Born Again story arc (in Daredevil #229<ref>Daredevil #229</ref>). They reunited on different occasions. In the storyline  Guardian Devil, Daredevil, plunging off a roof after being drugged by  Mysterio, saved himself and ended up in front of the Church were Sister Maggie was.<ref>Daredevil vol 1, #325</ref><ref>Daredevil vol 1, #348</ref><ref>Daredevil vol 1, #349</ref><ref>Daredevil vol 1, #375</ref><ref>Daredevil vol 1, #380</ref> She took care of him while he was resting. Later they hugged.<ref>Daredevil vol 2, #0.5</ref><ref>Daredevil vol 2, #4</ref><ref>Daredevil vol 2, #5</ref><ref>Daredevil vol 2, #7</ref><ref>Daredevil vol 2, #8</ref><ref>Daredevil vol 4, #6</ref><ref>Daredevil vol 4, #7</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
Sister Maggie has extensive training in Catholicism, along with some skills in medical first aid.

==In other media==

===Film===
[[Daredevil (film)|Daredevil]] (2003)

Maggie Murdock can be seen in the Director's cut of [[Daredevil (film)|Daredevil]] 2003, portrayed by [[Vivian Palermo Winther]] and credited as the Nun. Sister Maggie can be seen briefly in a flashback with Matt' after he gets struck by the chemicals that gave him his powers. The scene is where a nun (Maggie) is visiting the hospital and bending over him telling him to sleep. He touches the cross around her neck.

===Television===
[[Daredevil (TV series)|Daredevil]] (2015)

Maggie Murdock hasn't been seen on the Netflix Daredevil series, but she has been referenced to when her ex-husband [[Jack Murdock (comics)|Jack Murdock]] calls her to tell her that there's a good chance he'll be killed by the "[[Roscoe Sweeney|Fixer]]" after his next fight; and that he needs her to take care of Matt and his future if that happens.
