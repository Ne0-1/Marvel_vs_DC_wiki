'''Osiris''' is a [[fictional character]] appearing in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]], based loosely on the [[Osiris]] of [[Egyptian mythology]].  

There is also an [[Osiris (DC Comics)|Osiris]] in the [[DC Universe]].  He is Tomas, the younger brother of [[Isis (comics)|Isis]].  [[Black Adam]] granted him superpowers in order to help him recover from a severe [[Intergang]] beating in ''[[52 (comics)|52]]'' #24.
