'''Margaret Slade''' is a [[fictional character]] and [[Mutant (Marvel comics)|mutant]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]]. She makes her first appearance in ''[[X-Men]]: Apocalypse/Dracula #2''.

==Fictional character biography==

==Powers and abilities==
[[Image:Margaret powers.JPG|thumb|left|200px|Margaret uses her powers against Kabar]]
Margaret like many of [[Apocalypse (comics)|Apocalypse]]'s descendents possess a degree of molecular rearrangement. She usually uses this ability to transform her hands into weapons.
