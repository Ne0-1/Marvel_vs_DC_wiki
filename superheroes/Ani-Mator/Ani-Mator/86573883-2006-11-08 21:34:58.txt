The Ani-Mator was a geneticist employed by [[Cameron Hodge]] to research a means of stopping the process that creates [[Mutant (fictional)|mutants]].  Dr. Animus instead created a race of "Ani-Mates", which combined the characteristics of human beings and other animals, intending them to be a slave races for humanity.

One of the Ani-Mates, [[Bird-Brain]], escaped the Ani-Mator's island and befriended the [[New Mutants]].  The New Mutants returned to the island with Bird-Brain to stop the mad scientist's experiments.  The Ani-Mator, attempting to shoot [[Wolfsbane (comics)|Wolfsbane]], instead killed the New Mutant [[Cypher (comics)|Cypher]].  [[Magik (comics)|Magik]] exiled the Ani-Mator to the dimension of [[Limbo (comics)|Limbo]] in retailiation.
