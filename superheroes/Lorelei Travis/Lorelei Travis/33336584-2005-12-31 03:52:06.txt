'''Lorelei''' is the name of two [[fictional character]]s in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]]

==Savage Land mutate==
{{cleanup-date|November 2005}}

Lorelei was a [[Savage Land]] mutant until Magneto turned her into a mutant siren(X-Men #63). At the end of the comic the machine that Magneto had used to make his savage land creations was destroyed. Lorelei lost her siren powers. 

Some time later she was seen in Defenders #15-16 with her powers (somehow she got them back somewere in the middle of X-Men and Defenders).  And her last appearance with the Brotherhood was in Champions #17. 

Years later she seen in Captain America (1st Series) #415-417.  She hasn't been seen since her last apperance in Captain America but has her own trading card (Marvel VS DC card game).

==Asgardian==
Lorelei is an [[Asgard (comics)|Asgardian]] in the Marvel Comics universe.

Similar to her elder sister [[Enchantress (Marvel Comics)|Amora]] in goals and methods, she attempted to ensnare [[Thor (Marvel Comics)|Thor]] with a love potion, and succeeded for a time until Amora, in revenge, cast a spell on her to make her enamoured of [[Loki (comics)|Loki]].
