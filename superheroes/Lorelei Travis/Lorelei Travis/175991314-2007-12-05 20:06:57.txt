'''Lorelei Travis''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[Mutant (Marvel comics)|mutant]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] Universe. Her first appearance was in ''District X #1''.

==Fictional character biography==
'''Lorelei Travis''' is the name of a young girl who has "living" hair. Lorelei is an [[exotic dancer]] at the mob leader Daniel Kaufman's ''Wildkat Klub'' in [[District X|Mutant Town]] located in [[New York City]]. Lorelei had managed to become one of the Wildkat Klub's household names through the artistic use of her genetic mutation.

During a recent investigation into Kaufman's businesses by [[Lucas Bishop]] and [[Ishmael Ortega|Ismael Ortega]], Lorelei survived a bombing of the Wildkat Klub perpetrated by Kaufman's enemies, sustaining minor injuries.

She is one of the few mutants to retain her powers after the effects of [[Decimation (comics)| M-Day]], although a group of militant [[Purity (comics)|Purity]] members cuts all of her hair out of vengeance. Unlikely help comes in the form of [[Toad (comics)|Toad]] who, after saving her, escorts her to the [[X-Mansion]] to join the other members of [[the 198]]. Her hair is  restored by the healing powers of [[Mister M (comics)|Mr. M]] whom she starts a relationship with not long after.

==Powers and abilities==
Lorelei has [[prehensile]] hair, which allows her to animate her hair through mental control.
