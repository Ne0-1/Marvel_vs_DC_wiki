'''Lorelei Travis''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[mutant (fictional)|mutant]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] Universe. Her first appearance was in ''District X #1''.

==History==
'''Lorelei Travis''' is the name of a young girl who has "living" hair. She is one of the few mutants to retain her powers after the effects of [[Decimation (comics)| M-Day]], although a group of militant [[Purity (comics)|Purity]] members cuts all of her hair out of vengeance. Unlikely help comes in the form of [[Toad (comics)|Toad]] who, after saving her, escorts her to the [[X-Mansion]] to join the other members of [[the 198]]. Her hair is  restored by the healing powers of [[Mister M (comics)|Mr. M]] who she starts a relationship with not long after.

==Notes==
Other [[Marvel Comics|Marvel]] characters known as '''Lorelei''' have been:
*The [[Asgardians (comics)|Asgardian]] sister of [[Enchantress (Marvel Comics)|Amora the Enchantress]].  [[Lorelei (Marvel Comics)|Lorelei]] was a former pawn of [[Pluto (comics)|Pluto]] and succeeded in briefly seducing [[Thor (Marvel Comics)|Thor]] by tricking him into drinking golden mead and she first appeared in ''Thor'' vol. 1 #337
*[[Mantis (Marvel Comics)|Mantis]] briefly took the name '''Lorelei''' as revealed in the ''Official Handbook of the Marvel Universe: Avengers 2005''.
*[[Lorelei (Savage Land Mutate)|Lorelei]] is also the codename/possible real name of one of the [[Savage Land Mutates]] and former member of the [[Brotherhood of Evil Mutants]].  She first appeared in ''X-Men'' vol. 1 #63.
