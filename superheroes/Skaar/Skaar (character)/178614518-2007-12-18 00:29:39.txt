'''Skaar''' is a [[fictional character]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]], created by [[Greg Pak]]. He is the son of the [[Hulk (comics)|Hulk]] and the Shadow Warrior [[Caiera|Caiera the Oldstrong]], conceived during the "Planet Hulk" storyline. He will star in his own ongoing series, ''Skaar: Son of Hulk'', which launches in [[March 2008]].<ref>http://forum.newsarama.com/showthread.php?t=136412</ref><ref>http://www.comicbookresources.com/news/newsitem.cgi?id=12387</ref><ref>http://comics.ign.com/articles/835/835501p2.html</ref> Skaar first appeared in ''[[World War Hulk]]'' #5 (November 2007).<ref>''World War Hulk'' #5</ref>

==Alternate Versions==
===What If?===
In the ''[[List of What If issues#Sixth Series (2007)|What If?]]'' storyline ''What if Caiera the Oldstrong had survived the destruction of Sakaar instead of the Hulk?'', a 21 year old version of Skaar appeared near the end (Interestingly, he was only partly seen; Most of him was  obscured by shadows).
