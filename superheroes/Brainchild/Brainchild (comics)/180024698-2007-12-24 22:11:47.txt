'''Brainchild''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Universe]].

==Fictional character biography==
Brainchild was once a [[Savage Land Races#Swamp Men|Swamp Man]] of the [[Savage Land]], a tropical preserve hidden in Antarctica.  He became one of the [[Savage Land Mutates]], changed by [[Magneto (comics)|Magneto]] to give him unusually high intelligence, although he at least formerly only had the emotional level of a child.  Brainchild was one of Magneto's first Savage Land Mutates, and has been involved in all the Mutates' subsequent activities.
