'''Attuma''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[supervillain]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]]. He is an [[Atlantis|Atlantean]] warlord who is the nemesis of [[Namor the Sub-Mariner]], and is the father of the superhero [[Andromeda (Marvel Comics)|Andromeda]]. He first appeared in ''[[Fantastic Four]]'' volume 1 #33 (1964).
