'''Sun Girl (Mary Mitchell)''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Comics]] Universe.  She first appeared in ''Sun Girl'' #1 (August 1948).

==History==
A personal secretary for the [[Jim Hammond|original Human Torch]] in [[World War II]], Mary Mitchell fell in love with him and became his partner as well as his sidekick, after [[Toro (comics)|Toro]] left to tend to his ailing foster mother.
She fought alongside the Torch until Toro returned and replaced her.
