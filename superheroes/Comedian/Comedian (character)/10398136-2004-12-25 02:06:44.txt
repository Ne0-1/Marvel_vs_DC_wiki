'''The Comedian''' is a [[fictional]] [[superhero]] who is a central character in the classic [[comic]] book series, ''[[Watchmen]]'' (by [[Alan Moore]] and [[Dave Gibbons]], published by [[DC Comics]]). He is a modified version of the character [[Peacemaker (comics)|The Peacemaker]] from [[Charlton Comics]].

His civilian name is Edward Morgan Blake. He was born in [[1924]], and the storyline sets off when he is killed in [[1985]].

The key element of his personality was that he saw life as a dark joke and laughed at his own behaviour which was typically antisocial and violent in the extreme.
