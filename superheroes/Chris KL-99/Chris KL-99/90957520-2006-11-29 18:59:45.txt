'''Chris KL-99''' is a 1950s [[science fiction]] [[comic book]] character whose adventures were published by [[DC Comics]]. He first appears in ''[[Strange Adventures]]'' #1 (August 1950), and was created by science fiction author [[Edmond Hamilton]] and artist Howard Sherman.

==Fictional character biography==
He was created/written by science fiction author Edmond Hamilton, and is loosely based on pulp-magazine character [[Captain Future]].

Chris KL-99's adventures usually involved space exploration. There is a subplot revolving around the search for his parents who went missing a number of years previously.

==Brief bibliography==
His first appearance came in 1950's ''[[Strange Adventures]]'' #1. He appeared in issues #1-3, 5, 7, 9, 11, 15. His origin was given in issue #7. He was assisted by Halk a Martian giant, and Jero a diminutive Venusian.

He has not appeared since that time, except for an appearance in ''[[Secret Origins]]'' #43, and brief cameos in ''[[DC Comics Presents]]'' #78 and ''[[Crisis on Infinite Earths]]'' #10.
