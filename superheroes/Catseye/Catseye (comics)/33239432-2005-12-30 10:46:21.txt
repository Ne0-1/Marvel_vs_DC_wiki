'''Catseye''' (real name: '''Sharon Smith''') is a [[fictional character]], a [[mutant (fiction)|mutant]] in the [[Marvel Universe]]. She was created by [[Chris Claremont]] and [[Sal Buscema]] in ''New Mutants'' vol. 1 #16 ([[1984]]). Catseye was a member of the original [[Hellions]]. She was killed by [[Trevor Fitzroy]] in ''Uncanny X-Men'' #281 ([[1991]]). Catseye's mutant power is the ability to transform into either a housecat or a human-panther hybrid. In her panther form, she has superhuman strength, agility and reflexes, in addition to keen senses, razor-sharp claws and a [[prehensile tail]].
