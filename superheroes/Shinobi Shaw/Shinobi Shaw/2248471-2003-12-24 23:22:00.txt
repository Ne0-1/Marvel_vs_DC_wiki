'''Shinobi Shaw''' is a fictional comic book villain featured in various titles published by the [[Marvel Comics]] Group.
Shinobi Shaw is a foe of the [[X-Men]] and [[Wolverine (comics)|Wolverine]].
He is the son of [[Sebastian Shaw (comics)|Sebastian Shaw]], the leader of [[the Hellfire Club (comics)|the Hellfire Club]].
Some of Shinobi Shaw's body is prosthetic as a result of injuries suffured battling Wolverine.
