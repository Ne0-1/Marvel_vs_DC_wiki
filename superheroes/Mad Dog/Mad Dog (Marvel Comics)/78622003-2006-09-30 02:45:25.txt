'''Mad Dog (Robert "Buzz" Baxter)''' is a [[fictional character]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] Universe. His first appearance was in ''Defenders vol. 1 #125''.

==History==
Buzz Baxter is a [[mercenary]] and is the former security chief of the [[Brand Corporation]].  He also was a [[colonel]] in the [[United States Air Force|USAF]].  He is a former agent of the [[Secret Empire]] and also for [[Roxxon]].  He directed [[Mutant Force]] and is the ex-husband of [[Hellcat (comics)|Patsy Walker]].  He was mutated by the Brand Corporation.

==Notes==
Although officially the only character to be known as '''Mad Dog''', there have been other characters who has used a Mad Dog type moniker.  This include:
*[[Spider-Man (comics)|Peter Parker]], who was known as '''Mad Dog #336''' in ''Amazing Spider-Man'' vol. 1 #295.
*[[Captain Zero]] was known as '''Mad Dog #736''' in ''Amazing Spider-Man'' vol. 1 #295.
*[[Brainstorm (comics)|Brainstorm]] was known as ''Mad Dog #2020, also in ''Amazing Spider-Man'' vol. 1 #295.
*In ''Combat Kelly and the Deadly Dozen'' #6 there was a character called '''"Mad Dog" Martin'''.
*And is ''Thor'' vol. 1 #426, a character called '''"Mad Dog" Rassitanio''' appeared.
