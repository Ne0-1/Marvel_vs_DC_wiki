'''Equinox''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[supervillain]] appearing in the [[Marvel Universe]]. He can generate both intense [[heat]] and extreme cold similarly to the heroes the [[Human Torch]] and [[Iceman (comics)|Iceman]] (with whom he first battled). He has also come into conflict with a number of other [[superhero|superheroes]], including [[Spider-Man]], [[Henry Pym|Yellowjacket]], the [[Wasp (comics)|Wasp]], and the [[Falcon (comics)|Falcon]].
