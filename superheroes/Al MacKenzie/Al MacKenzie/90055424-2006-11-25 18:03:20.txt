'''Al MacKenzie''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Universe]].  He was once the [[Central Intelligence Agency|C.I.A.]] liaison to [[S.H.I.E.L.D.]]  He became romantically involved with Contessa [[Valentina Allegra di Fontaine]] after awhile, which led to an estrangement between him and [[Nick Fury]].  Because of this, he returned to the CIA with the Contessa.

MacKenzie later joined the S.H.I.E.L.D. organization full time, and is now the senior liaison officer to the C.I.A.
