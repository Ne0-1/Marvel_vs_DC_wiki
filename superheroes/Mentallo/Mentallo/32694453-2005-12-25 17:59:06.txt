'''Mentallo''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[supervillain]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]].

He is a mutant telepath of moderate abilty, who uses technology to increase his power.  He once formed a partnership with the [[Fixer (comics)|Fixer]].

His most recent major role in the [[Marvel Comics]] Universe was using his telepathy as part of a conspiracy to break out of prison and acquire a device that would allow his employer to control all the supervillains who ever worked for Justin Hammer.
