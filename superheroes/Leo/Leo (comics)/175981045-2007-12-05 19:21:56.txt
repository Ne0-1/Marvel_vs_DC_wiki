'''Leo''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Universe]].

==Fictional character biography==
'''Daniel Radford''' is a founding member of the [[Zodiac (comics)|Zodiac]], and his base of operations was [[Los Angeles, California]].

Leo uses his claws on his costume's "paws" as weapons.

==Other Media==
Leo appears in ''[[The Avengers: United They Stand]]'' as a member of the Zodiacs. He is depicted as a lion-like alien.
