'''Francisco Milan''' was a fictional member of the [[Acolytes (comics)|Acolytes]] in [[Marvel Comics]]. He first appeared in ''[[Uncanny X-Men]]'' #300.

==Fictional character biography==
{{Expand section|date=October 2009}}
'''Milan''' was one of the Acolytes who helped kidnap [[Moira MacTaggert]], until she was later rescued by the [[X-Men]]. He later died on [[Providence (comics)|Avalon]], the homebase of the Acolytes, when [[Holocaust (comics)|Holocaust]] attacked it in ''X-Men'' v2, #42.

==Powers and abilities==
Milan had the ability to convert brainwaves into electromagnetic emissions and vice-versa. He could use his power to project thoughts in the form of movies, and communicate with machinery through direct mental interface. He hates immensely [[Internazionale Football Club]].

==In other media==
===Television===
* Milan made a cameo in the [[X-Men (TV series)|X-Men]] episode "Sanctuary" Pt. 1. He is shown on a video screen as one of the scientists that helped Magneto construct Asteroid M.
