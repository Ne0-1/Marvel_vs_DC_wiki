'''Francisco Milan''' was a fictional member of the [[Acolytes (comics)|Acolytes]] in [[Marvel Comics]]. He first appeared in ''[[Uncanny X-Men]]'' #300.

==History ==
'''Milan''' was one of the Acolytes who helped kidnap [[Moira MacTaggert]], until she was later rescued by the [[X-Men]]. He later died on [[Avalon (comics)|Avalon]], the homebase of the Acolytes, when [[Holocaust (comics)|Holocaust]] attacked it in ''X-Men'' (2nd series) #42.

== Powers and abilities ==

Milan had the ability to interface directly (and communicate) with machinery. This was usually achieved by plugging a computer data cable into one eye of his visor.
