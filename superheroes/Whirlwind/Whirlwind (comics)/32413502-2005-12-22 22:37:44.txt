Whirlwind is a [[fictional character]] in the [[Marvel Universe]]. Usually a nemesis of the [[Avengers (comics)|Avengers]], he is a mutant with superhuman strength, speed, and balance.

==Powers==

Superspeed, normally used to spin rapidly, so as to create a whilrwind (hence the name). However, he has demonstrated ground speed sufficient to outrace [[Quicksilver]]. His powers come from his being a mutant.
