'''Goom''' is a fictional extraterrestrial character from the [[Marvel Universe]] who first appeared in ''Tales of Suspense'' vol. 1 #15.

==History==
'''Goom''' is an alien from Planet X who crashed to Earth.  He was captured by [[S.H.I.E.L.D.]] and placed in their [[Nick Fury's Howling Commandos|Howling Commandos]] Monster Force.  It is possibly that he also dated Shivoor.  He had advanced technology which was seen in ''Tales of Suspense'' vol. 1 #15 including a spaceship and a time machine, which had the ability to change the age of others.
In ''Incredible Hulk Annual'' #5, [[Xemnu]] created a duplicate of Goom which was later destroyed by the Hulk.

==Powers==
'''Goom''' has mental powers and an immense head.
