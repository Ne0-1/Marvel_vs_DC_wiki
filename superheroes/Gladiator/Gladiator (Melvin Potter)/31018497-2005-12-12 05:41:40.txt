'''Gladiator''' (Melvin Potter) is a reformed [[supervillain]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]].

[[Image:Glad.jpg|Cover to Spectacular Spider-Man #77, featuring [[Spider-Man]] and Gladiator. Art by Al Milgrom.|thumb|200px]] 

==Character history==

Potter was one of the first enemies of the superhero [[Daredevil (comics)|Daredevil]]. In civilian life, he is a costume-designer. He eventually reformed and became one of the staunchest supporters of Daredevil.

During the "[[Daredevil: Born Again|Born Again]]" storyline, he was pressured into producing a Daredevil costume for a madman in the employ of the crimelord known as the [[Kingpin (comics)|Kingpin]]. Much later, Daredevil deposed the Kingpin and took control of Hell's Kitchen, and Potter became the hero's personal assistant. He was later coerced into working for the Kingpin's elderly predecessor, who attempted to kill Murdock and assume his old mantle but who instead died of a drug-induced heart attack.

==Powers and abilities==
Gladiator has no superhuman abilities. However, He is a superb martial arts fighter and very powerful. He wore a thick metal armour and was armed with an arsenal of edged weapons and whirling blades protruding from his wrists. 

==Ultimate Gladiator==
In the [[Ultimate Marvel]] continuity, Gladiator has a cameo appereance. He appears as an enemy of [[Ultimate Spider-Man|Spider-Man]], and is a crazed madman obsessed with Emperor [[Nurhachi]].
