'''''Empire''''' is a comic book series produced by [[DC Comics]], created by [[Mark Waid]] and [[Barry Kitson]]

The protagonist of the series is a [[Doctor Doom]]-like supervillain named [[Golgoth]] who has successfully defeated all superheroes and conquered the world, but must now contend with internal power struggles. The series was originally published by [[Gorilla Comics]], a company formed by Waid, [[Kurt Busiek]] and several others, but the company folded after only two issues were produced.  ''Empire'' was completed under the DC Comics label but is in its own distinct universe.

The complete mini-series has been collected into a graphic novel.  ''Empire'' ISBN 1-4012-0212-8.
