'''Thundra''' is a [[fictional character]] who exists in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]].  She is a [[barbarian]] from a [[matriarchy|matriarchal]] future timeline.  She was created by [[Stan Lee]] and [[John Romita, Sr.|John Romita]] for ''[[Savage Tales]]'' #1.

Thundra has [[superhuman]] strength and endurance, and is a skilled combatant with a sword or a chain, the latter of which is her weapon of choice.  She has battled against and alongside the [[Fantastic Four]] and the [[The Avengers (comics)|Avengers]].  She was briefly allied with the duplicate [[Hyperion (comics)|Hyperion]] by the [[Roxxon Oil Company]]; she later became the consort of the extra-dimensional warlord [[Arkon (comics)|Arkon]].
