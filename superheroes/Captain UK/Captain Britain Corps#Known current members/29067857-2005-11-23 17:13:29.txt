In [[Marvel comics]], '''the Captain Britain Corps''' is a league of super-heroes all known as [[Captain Britain]].  They are each essentially the same hero except they each come from an alternate reality.  They sometimes team up to face huge threats and are organized by the mystical entity Roma.
