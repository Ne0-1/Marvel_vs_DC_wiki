'''Black Marvel''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Comics]] Universe. He originally appeared in 1941 on the pages of ''Mystic Comics''. By contracutally binding himself with the archdemon Mephisto, he was able to obtain 4 superpowered costumes and start the crime fighting group known as the [[Slingers (comics)|Slingers]].
