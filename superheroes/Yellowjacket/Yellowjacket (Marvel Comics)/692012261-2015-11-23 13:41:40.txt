'''Yellowjacket''' is the name of several [[Character (arts)|fictional character]]s appearing in [[comic books]] published by [[Marvel Comics]]. Most of these versions exist in Marvel's main [[shared universe]], known as the [[Marvel Universe]].

==Hank Pym==
{{main|Hank Pym}}

'''[[Hank Pym|Dr. Henry "Hank" Pym]]''' is the first character to take on the Yellowjacket codename in the modern mainstream Marvel Comics.  He was also known as [[Ant-Man]], [[Giant-Man]], [[Goliath (comics)|Goliath]].  The character has been associated with several superhero teams in the Marvel Universe, including the [[Avengers (comics)|Avengers]] and the [[Defenders (comics)|Defenders]]. After a while he then later gives up the mantle and mentors [[Scott Lang]] as the new Ant-Man.

==Rita DeMara==
{{main|Yellowjacket (Rita DeMara)}}

'''[[Yellowjacket (Rita DeMara)|Rita DeMara]]''' is the second character to take on the Yellowjacket codename in the modern mainstream comics.  She debuted as a [[supervillainess]], joining the supervillain groups known as the [[Masters of Evil]] and the [[Femizons]].  DeMara later became a [[superheroine]] and joined the ranks of the [[Avengers (comics)|Avengers]] and the [[Guardians of the Galaxy (1969 team)|Guardians of the Galaxy]].  She was killed by the supervillain [[Immortus]],<ref>''Avengers: The Crossing'' #1</ref> but was later resurrected in the ''[[Chaos War (comics)|Chaos War]]'' storyline.<ref>''Chaos War'' #2</ref>

==Other versions==

===Ultimate Marvel===
Before Hank Pym, the original [[Ultimate Marvel]] incarnation of Yellowjacket is '''[[Ultron]]''', a robot designed to be an expendable super soldier.<ref>''Ultimates 3'' #1 - 4 (Feb. - May 2008); #5 (Nov. 2008)</ref> He is later killed when Hank Pym, as Ant-Man, rips his head off. In ''Ultimatum'', Hank takes up the mantle of Yellowjacket, wanting to make up for what his creation did.{{issue|date=October 2015}}
