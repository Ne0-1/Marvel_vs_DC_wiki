'''Jim Morita''' is a fictional character appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]].

==Publication history==
Created by [[Roy Thomas]] and [[Dick Ayers]], Jim Morita first appeared in ''[[Sgt. Fury and his Howling Commandos]]'' #38 (January 1967).

==Fictional character biography==
Jim Morita is a Japanese-American [[soldier]] during [[World War II]]. Morita is a [[Nisei]] soldier whose squadron assisted [[Nick Fury|Sergeant Fury]]'s [[Sgt. Fury and his Howling Commandos|Howling Commandos]] on several missions. His squad was at one point captured and sent to a [[Nazi Party|Nazi]] Prison Camp, but were broken out by the Commandos.<ref>{{Cite comic |writer = [[Mike Friedrich]] |penciller = Dick Ayers |story = The Informer |title = Sgt. Fury and his Howling Commandos |issue = 57 |date = August 1968 |publisher = Marvel Comics}}</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
Jim Morita was a trained soldier.
