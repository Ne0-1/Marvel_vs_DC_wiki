'''Virtuoso''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[supervillain]] in the [[DC Universe]]. Created by [[Gail Simone]] and [[Dale Eaglesham]], she first appeared in ''[[Villains United]]'' #5 (November 2005).

==Fictional Biography==

Virtuoso first appears as part of a group of [[Secret Society of Super Villains|Society]] members launching an assault of the [[Secret Six (comics)|Secret Six]] and their headquarters called '[[The House of Secrets]]'. The Secret Six have long opposed the Society.

Among Virtuoso's allies are [[Deathstroke (comics)|Deathstroke]], [[Giganta]], and [[Solomon Grundy (comics)|Solomon Grundy]]. During the battle, Virtuoso is stabbed in the shoulder.

Not much is known about her.  She seems to have acquired the powers and [[violin]] of the late [[Fiddler (comics)|Fiddler]], who had been executed in a previous Secret Six mission.
