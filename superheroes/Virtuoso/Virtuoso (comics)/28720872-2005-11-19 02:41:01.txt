'''Virtuoso''' first appeared in ''[[Villains United]] #6'' as part of a group of [[Secret Society of Supervillains|Society]] members launching an assault of the [[Secret Six (comics)|Secret Six]]. Not much is known about her, only she seems to have acquired the powers and [[violin]] of [[Fiddler (comics)|the Fiddler]].
