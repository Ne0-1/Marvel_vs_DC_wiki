In the [[Crisis on Infinite Earths]] [[comic book]] mini-series, '''Lady Quark''' was one of the rulers of her home world, [[Multiverse (DC Comics)|Earth-Six]].  She had the ability to alter matter on the subatomic level.  Her husband, Lord Volt, and daughter, Princess Fern, were both killed when Earth-Six was destroyed.  She was saved at the last minute by [[Pariah (comics)|Pariah]].
