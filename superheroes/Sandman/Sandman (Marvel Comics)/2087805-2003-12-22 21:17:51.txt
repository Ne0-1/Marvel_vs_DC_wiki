'''The Sandman''' is a [[comic book]] [[supervillain]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]], and an enemy of [[Spider-Man]].  <!-- When did he first appear? -->

The Sandman has the ability to transform into a malleable [[sand]]-like substance which can be hardened, dispersed, or shaped according to his will.  He is also immensely strong.  This ability was the result of accidental [[radiation]] bombardment.

At one point he reformed and became a member of [[The Avengers (comics)|The Avengers]], but he later reverted to his villainous ways.
