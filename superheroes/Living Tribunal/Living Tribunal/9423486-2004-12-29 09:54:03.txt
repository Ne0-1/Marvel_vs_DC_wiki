The '''Living Tribunal''' is a [[fictional character]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|Universe]]. The Living Tribunal is an [[omnipotence|omnipotent]] entity that is empowered by an unknown force, the "One Above All" ([[God]]), to pass judgment on beings capable of changing the universe and to maintain the "cosmic balance of power." The Living Tribunal is usually the most powerful being in the entirety of the Marvel Universe.
