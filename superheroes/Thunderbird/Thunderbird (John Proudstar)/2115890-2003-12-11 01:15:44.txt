'''Thunderbird''' (real name '''John Proudstar''') is a [[comic book]] [[superhero]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]]. He was one of the second generation of characters to join the [[X-Men]] along with [[Nightcrawler]], [[Colossus (comics)|Colossus]], [[Banshee (comics)|Banshee]], [[Storm (comics)|Storm]], and [[Wolverine (comics)|Wolverine]].  He was killed on a mission shortly after he joined the team. He was a [[Native American]] Indian ([[Apache]]) who had once been a corporal in the [[US Marines]].

John Proudstar is survived by a younger brother, '''James Proudstar''', who currently also uses the name '''Thunderbird'''.  James Proudstar has similar abilities yet much greater physical strength.
