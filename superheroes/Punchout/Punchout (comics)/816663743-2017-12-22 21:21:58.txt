'''Punchout''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[Mutant (Marvel Comics)|mutant]] appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]]. Her first appearance was in ''Uncanny X-Men #235''.

==Fictional character biography==

The super-strong mutant known only as Punchout was a member of the super-powered [[Press Gang (comics)|Press Gang]], law enforcers, from the island nation of [[Genosha]].

After repeated disruptions to the Genoshan government, the island fell into a civil war.{{Issue|date=May 2010}} Punchout stayed with a small group of Press Gang members, fighting against the rebels. In one battle, she was knocked down by [[Jennifer Ransome]].{{Issue|date=May 2010}}

With the genocide of the entire Genoshan population by [[Sentinel (comics)|Sentinels]] controlled by [[Cassandra Nova]],{{Issue|date=May 2010}} Punchout's final fate is unknown. It is unknown if she retained or lost her mutant powers during the [[Decimation (comics)|M-Day]].

==Powers and abilities==
She has superhuman strength and endurance.
