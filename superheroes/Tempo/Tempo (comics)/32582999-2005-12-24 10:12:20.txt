'''Tempo''' is a fictional [[comic book]] character in the [[Marvel universe]]. She is a black, female [[mutant (fictional)|mutant]] that appears to be from the southern [[United States]]. Her mutant ability allows her to cause time to flow at different rates for individuals. The person she affects may be tired after the effect relative to its duration. She was a member of both incarnations of the [[Mutant Liberation Front]], and enemy of the [[X-Men]], [[X-Factor]], [[New Mutants]], and [[X-Force]]. She eventually betrayed the group, and left to live her own life.
