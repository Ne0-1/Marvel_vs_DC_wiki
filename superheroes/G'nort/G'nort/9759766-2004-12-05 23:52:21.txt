'''G'nort''' is a [[fictional character]] in the [[DC Comics]] [[DC Universe|universe]]. G'nort was a member of the [[Green Lantern Corps]] and later a [[Darkstar (DC Comics)|Darkstar]]. He resembles a [[humanoid]] [[dog]] and is throughouly incompetant and generally disliked by other heroes; in fact, he is usually portrayed as being a pathetic loser in the spirit of characters such as [[The Elongated Man]]. He was a member of the [[Justice League Antarctica]].
