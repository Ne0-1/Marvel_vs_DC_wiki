'''Phantazia''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[mutant]] [[supervillain]]ess in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]].  She possesses extensive powers based on [[electromagnetism]], and can use these powers to dampen others' superhuman powers.  Named Eileen Harsaw, she is an intelligent woman, apparently possessing a PhD, and she was once part of [[Toad (comics)|Toad]]'s [[Brotherhood of Evil Mutants]] until the team disbanded.  For the past couple years, estimating to about 18, she has never appeared in another comicbook until now. She has recently appeared in the House of M series arc.
