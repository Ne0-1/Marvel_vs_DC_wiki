'''Finn Cooley''' is a [[Fictional character|fictional]] [[supervillain|villain]], an enemy of the [[Punisher]] from the [[Marvel Comics]] MAX ''[[Punisher]]'' series.  He was created by writer [[Garth Ennis]] and artist [[Leandro Fernandez (artist)|Leandro Fernandez]], and first appears in the story titled ''Kitchen Irish''.

==History==
Finn Cooley is a hideously [[disfigurement|disfigured]] [[Ireland|Irish]] [[criminal]], a [[mob boss]] and a [[bomb|bombs expert]]. Cooley must wear a facial mask as half his face was blown off in an explosives accident.

==Powers and abilities==
Since Finn has no superpowers. He is a bombs expert and has decent hand to hand combat skills. 

==Other Media==
===Video games===
* Finn Cooley is a playable character in the action game ''[[The Punisher: No Mercy]]'' for [[Playstation 3]] via the [[PSN]].
