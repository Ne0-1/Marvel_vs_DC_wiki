'''Lord Dark Wind''' (Professor '''Kenji Oyama''') is a fictional character appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]]. He is the father of [[X-Men]] [[supervillainess]] [[Lady Deathstrike]] and [[Lord Deathstrike]] and the inventor of the [[adamantium]] bonding process.

==Publication history==
{{expand-section|date=June 2015}}
The character first appeared in ''[[Daredevil (Marvel Comics series)|Daredevil]]'' #196 (July 1983).<ref>''Daredevil'' Vol. 1 #196-199</ref> He was created by [[Denny O'Neil]], [[Larry Hama]], and Klaus Jensen.

==Fictional character biography==
Professor Kenji Oyama was a [[Japan]]ese [[scientist]] who was also a powerful [[Boss (crime)|crime lord]] known as Lord Dark Wind. Kenji is most famous for creating the [[adamantium]]-to-bone bonding process that would be used on Wolverine, [[Bullseye (comics)|Bullseye]], and others. He is also the father of Yuriko Oyama, the woman who would later become [[Lady Deathstrike]], and Lord Deathstrike. 

A Japanese kamikaze pilot during [[World War II]], the bombs on his plane failed to explode when he crashed into an American aircraft carrier, and he miraculously survived, though his face was horribly scarred.<ref>''Daredevil'' Vol. 1 #197</ref> He wrote a book about his experiences and made a fortune from its sales, which he used to buy a private island and set himself up as a lord.<ref name="DD198"/> During this time he married and had three children. Feeling shamed by his failure decades earlier, he permanentally adorned his face with a black cowl and scarred the faces of his children in a ritual design.<ref name="DD198">''Daredevil'' Vol. 1 #198</ref> Seeing [[mercantilism]] as inherently dishonorable,<ref name="DD199">''Daredevil'' Vol. 1 #199</ref> he began funding violent political activity, and ordered his two sons to assassinate the Japanese prime minister; they died in the attempt.<ref name="DD198"/>

Intending to employ the assassin [[Bullseye (comics)|Bullseye]], he freed him from prison and performed surgeries to replace his damaged vertebrae with adamantium.<ref>''Daredevil'' Vol. 1 #196-198</ref> [[Daredevil (Marvel Comics character)|Daredevil]] pursued Bullseye to his estate. His daughter Yuriko allied herself with Daredevil. To free her lover, Kiro, from her father's servitude, and to gain vengeance for her scarring and the death of her two brothers, she slew Lord Dark Wind.<ref name="DD198"/> Yuriko would later belatedly embrace her father's ideals and go on a quest to track down the person who dishonored him.<ref>''Alpha Flight'' Vol. 1 #33-34</ref>

==Other media==
===Television===
* In ''[[X-Men (TV series)|X-Men]]'' episode "Out of the Past," [[Professor Thorton]] of the Weapon-X project mentions that the adamantium bonding process was developed by '''Professor Oyama'''. Oyama was evidently killed during Wolverine's rampage through the Weapon-X facility, causing Lady Deathstrike to seek revenge.

===Video games===
* Lord Dark Wind is mentioned by Yuriko in the game ''[[X2: Wolverine's Revenge]]''.
