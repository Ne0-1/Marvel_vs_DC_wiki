'''Sirocco''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[Mutant (Marvel Comics)|mutant]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] Universe. His first appearance was in ''New Mutants Annual #7''.

==Fictional character biography==
Sirocco was part of the [[Iraq]]i mercenary team called [[Desert Sword]] when they fought [[Freedom Force (comics)|Freedom Force]], led by [[Crimson Commando]]. Desert Sword was to prevent Freedom Force from liberating a [[Germans|German]] scientist named Reinhold Kurtzmann in [[Kuwait]]. Desert Sword ambushed them and Aminedi decapitated [[Super Sabre]] and cut off Crimson Commando's right arm.<ref>''New Mutants Annual'' #7 (1991)</ref> Kurtzmann was captured by Freedom force but then recaptured by Desert Sword. [[Pyro (Marvel Comics)|Pyro]] still managed to kill Kurtzmann though.<ref>''X-Men Annual'' #15 (1991)</ref> Sirocco and [[Aminedi]] trapped [[Avalanche (comics)|Avalanche]] and Commando in a mine field and as Commando's condition worsened Avalanche trapped the two under a pile of rock, though they later escaped, and rode a wave of dirt to their designated extraction point and got him to safety, leaving both [[Blob (comics)|Blob]] and Pyro who were later captured by Desert Sword.<ref>''X-Factor Annual'' #6 (1991)</ref>

It is unknown if Sirocco retained his powers after [[Decimation (comics)|M-Day]].

==Powers and abilities==
Sirocco can warp nearby weather patterns to create swirling air funnels that can strike with concussive impact or lift him into the air.
