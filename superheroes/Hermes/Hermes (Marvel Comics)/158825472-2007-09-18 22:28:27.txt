'''Hermes''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Universe]].

Hermes is the herald of [[Zeus (Marvel Comics)|Zeus]] in the [[Olympians (Marvel Comics)|Olympian pantheon]].

Hermes can run and fly at speeds excelling those of any other Olympian god or goddess.

Hermes is not to be confused with the [[Eternals (comics)|Eternal]] [[Makkari (comics)|Makkari]].

[[Zeus (Marvel Comics)|Zeus]] is his father.
