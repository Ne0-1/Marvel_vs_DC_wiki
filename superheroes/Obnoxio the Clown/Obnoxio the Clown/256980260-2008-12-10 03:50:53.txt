'''Obnoxio the Clown''' was the [[mascot]] of the [[Marvel Comics]] humor magazine ''[[Crazy (magazine)|Crazy]]''.<ref name="obnoxio">[http://daveslongbox.blogspot.com/2005/04/obnoxio-clown-marvel-comics-1983.html Obnoxio the Clown] information, Retrieved August 2008.</ref> 

== Character ==
Obnoxio was portrayed as a slovenly, vulgar, cigar-puffing middle-aged man in a perpetually torn and dirty [[clown suit]], with a dyspeptic and [[cynicism|cynical]] attitude.

== Comic book ==
'''''Obnoxio the Clown''''' was also the title of a one-shot [[Marvel Comics|Marvel]] [[comic book]] that was published in April 1983.<ref name="obnoxio" /> The plot centered around Obnoxio the Clown as a villain and unlikely ally against the [[X-Men]].<ref name="obnoxio" /> The comic book was only published once, and only issue number one exists.

The solo issue of the comic book was written and illustrated by [[Alan Kupperberg]].<ref>Comic Collector live. [http://www.comiccollectorlive.com/LiveData/Issue.aspx?id=acf47b1c-3668-4a8b-952f-9de5123de7c4 Obnoxio The Clown #1.] Retrieved November 2008.</ref>
