'''Obnoxio the Clown''' was a [[character (arts)|character]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] humor magazine ''[[Crazy (magazine)|Crazy]]''.<ref name="obnoxio">[http://daveslongbox.blogspot.com/2005/04/obnoxio-clown-marvel-comics-1983.html Obnoxio the Clown] information, Accessed August 2008.</ref>

==Character==
Obnoxio was portrayed as a slovenly, vulgar, cigar-puffing middle-aged man in a torn and dirty [[clown suit]], with a dyspeptic and [[Cynicism (contemporary)|cynical]] attitude.

==Comic book==
'''''Obnoxio the Clown''''' was also the title of a one-shot [[Marvel Comics|Marvel]] [[comic book]] that was published in April 1983.<ref name="obnoxio" /> The plot centered on Obnoxio the Clown as a villain and unlikely ally against the [[X-Men]].<ref name="obnoxio" /> He and the group, at the [[X-Mansion]], 'team-up' against Eye Scream, a villain that can transform into various types of ice cream.

The sole issue of the comic book was written, illustrated, colored, and lettered by [[Alan Kupperberg]];<ref>Comic Collector live. [http://www.comiccollectorlive.com/LiveData/Issue.aspx?id=acf47b1c-3668-4a8b-952f-9de5123de7c4 Obnoxio The Clown #1.] Accessed November 2008.</ref> in a comment which Kupperberg attributes to [[Peter David]],<ref>[http://www.alankupperberg.com/comicbook.html Kupperberg interview] from ''[[Comic Book Artist]]'' #16, archived at AlanKupperberg.com</ref> and which David attributes to [[James Owsley]],<ref>[http://www.peterdavid.net/index.php/2010/05/07/not-in-any-store/ Not in Any Store…], by Peter David, from [[Comics Buyer's Guide]] #1003, February 5, 1993; archived at PeterDavid.net</ref> the comic was described as "Written, penciled, inked and lettered by Alan Kupperberg. Yes, it’s untouched by human hands."

==In other media==
===Television===
* Obnoxio makes a cameo appearance in the ''[[Hulk and the Agents of S.M.A.S.H.]]'' episode "Fear Itself", voiced by [[John DiMaggio]]. The Agents of S.M.A.S.H. receive old television signals including a TV show starring Obnoxio which freaks [[Rick Jones (comics)|A-Bomb]] out. He somewhat appears later in the episode as an part of an illusion caused by the [[Null (comics)|Null]] that releases people's fears where Null unleashes this illusion on A-Bomb.

===Video games===
* Android replicas of Obnoxio the Clown appears as a enemies during [[Wolverine]] levels in [[Spider-Man/X-Men: Arcade's Revenge]].

* Though he is not seen in the game, Obnoxio the Clown is briefly mentioned in the [[Facebook]] game ''[[Marvel: Avengers Alliance]]''. Obnoxio the Clown is mentioned to have been murdered by the Circle of Eight.

===Novel===
* In the alternate reality depicted in the "Doctor Doom: The Chaos Trilogy" novels, written by Steven A. Roman, Obnoxio has a popular slapstick comedy show. Like anything on television, the show is approved by Doctor Doom's administration.{{Citation needed|date=April 2010}}
