'''Obnoxio the Clown''' was the [[mascot]] of the [[Marvel Comics]] humor magazine ''[[Crazy (magazine)|Crazy]]''.<ref name="obnoxio">[http://daveslongbox.blogspot.com/2005/04/obnoxio-clown-marvel-comics-1983.html Obnoxio the Clown] information, Accessed August 2008.</ref> 

== Character ==
Obnoxio was portrayed as a slovenly, vulgar, cigar-puffing middle-aged man in a torn and dirty [[clown suit]], with a dyspeptic and [[cynicism|cynical]] attitude.

== Comic book ==
'''''Obnoxio the Clown''''' was also the title of a one-shot [[Marvel Comics|Marvel]] [[comic book]] that was published in April 1983 <ref name="obnoxio" /> The plot centered around Obnoxio the Clown as a villain and unlikely ally against the [[X-Men]].<ref name="obnoxio" /> He and the group, at the [[X-Mansion]], 'team-up' against a villain that can transform into various types of ice cream. The comic book was only published once, and only issue number one exists.

The solo issue of the comic book was written and illustrated by [[Alan Kupperberg]].<ref>Comic Collector live. [http://www.comiccollectorlive.com/LiveData/Issue.aspx?id=acf47b1c-3668-4a8b-952f-9de5123de7c4 Obnoxio The Clown #1.] Accessed November 2008.</ref>

== Cultural references ==
* Obnoxio calls the [[X-Men]] the “yeechs-men” in the first issue of Obnoxio the Clown.<ref name="obnoxio" />

== Novel ==
In the alternate reality depicted in the "Doctor Doom: The Chaos Trilogy" novels, written by [[Steven A. Roman]], Obnoxio has a popular slapstick comedy show. Like anything on television, the show is approved by Doctor Doom's administration.
