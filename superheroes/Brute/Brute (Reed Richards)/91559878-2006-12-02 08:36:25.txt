'''Brute''' is a [[fictional character]] featured in [[Marvel Comics]] publications, most notably ''[[Fantastic Four]]''.

Brute is a version of Reed Richards ([[Mister Fantastic]], leader of the [[Fantastic Four]]) from a world called Counter Earth. When he becomes the Brute, he possesses [[superhuman]] strength. He infiltrated the Fantastic Four by posing as Mister Fantastic during a time when the other Reed had lost his stretching powers. The Brute was later a member of the [[Frightful Four]].
