'''Vixen''' (alter ego, Fashion Model, Mari Jiwe McCabe) is a [[superhero]] in the [[DC Comics]] [[DC Universe|universe]]. She was nearly the first female African-American DC superhero to star in her own series, but the first issue of her series was cancelled in the "DC Implosion" in 1978. (It was subsequently printed in ''[[Cancelled Comics Cavalcade]]''.) Her first appearance distributed to the public was in ''[[Action Comics]]'' #521 (1981).

She was a member of the [[Justice League]] during the "Justice League Detroit" era. She has had memberships with [[Suicide Squad]], [[Checkmate(comics)|Checkmate]], and [[The Ultramarines (comics)|the Ultramarines]], and has teamed up with the similarly-powered hero [[Animal Man]]. While a member of the Squad she fell in love with and had a failed relationship with the [[Bronze Tiger]].

==Other media==
[[Image:Vixen JLU.jpg|thumb|left|250px|Vixen in ''Justice League Unlimited'']]
Her character was introduced in the [[Cartoon Network]] animated series ''[[Justice League Unlimited]]'' as a fashion model and the girlfriend of [[Green Lantern]] [[John_Stewart (comics)|John Stewart]]. Vixen's voice on the show is provided by [[Gina Torres]]. Vixen has become a very popular recurring character on the show.






== Powers and abilities ==
A supernatural talisman called the Tantu Totem grants her the ability to mimic the &ldquo;powers&rdquo; of animals, such as the strength of an elephant, or the speed of a cheetah.
