'''Moondragon''' is a [[fictional character]] in [[Marvel Comics]]' [[Marvel Universe|universe]].

She has been a member of the [[Infinity Watch]] (during which time she possessed the [[Mind Gem]]), the [[Defenders]], and the [[The Avengers (comics)|The Avengers]].

A powerful [[psychokinesis|telekinetic]], [[telepathy|telepath]], and [[martial arts|martial artist]], Moondragon's most notable characteristics are her shaven head and her overweening arrogance. She has recently been confirmed to be a [[lesbian]].

She is Heather Douglas, the daughter of Arthur Douglas, whose reanimated corpse was made into [[Drax the Destroyer]].
