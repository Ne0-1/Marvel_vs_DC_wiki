'''The Scarecrow''' (Ebenezer Laughton) is a Marvel Comics supervillain. He wears a scarecrow costume and is extremely flexible due to heavy training. He is very similar to the DC Comics character [[Scarecrow (comics)|Scarecrow]] in that he is also able to induce fear in his enemies, although he does so without the use of any kind of chemical. He is an [[undead]] character in that he was raised by the sorcerer [[Stern (comics)|Stern]]. Before this he possessed no superhuman powers save for his "rubber man" ability, but after his reserrection he gained superhuman strength, speed and endurance, as well as the supernatural ability to induce fear in his enemies.
