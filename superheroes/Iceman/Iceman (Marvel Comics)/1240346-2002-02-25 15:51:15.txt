A character in the [[Marvel Comics]] universe. Like the name implies, ice is his main power.  He can change his body into ice which makes him more resilient to damage, shoot ice from his hands in order to freeze enemies or create ice structures, and travel around at fast speeds by creating an ice slide and riding it.

He started off as one of the original members of [[X-Men film|The X-Men]], then joined [[X Factor]], and was even in the [[cartoon]] show [[Spiderman and His Amazing Friends]].
