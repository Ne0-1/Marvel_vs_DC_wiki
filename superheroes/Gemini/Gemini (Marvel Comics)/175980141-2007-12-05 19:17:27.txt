'''Gemini''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Universe]].

==Fictional character biography==
'''Joshua Link''' is a founding member of the [[Zodiac (comics)|Zodiac]], and his base of operations was [[Boston, Massachusetts]].

Due to an electrical accident, Link became psionically linked with his twin brother Damian, a policeman. Hence, Joshua Link can take mental control of Damian Link's mind and body, and can add Damian's physical strength to that of his own body.

==Other Media==
Gemini was among the Zodiacs that appear in ''[[The Avengers: United They Stand]]'' voiced by [[Julie Lemieux]]. Gemini is depicted as a two-headed alien (one male head, one female head) with four arms.
