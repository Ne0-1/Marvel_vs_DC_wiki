'''Capricorn''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Universe]].

==Fictional character biography==
'''Willard Weir''' is a founding member of the [[Zodiac (comics)|Zodiac]] criminal cartel, and his base of operations was [[Detroit, Michigan]].

Capricorn dresses in a goat-like costume. Through unknown means, Capricorn can cause his costume's horns to extend, wrap themselves around opponents, and throw the opponents about.

==Other Media==
Capricorn appears in ''[[The Avengers: United They Stand]]'' as a member of the Zodiacs. He is depicted as a goat-horned green-skinned alien that wields a flail in battle.
