She-Venom is a [[fictional character]] in [[Marvel comics]]. She is the wife of Venom.

==Character History==

Anne Weying first appeared in Amazing Spider-Man 375. She was Venom's ex-wife, who was a lawyer. She helped out Spider-Man by sharing some of Eddie's history with him. Later, she followed Spider-Man to the amusement park where Venom had Peter's (fake) parents and managed to convince Eddie to give up his vendetta. Later, Sin-Eater II shot Anne, who became She-Venom. In Amazing Spider-Man (vol 2.) 19, Anne Weying commited suicide.

==Powers and abilities==

She-Venom posseses all the powers the original Venom has, including suit generated webbing, shape-shifting, superstrength and the inability to be detected by [[Spider-Man]]'s "spider-sense".
