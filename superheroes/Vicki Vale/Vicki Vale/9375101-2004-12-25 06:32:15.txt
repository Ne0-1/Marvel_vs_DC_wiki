'''Vicki Vale''' is a [[fictional character]] in the [[DC Comics]] [[DC Universe|universe]], a reporter who was once a love interest of Bruce Wayne ([[Batman]]).  She first appeared in ''Batman'' #49 (1950), and became a recurring character in the series.  The character is best known for her prominent role in the [[1989]] film ''[[Batman (1989 film)|Batman]]'', in which she was portrayed by [[Kim Basinger]].
