'''Fahnbullah Eddy''', also known as '''Gorilla Girl''', is a [[fictional character|fictional]] [[comic book]] character within [[Marvel Comics]]' [[shared universe]] the [[Marvel Universe]]. She first appeared in ''[[Marvel Team-Up]]'' #91 (March 1980), and was created by [[Steven Grant]] and [[Pat Broderick]].

==Fictional character biography==
Fahnbullah Eddy worked at a carnival as a member of the '''Freaks''', a group of [[circus freaks]] that included [[Muck Monster]] and [[Six (comics)|Six]]. When she and the Freaks fell under the control of the sorcerer [[Moondark]], they were forced to battle [[Spider-Man]]. Gorilla Girl and the Freaks were later seen in a fight against the criminals [[Hammer and Anvil (comics)|Hammer and Anvil]].<ref>''Marvel Tales'' #256</ref>

After the events of the [[Civil War (comics)|Civil War]], Gorilla Girl was captured by the [[Thunderbolts (comics)|Thunderbolts]]. During her capture, she threw [[Robbie Baldwin|Penance]] down three flights of stairs, after which Penance beat her severely.<ref>''Thunderbolts: Desperate Measures''</ref>. She later [[Superhuman Registration Act|registered]] with the [[Avengers: The Initiative|Initiative]] and joined [[Camp Hammond]] along with [[Annex (comics)|Annex]], [[Prodigy (comics)#Prodigy (Ritchie Gilmore)|Prodigy]] and others.<ref>''Avengers: The Initiative'' #13</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
Fahnbullah Eddy is able to shapeshift into a super-strong, highly agile [[gorilla]].
