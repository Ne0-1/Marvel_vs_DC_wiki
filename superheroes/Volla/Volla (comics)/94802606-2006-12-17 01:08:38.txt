'''Volla''' is a [[fictional character]] appearing in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]]. Volla died after making known her prophecies of Ragnarok, but those who are brave and powerful enough to journey into the realm of the dead may still obtain prophecies from her. She first appeared in Thor (1st series) #127.

According to Thor #295, Erda is Volla's sister.

==Powers and abilities==
Volla had precognitive powers, which allow her to see alternate futures
