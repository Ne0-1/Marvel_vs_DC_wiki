'''Sleeper''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Universe]].

The Sleeper was the most powerful of four robots designed by Nazi Germany. It battled [[Captain America]], and later became a member of the [[Skeleton Crew (comics)|Skeleton Crew]].
