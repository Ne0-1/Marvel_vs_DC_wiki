'''Pixie''' is the name of several  [[fictional character]]s in [[comic books]] of the [[Marvel Universe]]: 
#[[Pixie (Morlock)|Pixie]] was a member of the [[Morlocks (comics)|Morlocks]], a group of outcast [[mutant (fictional)|Mutants]].
#[[Pixie (eternal)|Pixie]] was an [[Eternals (comics)|Eternal]] who was a member of the [[First Line (comics)|First Line]] in ''[[Marvel: The Lost Generation]]''. In addition to the usual Eternal powers, she also used "pixie dust" that turned people to stone.
#Pixie is the alias of '''Megan Gwynn''', a [[mutant (comics)|mutant]] who attends the [[Xavier Institute]].

==Character biography==

Megan Gwynn is a [[Welsh people|Welsh]] teenager who attends the Xavier Institute for mutants.  After enrolling at the Institute, Gwynn was assigned to the Paragons training squad under the tutelage of former [[New Mutants|New Mutant]], [[Wolfsbane (comics)|Rahne Sinclair]]. When it was revealed that Sinclair had been in a romantic relationship with a student, [[Elixir (comics)|Josh Foley]], she left the school, and was replaced by her former teammate, [[Magma (comics)|Magma]].

Gwynn as proven herself to be an amiable and cheerful student, and she fit in well with her squad.  Following the events of [[House of M]], almost all of the Institute's students were [[Decimation|depowered]], leading to the dissolution of the school's training squads. Gwynn is one of only 27 student, along with her fellow former Paragons, [[Trance (comics|Trance]], [[Wolf Cub (comics)|Wolf Cub]], and [[Match (comics)|Match]], to have retained her mutant abilities.

Recently, 42 of Gwynn's former classmates were killed when a bus carrying several depowered students was ambushed.  Among the confirmed deaths was Gywnn's fellow Paragons, DJ and Preview.

==Powers==

Gwynn possesses rainbow-colored butterfly wings that allow her to fly.  In addition, she produces a "pixie dust" that causes [[hallucination]]s.

==Trivia==

*In the ''New X-Men: Academy X: Yearbook Special'', Gywnn was voted Friendliest Student.

*Gwynn wears a bicycle helmet during training sessions.

*Gwynn has a crush on [[Cyclops (comics)|Cyclops]].
