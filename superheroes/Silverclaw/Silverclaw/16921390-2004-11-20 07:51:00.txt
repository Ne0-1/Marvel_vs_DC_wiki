'''Silverclaw''', real name Maria De Guadalupe "Lupe" Santiago, is a [[superhero]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]].  She is a reserve member of the [[Avengers (comics)|Avengers]] and is the fifty-first superhero to join the group.

Silverclaw has the power to change into various animal forms.  This power is a mystical quality that she inherited from her mother, Peliali, the volcano goddess of the Kamekeri.
