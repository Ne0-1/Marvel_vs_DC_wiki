'''Byrrah''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character. He is a prince of Atlantis, and cousin to [[Namor the Sub-Mariner]]. He first appeared in ''Marvel: Mystery Comics'' #82. 

==History==
They were close friends at childhood and possible heir to the throne until Namor's return. When Namor did return, Byrrah formed an alliance with Namor's enemies ([[Attuma]] and [[Warlord Krang|Krang]]) to defeat him, but failed. 

==In other media==
Byrrah appears as a villain in the RPG video game ''[[Marvel: Ultimate Alliance]]''.
