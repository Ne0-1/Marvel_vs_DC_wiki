'''Bloodstrike''' ('''Eric Conroy''') is a [[Mutate (comics)|mutate]] [[supervillain]] appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]].

==Fictional character biography==
Eric Conroy's father Lt. Mark Conroy served in the Vietnam war. Conroy's unit, calling themselves the ''Half-Fulls'' discovers an ancient temple deep in the jungles of [[Cambodia]]. There they met [[Tai (comics)|Tai]], a powerful mystic, who convinces most of them to marry the six daughters of the ''Dragon's Breadth'' cult. The soldiers return home with their new brides. Mark and his wife had Eric soon after.<ref>''New Warriors'' #24 (1992)</ref>

Eric was once the enforcer for a mobster in [[Chicago]] until the [[Left Hand (comics)|Left Hand]] kills his boss and recruits him to join the [[Folding Circle]].<ref>''New Warriors'' vol. 1 #21 (1992)</ref> The Left Hand is Diego Casseas, one of the members of Conroy's unit, who had stolen the mystical power inherent in his own child. He is now recruiting the Dragon's Breadth children in order to take control of the ''Well of All Things''. This is an ancient fountain of power that exists deep inside the Cambodian temple. In one of his first missions Eric kills a security guard. This action transforms his body; unbreakable pink material wraps around his arms, legs and waist.<ref>''New Warriors'' #22</ref>

The Folding Circle arrives at the temple, along with the [[New Warriors]]. In the resulting battle, it is learned that Tai wants to sacrifice everyone but herself so she can gain the Well's powers. The teams work together in effort to save their own lives. Ultimately, Tai is seemingly slain by [[Night Thrasher]]. The Folding Circle escapes, stealing the [[List of vehicles in Marvel Comics#Quinjet|Quinjet]] the New Warriors had originally stolen from the [[Avengers (comics)|Avengers]]. The Folding Circle crashes in [[Madripoor]] and try to become players in the Madripoor underworld. The Circle takes over the organization of [[Aardwolf (comics)|Aardwolf]], a mutant drug lord.<ref>''Night Thrasher'' vol 2 #2</ref> Later, Night Thrasher and [[Silhouette (comics)|Silhouette]] defeat the entire Circle.<ref>''Night Thrasher'' vol 2 #4</ref>

===Civil War/The Initiative===
Eric has been identified as one of the 142 registered superheroes who appear on the cover of the comic book ''[[Avengers: The Initiative]]'' #1.<ref>[https://archive.is/20120526061017/http://www.marvel.com/news/comicstories.947 ''Avengers: The Initiative'' #1 Character Map]</ref>

===Universal Wellspring===
Bloodstrike is a [[mutate (comics)|mutate]] whose powers come from the Universal Wellspring.<ref>''[[Thunderbolts (comics)|Thunderbolts]]'' #107</ref>
