The '''Trapster''', formerly known as ''Paste-Pot Pete'', is a [[supervillain]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]].

The Trapster was a longtime member of the [[Frightful Four]], alongside such other villains as the [[Wizard (Marvel Comics)|Wizard]], the [[The Sandman (Marvel Comics)|Sandman]], and [[Medusa (comics)|Medusa]]. Calling him by his former name (Paste-Pot Pete) sends him into a rage. The Trapster does not possess any superhuman powers, but relies on a variety of technological devices. His primary weapon is a gun that shoots a powerful glue.
