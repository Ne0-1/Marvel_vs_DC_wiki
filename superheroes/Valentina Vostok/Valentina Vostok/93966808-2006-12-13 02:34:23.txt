'''Valentina Vostok''' (alternately spelled Vostock) is a [[fictional character]] by [[DC Comics]]. She first appeared in ''[[Showcase (comics)|Showcase]]'' #94, (August 1977), and was created by [[Paul Kupperberg]] and [[Jim Aparo]].

==Fiictional character biography==
Lt. Col. Valentina Vostok of the [[Soviet Air Force]] chose to defect to the [[USA]], stealing an experimental Russian fighter plan in order to accomplish this. The plane malfunctioned en route and she crash-landed at the spot where the original [[Doom Patrol]] had apparently perished and was infused with the energy being which formerly inhabited the body of the Patrol's [[Negative Man]]. 

Inevitably, she became '''Negative Woman''' and was recruited for the second Doom Patrol for several years. While on the team she had a brief affair will her teammate [[Joshua Clay]]. Later, the energy being eventually quit her body and returned to Larry Trainor (Negative Man), whereupon she began to work for the American secret services, being involved in a number of US government departments until she ended up in [[Checkmate (comics)|Checkmate]]. Her current whereabouts are unknown.

==Powers and abilities==
*Valentina formerly possessed a radioactive 'soul-self', and was capable of flight, intangibility and could generate minor explosions upon contact with positive energy. She no longer possesses these abilities.
