'''Jimmy Hudson''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[superhero]] in the [[Ultimate Marvel]] universe. He first appeared in the ''[[Ultimatum (Ultimate Marvel)|Ultimatum]]''s follow up ''[[Ultimate Comics: X]]'' by [[Cullen Bunn]] and art from [[David Messina]] and [[Art Adams]].<ref>{{cite web|last1=Ching|first1=Albert|title=Bunn Writes Two Generations of Ultimate Comics Wolverine|url=http://www.newsarama.com/10672-bunn-writes-two-generations-of-ultimate-comics-wolverine.html|website=Newsarama|accessdate=5 August 2014}}</ref> He is the son of [[Ultimate Wolverine]]. <ref>''Ultimate Comics: X'' #1</ref>

==Fictional character biography==
Jimmy Hudson is the son of Wolverine/James Howlett and Magda. Wolverine fought alongside [[Guardian (Marvel Comics)|James Hudson]] in the [[Iraq war]] and entrusted James with his newborn son. James later becomes a [[sheriff]] in [[Florida]] and with his wife [[Vindicator_(comics)|Heather]] raised Jimmy in [[Port St Lucie, Florida]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Jimmy Hudson (Character)|url=http://www.comicvine.com/jimmy-hudson/4005-69059/|website=Comic Vine|publisher=Gamespot|accessdate=5 August 2014}}</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
Like Wolverine, Jimmy possesses a regenerative healing factor, the signature three claws, and is able to coat them in an [[organic metal]].
