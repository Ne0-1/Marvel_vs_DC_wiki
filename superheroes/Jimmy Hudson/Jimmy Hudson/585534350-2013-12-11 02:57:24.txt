'''Jimmy Hudson''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[superhero]] in the [[Ultimate Marvel]] universe. He first appeared in the ''[[Ultimatum (Ultimate Marvel)|Ultimatum]]''s follow up ''[[Ultimate Comics: X]]'' by [[Jeph Loeb]] and [[Art Adams]]. He is the son of [[Ultimate Wolverine]]. <ref>''Ultimate Comics: X'' #1</ref>

==Fictional character biography==
Jimmy Hudson is the son of Wolverine/James Howlett and Magda. Wolverine fought alongside [[Guardian (Marvel Comics)|James Hudson]] in the [[Iraq war]] and entrusted James with his newborn son. James later becomes a [[sheriff]] in [[Florida]] and his wife [[Heather Hudson (Exiles)|Heather]] raise Jimmy.

==Powers and abilities==
Like Wolverine, Jimmy possesses a regenerative healing factor, the signature three claws, and is able to coat them in an [[organic metal]].
