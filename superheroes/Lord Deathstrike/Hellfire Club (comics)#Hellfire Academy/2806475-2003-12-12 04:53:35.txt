In the [[Marvel Universe|universe]] of [[Marvel Comics]], '''The Hellfire Club''' is a [[New York City]] [[club]], counting among its members many famous and influential members of society, that serves as a cover for a [[conspiracy]] opposed by the [[X-Men]].
In this fictional canon, the secret Inner Circle of the Hellfire Club is a group of evil [[mutant]]s that has been around for at least two hundred years.

During [[Chris Claremont]]'s first run as X-Men writer, agents of the Hellfire Club kidnapped the X-Men and took over the mind of [[Jean Grey|Phoenix]], to be rescued by [[Wolverine (comics)|Wolverine]], [[Cyclops (comics)|Cyclops]], and Phoenix herself. This meddling with Phoenix's mind eventually led her to become Dark Phoenix.

Notable members of the Inner Circle include [[Sebastian Shaw (comics)|Sebastian Shaw]] (aka the Black King), [[Emma Frost]] (aka the White Queen), [[Selene (comics)|Selene]], [[Tessa]] (both known as the Black Queen at one point), [[Donald Pierce]] (aka the White King), Jason Wyngarde (aka [[Mastermind (comics)|Mastermind]]), [[Harry Leland]], and [[Trevor Fitzroy]].
It has appeared in numerous incarnations throughout the X-Men titles and other Marvel series.
