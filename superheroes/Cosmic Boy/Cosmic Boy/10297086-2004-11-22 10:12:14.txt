'''Cosmic Boy''' (Rokk Krinn of the [[planet]] [[Braal]]) is a [[comic book]] [[superhero]] in the [[DC Comics]] [[DC Universe|universe]].  He has the [[superhuman]] power of generating and controlling [[magnetism]].  He is a member of the [[Legion of Super-Heroes]] in the [[future]].  He first appeared in ''[[Adventure Comics]]'' #247 ([[April]] [[1958]]).

He is a founding member of the Legion of Super-Heroes, along with [[Lightning Lad]] and [[Saturn Girl]].
