The '''Human Top''' ('''David Mitchell''') is a fictional character appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]].

==Publication history==
The Human Top first appeared in ''[[Invaders (comics)|Invaders]]'' #27-28 (April–May 1978) and was created by [[Roy Thomas]] and [[Frank Robbins]].

The character subsequently appears in ''The Invaders'' #38-39 (March–April 1979), #41 (September 1979), ''The Invaders'' #1 (May 1993), ''Citizen V and the V-Battalion'' #2 (July 2001), and ''Citizen V and the V-Battalion: The Everlasting'' #1 (March 2002), and #3 (June 2002).

==Fictional character biography==
David "Davy" Mitchell was a World War II hero, and member of [[Kid Commandos]].  He had the power to spin like a top at super-speed.

During [[World War II]], teenagers David "Davy" Mitchell and Gwenny Lou Sabuki, were present at a stateside battle in which sidekicks [[Bucky]] (Bucky Barnes) and [[Toro (comics)|Toro]] (Thomas Raymond) of the superhero team the [[Invaders (comics)|Invaders]] fought the supervillain [[Agent Axis#Marvel Comics|Agent Axis]]. During the battle, one of Gwenny's father's inventions accidentally gave Gwenny and Davy superhuman powers. She became [[Golden Girl#Golden Girl (Gwenny Lou Sabuki)|Golden Girl]], and he the Human Top.<ref>''Invaders'' #28</ref> The four youthful heroes defeated Agent Axis, and later formed the Kid Commandos, who were allied with the adult Invaders.{{issue|date=April 2013}}

After the war, he was a member of the Penance Council and [[V-Battalion]].{{issue|date=April 2013}}

His son, as Twister, had tried using mechanical means to simulate the powers of the Human Top, but eventually decided to serve the V-Battalion in a scientific capacity.{{issue|date=April 2013}}  Davy is also the grandfather of [[Topspin (comics)|Topspin]].{{issue|date=April 2013}}

In his later years as seen during the "Last Days" part of the ''[[Secret Wars (2015 comic book)|Secret Wars]]'' storyline, Davy moved into Valhalla Villas, a retirement community for [[Golden Age of Comic Books|Golden Age]] heroes and villains that is located in Miami. He and the rest of the retirees were temporarily de-aged and went back into action one last time before the collision between Earth-616 and Earth-1610.<ref>''Ant-Man: Last Days'' #1</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
After being exposed to a scientific invention, the Human Top can spin like a top at superhuman speeds.
