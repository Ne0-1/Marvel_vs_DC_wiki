'''Pip the Troll''' is a [[fictional character]] who is published by [[Marvel Comics]].  He is Prince Gofern, an alien of the Laxadazian race who was mutated into a stunted, troll-like form during a bout of drunkenness brought on by a [[mutagen]]ic [[hallucinogen]].  He is a frequent companion of [[Adam Warlock]], and was a member of the [[Infinity Watch]], which protected the [[Infinity Gems]]; he guarded the [[Space Gem]].
