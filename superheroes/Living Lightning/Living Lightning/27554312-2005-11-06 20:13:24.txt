'''Living Lightning''' is a [[fictional character]] from the [[Marvel Universe]]. He is an inactive member of the [[Avengers (comics)|Avengers]].

==Powers==
In his true form, Living Lightning is just that, a sentient electrical force with no mass.  In this form, he can fly, reaching sub-light speeds, withstanding the vacuum of space, and is impervious to most physical and energy attacks.  He also posesses the ability to fire bursts of electricity and electrical fields of varying intensities in his solid form, however, he must wear a special containment suit to retain a solid form.
