'''Superman & Batman: Generations''' is an [[Elseworlds]] [[comic book]] series written and illustrated by [[John Byrne]].

It deals with the question of 'What would happen if people in comic books actually aged?'.

The first Generations storyline kept skiping forward 10 years to the next part of the story. The following two series Generations II and Generations III fitted into the same continuity and told series fitting in the intervening years.
