'''Robert "Rebel" Ralston''' is a [[fictional character]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] Universe. His first appearance was in ''Sgt. Fury and the Howling Commandos vol. 1 #1''.

==Fictional character biography==
'''Rebel Ralston''' is a native of [[Kentucky]] and a founding member of the original [[Howling Commandos]] and fought alongside the team during [[World War II]].  Ralston was an accomplished [[horseman]] and was affluent with a [[lasso]].
