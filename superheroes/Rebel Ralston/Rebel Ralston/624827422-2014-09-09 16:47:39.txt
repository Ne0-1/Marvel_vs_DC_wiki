'''Robert "Rebel" Ralston''' is a [[fictional character]] in [[Marvel Comics]]. His first appearance was in ''[[Sgt. Fury and his Howling Commandos]]'' #1 (May 1963).

==Fictional character biography==
'''Robert "Rebel" Ralston''' is a native of [[Kentucky]] and a founding member of the original [[Sgt. Fury and his Howling Commandos|Howling Commandos]] led by [[Nick Fury]]. Rebel Ralston fought alongside the team during [[World War II]].{{Issue|date=January 2010}}  Ralston was an accomplished [[Equestrianism|horseman]] and was skilled with a [[lasso]]. He was also the "creator" of the Howlers battle cry "Wahoo!"{{issue|date=April 2013}} Mr. and Mrs. Ralston were seen.<ref>''Sgt. Fury and his Howling Commandos'' #24</ref>

After the war, he was one of the founding members of the [[V-Battalion]] and the [[Penance Council]].{{Issue|date=January 2010}} He was later elected Senator of [[Texas]], apparently with clandestine help from the V-Battalion.<ref>''Citizen V and the V Battalion #2''</ref> He rejoined his old comrades in the Howling Commandos to battle a Baron Strucker robot. The Commandos and Captain America stopped the Strucker robot but the Howling Commandos' superior, [[Happy Sam Sawyer|Captain Sam Sawyer]] was killed.{{issue|date=April 2013}} He survived an assassination attempt while co-chairing the Senate Defense Committee.{{issue|date=April 2013}} He has worked closely with S.H.I.E.L.D. and the Commission on Superhuman Activities.<ref>''The Official Handbook of the Marvel Universe A-Z'' Vol. 9</ref>

When Nick Fury was seemingly killed by [[Punisher]], Robert Ralston and [[Iron Man]] advised the President not to attend the funeral in the event that the supervillains attack it.<ref>''Incredible Hulk'' #434</ref>

In the form of Lt. Dallas, Omnibus placed Senator Robert Ralston under mental control to turn over information which he can use to fund his terrorist organization called "The Alliance."<ref>''Incredible Hulk'' #436</ref> When [[Hulk (comics)|Hulk]] was captured by Major [[Glenn Talbot]], Senator Robert Ralston observed Bruce Banner in custody and refused to have Glenn Talbot kill Bruce Banner until the President has made a decision. Senator Robert Ralston attended a briefing on "The Alliance" with Glenn Talbot, [[Dum Dum Dugan]], [[Valentina Allegra de Fontaine]], and [[Henry Peter Gyrich]].<ref>''Incredible Hulk'' #437</ref> Senator Robert Ralston and his allies continued to debate about "The Alliance." They could not determine who "The Alliance" were and what their motive is.<ref>''Incredible Hulk'' #439</ref> After Hulk had claimed responsibility for "The Alliance" in order to serve as an enemy that the people can fight back against, [[Thor (Marvel Comics)|Thor]] confronted Senator Robert Ralston and the others stating that he will confront Hulk.<ref>''Incredible Hulk'' #440</ref>

Senator Robert Ralston was contacted by [[J. Jonah Jameson]] as he searched for support against [[Bastion (comics)|Bastion]] and Operation: Zero Tolerance. Senator Robert Ralston suggested to J. Jonah Jameson to try Senator [[Robert Kelly (comics)|Robert Kelly]] (who was more frustrated with the mutant issue).<ref>''Uncanny X-Men'' #346</ref>

Senator Robert Ralston traveled to Sudan where he met many of his former Howling Commandos teammates (consisting of Nick Fury, Captain America, [[Sharon Carter]], and John Garrett) at Fury's Sandbox for one last party together. Nick Fury talked with Senator Robert Ralston for a moment about the support for Nick Fury's operation that would get the U.S. Senate and Senator Robert Ralston only asked him how soon Nick Fury will need those votes.<ref>''Secret Warriors'' #17</ref> Senator Robert Ralston listed to a good few stories from the past.<ref>''Secret Warriors'' #18</ref> Senator Robert Ralston raised his glass in a toast to the fallen members of the Howling Commanders<ref>''Secret Warriors'' #19</ref>

Senator Robert Ralston spoke to the [[United Nations]] about the President's plans for international funds to provide economic, political, and military stabilities in areas suffering from international terrorist threats. Senator Robert Ralston was appointed control over the fund.<ref>''Secret Warriors'' #27</ref>

Senator Robert Ralston picked up [[Jasper Sitwell]] and Dum Dum Dugan from the United Nations' prison and brought them to his limousine waiting for them with Nick Fury and John Garrett already inside.<ref>''Secret Warriors'' #28</ref>

As part of the ''[[Marvel NOW!]]'' event, Senator Robert Ralston attended a weapons expo where he met with [[Maria Hill]] and [[Nick Fury, Jr.]] as they noticed the [[Iron Patriot]] armor on display as one of the items at the convention. A fight broke out against [[Advanced Idea Mechanics|A.I.M.]], and Ralston was apparently killed during the fight.<ref>''Secret Avengers'' vol. 2 #3</ref>
