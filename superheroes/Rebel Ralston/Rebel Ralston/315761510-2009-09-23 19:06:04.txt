'''Robert "Rebel" Ralston''' is a [[fictional character]] in [[Marvel Comics]]' [[shared universe]], the [[Marvel Universe]]. His first appearance was in ''[[Sgt. Fury and his Howling Commandos]]'' vol. 1 #1 (May 1963).

==Fictional character biography==
'''Rebel Ralston''' is a native of [[Kentucky]] and a founding member of the original [[Sgt. Fury and his Howling Commandos|Howling Commandos]] and fought alongside the team during [[World War II]].  Ralston was an accomplished [[horseman]] and was affluent with a [[lasso]]. In more recent times he was elected Senator of [[Texas]].  In Sgt. Fury and his Howling Commandos #24 Mr. and Mrs. Ralston were seen.
