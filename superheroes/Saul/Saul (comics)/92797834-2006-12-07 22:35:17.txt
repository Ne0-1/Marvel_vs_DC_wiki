'''Saul''' (real name '''Garbha-hsien''') is a fictional [[Marvel Comics]] character. His first appearance was in ''X-Force (1st series) #10.'' He is both a [[mutant (comics)|mutant]] and one of the [[Externals]].

Saul encountered [[Apocalypse (comics)|Apocalypse]] during the 12th Century AD, and was one of a group of Externals that called themselves the High Lords and met regularly. Saul was trying to gain entry into a crashed Celestial vessel in the Mongolian mountains, when he was bested by Apocalypse. Recently he was killed by [[Selene (comics)|Selene]], when she drained the life force from several Externals. Saul's exact age is unknown, but he has said he was older than Apocalypse in age.

==Powers==

Channel biological plasma energy into explosive force discharges and feats of anti-gravity levitation, teleportation and immortality.
