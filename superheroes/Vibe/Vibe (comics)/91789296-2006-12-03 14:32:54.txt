'''Vibe''' was a [[superhero]] in the [[DC Comics]] [[DC Universe|universe]]. He first appeared in ''Justice League of America Annual'' #2 (November 1984).

==Character history==

Born '''Paco Ramone''', he was a former [[Puerto Rican]] gang leader (A member of the Detroit gang "Los Lobos") who had the power to produce sound waves. He was introduced as a new member of the [[Justice League]] during the "[[Justice League#Detroit|Justice League Detroit]]" era. The character was considered to be a [[stereotype]] and, like most of the new Detroit League characters, was massively unpopular. He was soon killed off by [[Professor Ivo]].

He has a brother named Armando who has the same powers, Armando has gone by the codename '''Reverb''' and is currently known as '''Hardline'''. Armando was and might possibly still be a member of the corporate hero team known as the [[The Conglomerate (comics)|Conglomerate]]. 

==Other Media==

Vibe has made several appearances in the show ''[[Justice League Unlimited]].'' He was often seen next to other members of the Detroit League but he did not get a major role in any episode. He did have a major role in an issue of the ''Justice League Unlimited'' comic book series where he stopped [[Doctor Sivana]]'s plan to rebuild [[Mister Atom]].
