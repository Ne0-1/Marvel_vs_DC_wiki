'''Optoman''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[Mutant (Marvel comics)|mutant]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] Universe. His first appearance was in ''X-Statix #1''.

==Fictional character biography==
Optoman is a member the mutant superhero team, [[O-Force]]. Like all potential recruits, Optoman had to compete in a live [[television program]], hosted by [[Solomon O'Sullivan]], to win a place on the O-Force team. It's unknown if Optoman lost or kept his mutant powers after the [[M-Day]].

==Powers and abilities==
Optoman could generate what appeared to be flashes of light from his eyes that can stun people.
