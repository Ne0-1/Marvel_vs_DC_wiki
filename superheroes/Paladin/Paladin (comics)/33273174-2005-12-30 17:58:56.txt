'''Paladin''' (real name Paul Dennis) is a [[Marvel Comics]] character, a [[mercenary]]. Though not precisely a [[supervillain]], his mercenary activities and willingness to kill often bring him into conflict with [[superhero]]es. He first appeared in ''[[Daredevil]]'' v1 #150 (January [[1978]]).
