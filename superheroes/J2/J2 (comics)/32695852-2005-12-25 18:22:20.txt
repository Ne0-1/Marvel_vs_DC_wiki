[[image:J2x.png|Cover to ''J2'' #1. Art by Ron Frenz.|thumb]]'''J2''' (real name '''Zane Yama''') is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Universe]]. He comes from the alternate future '''[[MC2]]''', and is the son of the '''[[Juggernaut (comics)|Juggernaut]].''' He is a member of the future incarnation of the [[Avengers (comics)|Avengers]].

J2 once had his own series, which lasted for twelve issues, and has made a few appearances in ''[[Spider-Girl]]'' (also set in the MC2 universe, starring the daughter of [[Spider-Man]]).
