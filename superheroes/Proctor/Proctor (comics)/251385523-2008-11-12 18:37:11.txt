'''Proctor''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Universe]].

==Fictional character biography==
Proctor was an enemy of the [[Avengers (comics)|Avengers]], who apparently hailed from an alternate Earth. He was allegedly the Gann Josin (lifemate) of the [[Sersi]] from his world, who spurned him. Proctor created and led the Gatherers, to seek vengeance by killing all the Sersies across the multiverse.

Proctor apparently killed Marissa Darrow of the Avengers' Earth.<ref>''Avengers'' #348</ref> He sent the Gatherers to capture the [[Coal Tiger#Other Coal Tigers|Coal Tiger]] of an alternate Earth and to kill the [[Black Panther (comics)|Black Panther]] of the Avengers' Earth.<ref>''Avengers'' #355</ref> Proctor made plans to win back [[Magdalene (comics)|Magdalene]]'s love.<ref>''Avengers'' #356</ref> Proctor sent the Gatherers to find the [[Vision (Marvel Comics)|Vision]] of an alternate Earth.<ref>''Avengers'' #358</ref> Proctor held the Vision of the Avengers' Earth prisoner, and transferred the minds between that Vision and the alternate Earth Vision of the Gatherers. Proctor then took the Gatherers Vision to New York to infiltrate the Avengers.<ref>''Avengers'' #360</ref> Proctor then fought the Avengers' Vision.<ref>''Avengers'' #361</ref> Proctor destroyed the Gatherers' Vision, and battled the Avengers in his citadel. Proctor was confronted by [[Uatu]] the [[Watcher (comics)|Watcher]]. Proctor was attacked by Magdalene when she learned that he had ordered the murder attempt on [[Swordsman (comics)#Philip Javert|Swordsman]]. Proctor was impaled by the [[Black Knight (Dane Whitman)|Black Knight]]'s [[Ebony Blade|sword]], but escaped.<ref>''Avengers'' #363</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
Proctor possesses superhuman strength, speed, stamina, durability, agility, and reflexes. He has the ability to project destructive energy beams from his eyes. He also has telepathy, and the psionic ability to manipulate the chemical composition of the human brain.

Proctor has access to highly advanced technology of unknown origin. He wears armor and fabrics of unknown composition, and has access to teleportational and interdimensional travel technology.

Proctor is a formidable hand-to-hand combatant, and he is expert in the use of highly advanced technology of unknown origin.
