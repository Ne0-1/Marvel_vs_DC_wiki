'''Doctor Minerva''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Universe]].

Doctor Minerva is a [[Kree]] genetecist.  She was an enemy of the rogue Kree agent [[Mar-Vell|Captain Mar-Vell]], and later became the partner of [[Captain Atlas]].
