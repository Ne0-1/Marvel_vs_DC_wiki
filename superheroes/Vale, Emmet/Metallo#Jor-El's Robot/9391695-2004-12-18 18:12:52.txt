'''Metallo''' is a [[DC_Comics|DC Comics]] [[supervillain]] and enemy of [[Superman]].  John Corban, dying of a mysterious disease, became Metallo during a process that put his [[brain]] in a [[metallic|metal]] body, becoming a [[cyborg]].  Metallo is one of Supermans' most deadly enemies due to the Kryptonite energy unit concealed in his chest.
