[[Image:Winddancer_(comics).jpg|right|frame|Winddancer, by Randy Green]]'''Winddancer''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[mutant (fictional)|mutant]] in the [[Marvel Universe]], one of the student body in the [[X-Men|Xavier Institute]], and a member of the [[New Mutants]] squad therein.  Her real name is Sofia Mantega, and she first appeared in ''New Mutants'' (volume 2) #1.

==Powers==

Winddancer has the ability to control the movement of air, which includes the ability to generate wind, fly and, more subtly, amplify small vibrations in the air - allowing her to hear faraway conversations.
