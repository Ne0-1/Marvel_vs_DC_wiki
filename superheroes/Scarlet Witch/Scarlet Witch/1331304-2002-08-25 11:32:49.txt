'''The Scarlet Witch''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]]. Created by [[Stan Lee]] and [[Jack Kirby]] she first appeared in the pages of [[X-Men]] #4 (1962).
She is a [[mutant]] with the power to alter probabilities.  She is the twin sister of the speedster [[Quicksilver]] and, unknowingly, [[Magneto]]'s daughter.
She is also a member of Marvel's premier team [[The Avengers]] and married to the [[android]] Vision.
