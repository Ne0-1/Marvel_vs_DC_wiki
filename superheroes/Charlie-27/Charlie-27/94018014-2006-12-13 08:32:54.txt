'''Captain Charlie-27''' is fictional character, a soldier in the [[Earth-691]] timeline of the fictional [[Marvel Universe]], genetically engineered to live in [[Jupiter]]'s gravity.  As such, he has eleven times the muscle mass of a normal human.  He is a founding member of the [[Guardians of the Galaxy]].

[[Yellowjacket (Rita DeMara)|Rita Demara]] once earned his friendship by performing an impromptu surgery with her stings on a massive internal blood-clot, saving his life.
