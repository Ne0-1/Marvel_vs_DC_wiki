'''Captain Charlie-27''' is fictional character in the [[Earth-691]] timeline of the [[Marvel Universe]].  He first appeared in ''Marvel Super-Heroes'' #18 (January 1969).

==Fictional character biography==
Charlie-27 is a soldier and space militia pilot, genetically engineered to live in [[Jupiter]]'s gravity, born in Galileo City, Jupiter.  As such, he has eleven times the muscle mass of a normal human.  He was also a Captain in the United Lands of Earth Space Militia.

In 3007 A.D., the alien [[Badoon]] invade the solar system, performing various acts of genocide. Charlie is the only survivor of his world due to being on a long-term space mission. He joins forces with [[Martinex]], [[Yondu]], and [[Vance Astro]] to form the [[Guardians of the Galaxy]], a band of freedom fighters.<ref>''Marvel Super-Heroes'' Vol. 1 #18</ref> In 3014 A.D., he teamed with the time-traveling [[Thing (comics)|Thing]], [[Captain America]], and [[Sharon Carter]] to retake New York City from Badoon forces.<ref>''Marvel Two-in-One'' #5</ref> He later time-traveled to the 20th Century, and met the [[Defenders (comics)|Defenders]]. He returned to 3015 A.D. with [[Starhawk (comics)|Starhawk]] and the Defenders to defeat a Badoon invasion force.<ref>''Giant-Size Defenders #; ''Defenders'' #26-29</ref> 

Charlie-27 later departed post-war Earth on a space mission and met [[Nikki (comics)|Nikki]], who joined the Guardians of the Galaxy. He then visited the Asylum planet.<ref>''Marvel Presents'' #3-5</ref> Charlie helped defeat the [[Topographical Man]].<ref>''Marvel Presents'' #6-7</ref> Charlie then learned how the [[Silver Surfer]] repulsed the original Badoon invasion attempt of Earth in the 20th Century.<ref>''Marvel Presents'' #8</ref> He then met [[Aleta Ogord|Aleta]] and learned the origin of Starhawk. He battled the Reavers of Arcturus.<ref>''Marvel Presents'' #9-11</ref> Charlie then discovered the space station ''Drydock'', which the Guardians used as a base of operations for a time.<ref>''Marvel Presents'' #12</ref>

Charlie later teamed with the time-traveling [[Thor (Marvel Comics)|Thor]] to battle [[Korvac]] and his minions.<ref>''Thor Annual'' #6</ref> With the Guardians of the Galaxy, Charlie-27 traveled to the present, in pursuit of Korvac. He met the [[Avengers (comics)|Avengers]], and saved the life of a young [[Vance Astrovik]]. Charlie assisted the Avengers in battling Korvac. He attempted an Avengers membership meeting, but wound up parting company with the Avengers.<ref>''Avengers'' #167-168, 170, 177, 181</ref> Charlie attempted to prevent the meeting of Vance Astro's 20th and 31st Century selves, and battled the Thing to do so. With the Guardians, he then returned to the 31st Century.<ref>''Marvel Two-in-One'' #69</ref>

Charlie and the Guardians then went on a quest to find the lost [[Captain America's shield|shield]] of [[Captain America]]. He battled [[Taserface]] and the [[Stark (species)|Stark]]. He encountered [[Firelord]] and defeated the Stark.<ref>''Guardians of the Galaxy'' #1-4</ref> He battled the superhuman team known as [[Force (Guardians of the Galaxy)|Force]].<ref>''Guardians of the Galaxy'' #5-6</ref> He encountered [[Malevolence (comics)|Malevolence]].<ref>''Guardians of the Galaxy'' #7</ref> He located Haven, a lost colony of Earth founded by [[Mutant (Marvel Comics)|mutants]].  He battled [[Rancor (comics)|Rancor]] and her lieutenants.<ref>''Guardians of the Galaxy'' #8-11</ref> 

Charlie once had a romantic relationship with fellow Guardian Nikki, but the two broke it off.  They still remain friends. 

Though from the future, Charlie-27 manages to become involved in the [[Infinity War]]. He and his team save Avengers Mansion from an invasion by the [[Masters of Evil]]. Then both teams save each other from waves of evil doubles based on both teams. 

[[Yellowjacket (Rita DeMara)|Rita DeMara]] once earned his friendship by performing an impromptu surgery with her stings on a massive internal blood-clot, saving his life.

As with the rest of the team, Charlie's last appearance is on a mysterious planet as the team had become lost in space and time.

==Powers and abilities==
Charlie-27 is a member of a genetically engineered offshoot of humanity whose traits were designed for survival under the heavy gravity conditions of the planet Jupiter.  As such, he has superhuman strength, and enhanced durability and stamina.

Charlie is trained in hand-to-hand combat as a militiaman. He also has the ability to pilot most air and spacecraft of the 31st Century, including the Guardians' starships such as the ''Freedom's Lady'', ''Captain America I'', and ''Captain America II''.
