'''Ozone''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[mutant (fictional)|mutant]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] Universe. His first appearance was in ''X-Statix #1''.

Ozone is a member of America's newest mutant celebrity/superhero team, [[O-Force]]. Like all potential recruits, Ozone had to compete in a live [[television program]], hosted by [[Solomon O'Sullivan]], to win a place on the O-Force team. It's unknown if Ozone lost or kept her mutant powers after the [[Decimation (comics)|M-Day]].

==Powers==
He is apparently made of sludge-like pollution and could travel through solid objects.
