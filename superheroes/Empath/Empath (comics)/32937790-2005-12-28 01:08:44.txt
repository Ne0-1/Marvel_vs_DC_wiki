'''Empath''' (real name: '''Manuel Alfonzo Rodrigo de la Rocha''') is a [[fictional character]], a [[mutant (fiction)|mutant]] in the [[Marvel Universe]]. He was created by [[Chris Claremont]] and [[Sal Buscema]] in ''New Mutants'' vol. 1 #16 (1984) as a member of the original [[Hellions]]. He eventually moved on and joined the [[X-Corporation]]. Empath's mutant power is the power of [[empathy]], the ability to sense and manipulate a person's [[emotions]].
