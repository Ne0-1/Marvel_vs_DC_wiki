'''Turner D. Century''' is a [[comic book]] [[supervillain]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] universe.  He first appeared in ''[[Spider-Woman]]'' #33.  His final appearance was in ''[[Captain America]]'' #319, where he was killed by the mysterious [[Scourge of the Underworld]].
