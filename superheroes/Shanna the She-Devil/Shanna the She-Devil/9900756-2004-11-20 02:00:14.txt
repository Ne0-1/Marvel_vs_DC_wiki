Shanna the She-Devil is a [[Marvel Comics]] character resembaling [[Jane]], just as [[Ka-Zar]] resambels [[Tarzan]]. Shanna O'Hara was a New York City zoologist who became disillusioned with mordern life. Shanna moved to the [[Savage Land]] and met Ka-Zar. They fell in love and married him. They had a son, whom they named Matthew.
