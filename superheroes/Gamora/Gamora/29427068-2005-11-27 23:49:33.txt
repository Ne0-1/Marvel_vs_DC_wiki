'''Gamora''' is a [[fictional character]] who is published by [[Marvel Comics]].  She is the adopted daughter of [[Thanos]], and the occasional love interest of [[Adam Warlock]]. Her skin is pale green; she is the last of her species, who were eterminated by the Brotherhood of Badoon.

Gamora was a member of the [[Infinity Watch]] which protects the [[Infinity Gems]]; she held the [[Time Gem]]. She has amazing fighting abilities and a healing factor rivaling those of Wolverine.
