The '''Thunderer''' is a fictional character, a superhero which appeared in comic books published by Timely Comics. He first appeared in ''Daring Mystery Comics'' #7 (Timely, April 1941).

==Fictional character biography==
Frustrated that the United States didn't seem to be dealing with crime or Nazi saboteurs, radio operator Jerry Carstairs created a costume with a built in microphone and went out to fight for justice as the Thunderer.<ref>''Daring Mystery Comics'' #7-8</ref>

The Thunderer briefly changed his name (but not his costume) to the Black Avenger.<ref>''All-Winners Comics'' #6</ref>

Alongside a number of other heroes, the Thunderer patricipated in an air drop on a Nazi stronghold.<ref>''Marvels'' #1</ref>

The Thunderer attended a reunion of Second World War costumed heroes.<ref>''Captain America'' #442</ref>

==Other media==
===Television===
* The Thunderer appeared in the five-part ''[[Spider-Man (1994 TV series)|Spider-Man]]'' episode "[[Six Forgotten Warriors]]" voiced by [[Hansford Rowe]].{{cn|date=August 2011}} In this version, the Thunderer's powers are the result of an attempt at recreating the process that empowered Captain America.
