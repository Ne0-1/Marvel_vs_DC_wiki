The '''Khunds''' are a [[fictional]] [[Extraterrestrial life in popular culture|alien race]] in the [[DC Universe]], notable for extreme violence. They first appeared in ''Adventure Comics'' #346 (July 1966)

==Fictional history==
Extreme enemies of the [[Thanagarian]]s because of their planetary closeness <ref>''[[Legends]]''</ref>, they are sworn enemies. They superficially resemble [[human]]s, but average larger and more muscular in build, with pale [[magenta]] skin.

==In Other Media==
The Khunds are seen in the "Shadow of the Hawk" episode of the ''[[Justice League Unlimited]]'' series. They were fought by [[Hawkgirl|Shayera]] and [[Elongated Man]] back in the early years of the reformed Justice League.

The Khunds are mentioned but not seen in the "Champions" episode of the [[Legion of Super Heroes (TV series)|Legion of Super Heroes]] series.
