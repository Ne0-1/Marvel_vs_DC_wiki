The '''Khunds''' are a [[fictional]] [[Extraterrestrial life in popular culture|alien race]] in the [[DC Universe]], notable for extreme violence. They first appeared in ''Adventure Comics'' #346 (July 1966)

==Fictional history==
Extreme enemies of the [[Thanagarian]]s because of their planetary closeness (''Legends''), they are sworn enemies. They superficially resemble [[human]]s, but are much larger, with pale [[magenta]] skin.
