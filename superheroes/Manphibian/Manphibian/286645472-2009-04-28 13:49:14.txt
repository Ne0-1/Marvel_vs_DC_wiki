'''Manphibian''' is a fictional character from the [[Marvel Universe]] who first appeared in ''Legion of Monsters'' #1.  It has some resemblance to the "Gill Man"  seen in ''[[Creature from the Black Lagoon]]''.

==History==
'''Manphibian''' is an [[Extraterrestrial life in popular culture|extraterrestrial]] creature that arrived on [[Earth]] over 1000 years ago.  It is revived in 1975 and is later revealed to be captured by [[S.H.I.E.L.D.]]  It is then placed in the [[Nick Fury's Howling Commandos|Howling Commandos]] Monster Force.
