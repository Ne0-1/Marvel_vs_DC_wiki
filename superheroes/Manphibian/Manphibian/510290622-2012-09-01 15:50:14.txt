'''Manphibian''' is a fictional character in [[Marvel Comics]]. It has some resemblance to the "[[Gill-man|Gill Man]]" seen in ''[[Creature from the Black Lagoon]]''.

==Fictional character biography==
'''Manphibian''' is an [[Extraterrestrial life|extraterrestrial]] creature that arrived on [[Earth]] over 1000 years ago. It is revived in 1975 and is later revealed to be captured by [[S.H.I.E.L.D.]]{{Issue|date=September 2010}} It is then placed in the [[Nick Fury's Howling Commandos|Howling Commandos]] Monster Force.{{Issue|date=September 2010}}

==Powers and Abilities==
Manphibian possesses super-strength which is enough to tear a fifteen foot tree from the ground. Manphibian also has sharp claws and its skin is strong enough to withstand missiles.
