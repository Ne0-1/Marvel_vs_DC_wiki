'''Mister Fear''' is a [[fictional character|fictional]] [[supervillain]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel universe|universe]].  Four different individuals have played the role of Mister Fear.  Mister Fear is primarily a villain of [[Daredevil (comics)|Daredevil]], the "Man Without Fear."

'''Zoltan Drago''' was the propriator of a financially-ailing wax museum who inadvertently created the fear gas while trying to brng his wax figures to life. Drago decided that his new discovery was perfect for starting a new life as a master criminal.  He immediately teamed up with [[Ox (comics)|Ox]] of the [[Enforcers (comics)|Enforcers]] and the [[Eel (comics)|Eel]].

==Starr Saxon==
{{main|Machinesmith}}
Starr Saxon temporarily assumed the guise of Mister Fear, after killing Zoltan Drago in order to do so.
