'''Cowgirl''' ('''Deb Lowry''') is a [[fictional character]] in the [[Marvel Comics Universe]], most notably as a member of the [[Unlimited Class Wrestling Federation]].

==Publication history==
Cowgirl first appeared in ''[[Thing (comics)|The Thing]]'' vol. 1 #33 (March 1986), and was created by [[Mike Carlin]] and [[Ron Wilson (comics)|Ron Wilson]].

==Fictional character biography==
Deb Lowry was given superhuman abilities by the [[Power Broker]].  Entering the [[Unlimited Class Wrestling Federation]], she was trained by [[Auntie Freeze]] and given membership to the [[Grapplers (comics)|Grapplers]].  She witnessed [[Titania (Grapplers)|Titania]]'s defeat against [[Battleaxe (comics)|Battleaxe]], and was among the several women who tried to calm her down.  After the [[Thing (comics)|Thing]] was accused of killing Titania minutes later, Cowgirl joined her teammates in assaulting him.  However, they were soon stopped by [[Sharon Ventura]], who cleared the Thing of any suspection.

It is presumed Cowgirl continues to work with the Unlimited Class Wrestling Federation.

==Powers and abilities==
Cowgirl has superhuman strength and durability.
