'''Omerta''' (real name: '''Paulie Provensano''') is a [[fictional character]], a [[mutant (fiction)|mutant]] in the [[Marvel Universe]]. He was created by [[Scott Lobdell]] and [[Salvador Larroca]] in ''Uncanny X-Men'' #392 ([[2001]]). Paulie joined a temporary [[X-Men]] team formed by [[Jean Grey]] when most of the other X-Men were captured by [[Magneto (comics)|Magneto]]. Paulie's mutant power is an indestructible body which superhuman strength and protects him from physical harm. After the interim team's mission was completed, Paulie went his own way.  Later, during the short-lived ''Weapon X'' ongoing series, Paulie was being held in the Neverland mutant concentration camp.
