'''Arcade''' is a [[fictional character]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]].  Best described as an [[evil genius]], Arcade specializes in creative means of [[homicide]].  One of his notable methods is the use of the "Murderworld", in which an aray of mechanical devices create [[illusions]] customized for his victims.

Arcade has served as a professional [[assassin]], under the hiring of such figures as [[Doctor Doom]] and [[Shinobi Shaw]], putting his crafty skills and cunning against such heroes as the [[X-Men]], [[Spider-Man]], [[X-Force]] and [[Excalibur (comics)|Excalibur]].
