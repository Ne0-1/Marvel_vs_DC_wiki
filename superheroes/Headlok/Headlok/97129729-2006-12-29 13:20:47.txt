'''Headlok''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Universe]].  Very little is known about this mysterious menace.  He found the [[Griffin (comics)|Griffin]] roaming the [[Adirondack Mountains]], and used his mental powers to enslave the Griffin.  Headlok tricked the [[Avengers (comics)|West Coast Avengers]] into fighting the Griffin, using the distraction to try to take over their minds.  The Avenger [[Tigra]] was able to calm the Griffin's rage, and the Avengers were able to defeat Headlok.
