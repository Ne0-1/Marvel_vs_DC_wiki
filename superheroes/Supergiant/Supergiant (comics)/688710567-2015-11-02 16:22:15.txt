'''Supergiant''' is a supervillain appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]].

==Publication history==
Supergiant first appeared in ''[[Infinity (comic book)|Infinity]]'' #1 and was created by [[Jonathan Hickman]] and [[Jerome Opeña]].

==Fictional character biography==
Supergiant is a member of [[Thanos]]' [[Black Order (comics)|Black Order]]. She is a mentally unstable omnipath and telepathic parasite who seeks out intellect and devours it.<ref>''Infinity'' #1</ref>

In the search for Thanos' son [[Thane (comics)|Thane]] during the ''[[Infinity (comic book)|Infinity]]'' storyline, Supergiant and [[Corvus Glaive]] laid siege to the [[X-Mansion|Jean Grey School for Higher Learning]] and easily defeated the [[X-Men]]. They left after realizing that Thane was not there.<ref>''New Avengers'' vol. 3 #9</ref>

When the Black Order seized [[Wakanda (comics)|Wakanda]], Supergiant was left in control of [[Black Bolt]] who she would mentally order to activate the bombs the Illuminati hid in Wakanda's Necropolis.<ref>''Infinity'' #5</ref> When the [[Illuminati (comics)|Illuminati]] arrived to save Black Bolt, Supergiant used Black Bolt's ability to defeat the heroes. Upon activating the bomb, Supergiant was faced by [[Maximus (comics)|Maximus]] who had the trigger. Maximus triggered the bomb, but also used [[Lockjaw (comics)|Lockjaw]] to transport Supergiant along with the bomb to a distant uninhabited planet where she perished in the explosion of the bomb.<ref>''Infinity'' #6</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
Supergiant has telepathic abilities that allow her to mind-control anyone and feed on their intellect. In the animated series, she also seems to possess the ability to enlarge her body and increase in strength.

==In other media==
===Television===
* Supergiant appears in the ''[[Avengers Assemble (TV series)|Avengers Assemble]]'' episode "New Frontier," voiced by [[Hynden Walch]].

* Supergiant appears in the ''[[Guardians of the Galaxy (TV series)|Guardians of the Galaxy]]'' episode "Undercover Angle," voiced again by Hynden Walch. She an ex-girlfriend of [[Star-Lord]] (who stated that Supergiant broke up with him) and is shown to have size-shifting abilities. The [[Nova Corps]] had previously apprehended Supergiant at the time when the [[Guardians of the Galaxy (2008 team)|Guardians of the Galaxy]] were infiltrating the Nova Corps HQ posing as Nova Corpsman. When Supergiant was accidentally released, she fought Star-Lord, [[Drax the Destroyer]], [[Rocket Raccoon]], and [[Groot]] until she was knocked out by [[Gamora]].

===Video games===
* Supergiant appears in ''[[Marvel: Avengers Alliance]]''.
