'''Whiteout''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Universe]].  She was a [[Savage Land]] dweller, recruited by the [[Mutant (Marvel comics)|mutant]] [[Zaladane]] to join the [[Savage Land Mutates]].  With the Savage Land Mutates, she attacked the [[X-Men]] in Chile while they were looking for [[Polaris (comics)|Polaris]], but the X-Men defeated the Savage Land Mutates.

She also later appeared as one of [[Superia]]'s [[Femizons]].
