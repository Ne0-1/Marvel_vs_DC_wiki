'''Whiteout''' is a [[fictional character]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]].  She was a [[Savage Land]] dweller, recruited by the [[Mutant (Marvel comics)|mutant]] [[Zaladane]] to join the [[Savage Land Mutates]].  With the Savage Land Mutates, she attacked the [[X-Men]] in Chile while they were looking for [[Polaris (comics)|Polaris]], but the X-Men defeated the Savage Land Mutates.

She also later appeared as one of [[Superia]]'s [[Femizons]].

==Powers and abilities==
Whiteout has the ability to generate a blinding flash of white light. She can psionically select who is affected by her flash.
