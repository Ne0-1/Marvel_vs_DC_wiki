'''Turk Barrett''' is [[fictional character]], a small time criminal in the {{Marvel Universe}}. He first appears in ''[[Daredevil (Marvel Comics)|Daredevil]]'' (vol. 1) #69 (October 1970) and was created by writers [[Gary Friedrich]] and [[Roy Thomas]] and artist [[Gene Colan]].<ref name="comicbookdb1">{{cite web|url=http://www.comicbookdb.com/character.php?ID=8652 |title=Turk Barrett |publisher=Comic Book DB |date= |accessdate=2010-06-08}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|url=http://www.marvunapp.com/Appendix/turkbarretdd.htm |title=Turk (Daredevil character) |publisher=Marvunapp.com |date= |accessdate=2010-06-08}}</ref>

==Fictional character biography==
Turk has had many run-ins with Daredevil where his inept schemes are usually played as [[comic relief]].<ref name="comicbookdb1"/>

Turk steals the [[Mauler (comics)|Mauler]] armor and attempts to kill Daredevil. Barrett, however, is defeated in seconds.<ref>''Daredevil'' #176 (Nov. 1981)</ref>
