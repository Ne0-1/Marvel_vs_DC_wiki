'''Claire Temple''' is a fictional character appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]].

==Fictional character biography==
Claire Temple is the ex-wife of [[Bill Foster (comics)|Bill Foster]] and a romantic interest of [[Luke Cage]]. In her earliest portrayals, she was a nurse for Dr. Noah Burstein and his 42nd Street Storefront Clinic.<ref>*{{ Cite comic
 | writer=[[Archie Goodwin (comics)|Goodwin, Archie]]
 | penciller=[[George Tuska|Tuska, George]]
 | inker=[[Billy Graham (comics)|Graham, Billy]]
 | letterer=[[John Costanza|Costanza, John]]
 | editor=Lee, Stan
 | story=Vengeance is Mine!
 | title=[[Hero for Hire]]
 | volume=1
 | issue=2
 | date=August 1972 
 | publisher=[[Marvel Comics]]
 | location=[[New York, NY]]
 }}</ref> Claire encountered an injured Luke Cage and the two soon became romantically involved.{{issue|date=February 2015}} Claire left Luke temporarily when her ex-husband Bill Foster asked her to help him in Los Angeles.{{issue|date=February 2015}} Despite Claire's pleas for Cage not to follow her, Luke did pursue Claire to California and helped free her ex-husband from the [[Circus of Crime]].{{issue|date=February 2015}} Luke and Claire reconciled and the two returned to New York City.{{issue|date=February 2015}}

Claire grew tired of Luke's constant exposure to dangers and the two separated.{{issue|date=February 2015}} Claire went on to become a doctor and served as such in the New York City area.{{issue|date=February 2015}}

==Powers and abilities==
Claire Temple is a trained nurse.
