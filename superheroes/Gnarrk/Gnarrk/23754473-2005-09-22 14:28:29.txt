'''Gnaark''' is a [[fictional]] [[caveman]] who has been a member of various versions of the [[Teen Titans]], both in the comic book (in the early 1980s), and in the [[Teen Titans Go!|animated series]].

He lives in the Arctic Circle with his mistress, [[Kole]], who he helps out using his super-strength. He appeared in the animated series in the episode "Kole", in which they helped the Teen Titans to defeat Dr. Light and became members of the team.
