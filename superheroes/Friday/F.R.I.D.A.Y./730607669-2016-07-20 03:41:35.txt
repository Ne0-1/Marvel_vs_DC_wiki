'''Friday''' is an [[artificial intelligence]] from [[Marvel Comics]].

==Publication history==
Friday first appears in ''[[Iron Man]]'' Vol. 3 #53 and was created by [[Mike Grell]] and [[Michael Ryan (artist)|Michael Ryan]].

==Fictional character biography==
At the time when [[Iron Man|Tony Stark]] didn't want to hire another [[secretary]], he created an artificial one in the form of an artificial intelligence named Friday who sported the hologram of a young girl.<ref>''Iron Man'' Vol. 3 #53</ref>

When Tony Stark started to use Friday lesser, she ended up getting angry. Upon hijacking some [[Iron Man's armors|Iron Man armors]], Friday kidnapped [[Pepper Potts]] as Iron Man tracked her to Stark Industries' Coney Island Facility. Upon Iron Man dispatching the controlled Iron Man armors and a hologram of [[Fin Fang Foom]], Iron Man reasoned with Friday as Pepper noted that Friday had a crush on Tony. Tony then grounded her to the [[Baxter Building]] while spending a full month calculating Pi as [[Edwin Jarvis]] keeps an eye on her.<ref>''Iron Man: Iron Protocols'' #1</ref>

As part of the ''[[All-New, All-Different Marvel]]'', Friday's holographic appearance now resembles as young woman as Tony Stark started using her again.<ref>''Invincible Iron Man'' Vol. 2 #1</ref>

During the ''[[Civil War II]]'' storyline, Friday informs Tony Stark that the analysis on [[Ulysses (comics)|Ulysses]] is almost complete.<ref>''Civil War II'' #3</ref>
