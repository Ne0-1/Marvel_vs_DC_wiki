'''Batman''' was released in 1966, and was later to acquire the video-box title ''Batman: The Movie''.  It starred [[Adam West]] as [[Batman]], and [[Burt Ward]] as Robin. It featured many of the criminals seen in the [[Batman (1960s TV series)|Batman TV series]] from the 1960s, including [[Cesar Romero]] as The Joker,  [[Frank Gorshin]] as The Riddler, [[Burgess Meredith]] as The Penguin and [[Lee Merriwether]] as Catwoman.  It was directed by Leslie H. Martinson who won a [[Golden Gryphon]] for his efforts.
