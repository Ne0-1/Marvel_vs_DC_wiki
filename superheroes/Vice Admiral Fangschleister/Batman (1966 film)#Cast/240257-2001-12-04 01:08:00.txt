Batman - the movie. Starring ''Adam West'' as [[Batman]], and ''Burt Ward'' as Robin. Features many of the criminals seen in the Batman TV-series from the 1960s, Including ''Cesar Romero'' as ''The Joker'',  ''Frank Gorshin'' as ''The Riddler'', ''Burgess Meredith'' as ''The Penguin'' and ''Lee Merriwether'' as ''Catwoman''.



Released 1966 - directed by Leslie H Martinson.
