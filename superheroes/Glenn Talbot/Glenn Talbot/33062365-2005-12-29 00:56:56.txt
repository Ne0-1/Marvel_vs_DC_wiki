'''Colonel Glenn Talbot''' is a [[fictional character]] and an armed-[[villain]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]].  He was a military man who hunted [[The Incredible Hulk]] under General [[Thunderbolt Ross]].  He married [[Betty Ross]], the former love interest of the Hulk's human half Bruce Banner and the daughter of General Ross, but Betty eventually left Talbot when he sent The Hulk into the Microverse. He was killed when he was [[mind control]]led by the [[Leader (comics)|Leader]].

Glenn Talbot is played by [[Josh Lucas]] in the 2003 film ''[[Hulk (film)|Hulk]]''.
