'''Colonel Glenn Talbot''' is a [[fictional character]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]].  He was a military man who hunted [[The Incredible Hulk]] under General [[Thunderbolt Ross]].  He married [[Betty Ross]], the former love interest of the Hulk's human half Bruce Banner and the daughter of General Ross.  He was killed when he was [[mind control]]led by the [[Leader (comics)|Leader]].
