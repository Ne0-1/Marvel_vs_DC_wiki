The '''N'Garai''' are a [[fictional character|fictional race]] found in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]].  They are a race of [[Demons (Marvel Comics)|demons]] created by [[Chthon (comics)|Chthon]], and have come into conflict with the [[Midnight Sons]] and the [[X-Men]].  The N'Garai are ruled by [[Kierrok]] the Damned.
