'''Cerise''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[superhero]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]].  Although she has no powers, she is skilled in a number of species' weapons and [[martial arts]].

Cerise is an [[alien]] from the [[Shi'ar]] race. She arrived upon Earth after defecting from the empire's brutal army.  She became a member of [[Excalibur (comics)|Excalibur]] and was romantically involved with [[Nightcrawler (comics)|Nightcrawler]].  Eventually, the Shi'ar empress [[Lilandra]] pardoned Cerise for her desertion and brought her into the Empire to investigate other reports of brutality.
