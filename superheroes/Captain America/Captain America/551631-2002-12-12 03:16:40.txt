A [[Marvel Comics]] superhero, Captain America got his superhuman abilities with a sorum during WWII. He has remarkable fighting abilities and owns an indestructable shield which is made from a combination of [[Adamantium]] and [[Vibranium]].

One of his deadly foes is [[Red Skull]].

Captain America is also one of the oldest Marvel Comics characters still in use.  He was around during WWII where he did his part for the war effort and was revived from deep freeze in an issue of [[The Avengers]].
