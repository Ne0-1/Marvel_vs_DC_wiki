'''Agent X''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[superhero]] in [[Marvel Comics]]. He exists in the regular Marvel Universe, in the present timeline.

His real name is Alex Hayden, or [[Nijo (comics)|Nijo]]. He has no other known aliases, and his nationality and age are also a mystery to be revealed. His powers and abilities are fairly standard as superheroes go, consisting of augmented strength, agility, and dexterity, hyper-regenerative powers, and extreme skill at marksmanship. He is affilated with [[Agency X]].

He is a combined consciousness, residing in the body of the man named Nijo but posessing the mind and abilities of Wade Wilson, or [[Deadpool]].
