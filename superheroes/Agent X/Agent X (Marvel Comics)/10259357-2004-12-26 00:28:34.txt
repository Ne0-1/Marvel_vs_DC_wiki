[[Image:AgentX-01-01.jpg|thumb|right|Agent X]]'''Agent X''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[superhero]] in [[Marvel Comics]]' [[Marvel Universe|universe]].  He first appeared in ''Agent X'' #1, by [[Gail Simone]] and [[UDON]].

His real name is Nijo, though after suffering amnesia he was given the identity of Alex Hayden. His powers consist of augmented strength, agility, and dexterity and superhuman regeneration. He is a skilled marksman. He is affiliated with the mercenary outfit Agency X.

He is a combined consciousness, residing in the body of the man named Nijo but possessing certain abilities and mental attributes of [[Deadpool]] and the martial artist known as the [[Black Swan (comics)|Black Swan]].
