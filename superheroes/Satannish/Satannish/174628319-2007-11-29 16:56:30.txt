'''Satannish''' is a [[fictional character]] appearing in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]].  He is a very powerful extra-dimensional [[Demons (Marvel Comics)|demon]] who has clashed with [[Doctor Strange]].  Satannish is known for granting mortals mystical power in exchange for their souls. He is sometimes referred to as the son of [[Dormammu]] and the father of [[Daimon Hellstrom]] and [[Satana (Marvel Comics)|Satana]].

He has granted power to his cult, the [[Sons of Satannish]], and once empowered the [[Night Shift (comics)|Night Shift]].
