'''Satannish''' is a [[fictional character]] appearing in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]].  He is a [[Demons (Marvel Comics)|demon]] who has clashed with [[Doctor Strange]].  He is sometimes referred to as the son of [[Dormammu]] and the father of [[Daimon Hellstrom]] and [[Satana (Marvel Comics)|Satana]].
