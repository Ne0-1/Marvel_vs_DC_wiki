'''Ned Leeds''' is a [[comic book]] character in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]], part of the supporting cast of ''[[Spider-Man]]'', first introduced in [[1964]] in [[The Amazing Spider-Man]] # 25, by [[Stan Lee]] and [[Steve Ditko]]. He was [[Spider-Man | Peter Parker]]'s co-worker at the Daily Bugle. 

{{spoiler}}

He was married to one-time Spider-Man paramour [[Betty Brant]], though the two were separated at the time of his death.  At one point he was revealed to have been the [[supervillain]] [[Hobgoblin (comics)|The Hobgoblin]], though this was later [[retcon]]ned to him having been merely a stooge of the real Hobgoblin.  The character is currently deceased.
