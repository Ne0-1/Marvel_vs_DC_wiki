A supervillain named '''Hecate''' appeared in ''[[Ms. Marvel]]'' #11-13 (November 1977-January 1978), and was created by [[Chris Claremont]] and [[Sal Buscema]].

Hecate was an extradimensional female humanoid scientist and explorer, who was mistaken for the goddess [[Hecate]] when she visited Earth thousands of years ago.  She allied with and later opposed the [[Elementals (Marvel Comics)|Elementals]] while seeking the Ruby Scarab.{{Issue|date=April 2010}}

She can change shape, project energy bolts, and pull images from minds of others and make them real.
