'''Hecate''' is a fictional character in the [[Marvel Universe]].

==Publication history==
Hecate appeared in ''[[Ms. Marvel]]'' #11-13 (November 1977-January 1978), and was created by [[Chris Claremont]] and [[Sal Buscema]].

==Fictional character biography==
Hecate was an extradimensional female humanoid scientist and explorer, who was mistaken for the goddess [[Hecate]] when she visited Earth thousands of years ago.  She allied with and later opposed the [[Elementals (Marvel Comics)|Elementals]] while seeking the Ruby Scarab.

Hecate can change shape, project energy bolts, and pull images from minds of others and make them real.
