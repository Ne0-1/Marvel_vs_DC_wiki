'''Strength level:'''
Fandral is stronger than the average Asgardian male. Under optimal conditions he can lift (press)up to 35 tons.

'''Powers and abilities:'''
Fandral is a master swordsman, and excellent overall battlefield warrior. He is highly proficient in the use of all edged weapons, and his dexterity, speed, and agility are all significantly greater than the average Asgardian male. Like all Asgardians, Fandral's bone, skin and muscle are roughly 3 times denser than a typical human, giving him an extremely high resistance to injury. 

'''Description:'''
Fandral the Dashing is a Marvel Comics Character and a charter member of the Warrior's Three; a trio of Asgardian adventureres consisting of Fandral, [[Hogun]] the Grim, and [[Volstagg]] the Voluminous. They are supporting cast in Marvel's Thor comics and usually provide comic relief and side adventures.
