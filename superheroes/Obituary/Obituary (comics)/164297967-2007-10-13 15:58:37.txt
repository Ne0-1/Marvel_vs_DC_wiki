'''Obituary''' ('''Milton Peterson''') is a [[fictional character]], a [[Mutant (Marvel comics)|mutant]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] Universe. His first appearance was in ''[[X-Statix]]'' #1.

==Fictional character biography==
Obituary is a member of America's newest mutant superhero team, [[O-Force]]. Like all potential recruits, Obituary had to compete in a live [[television program]], hosted by [[Solomon O'Sullivan]], to win a place on the O-Force team.

===The Initiative===
Milton is being considered as a "potential recruit" for the [[Avengers: The Initiative|Initiative]] program, according to ''Civil War: Battle Damage Report''.

==Powers and abilities==
This mutant appears to have enhanced/superhuman strength and durability.
