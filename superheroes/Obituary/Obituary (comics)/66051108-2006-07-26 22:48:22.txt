'''Obituary''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[mutant (fictional)|mutant]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] Universe. His first appearance was in ''X-Statix #1''.

Obituary is a member of America's newest mutant superhero team, [[O-Force]]. Like all potential recruits, Obituary had to compete in a live [[television program]], hosted by [[Solomon O'Sullivan]], to win a place on the O-Force team. It's unknown if Obituary lost or kept his mutant powers after the M-Day.

==Powers==
This mutant appears to have enhanced/superhuman strength and durability.
