'''Val Rhymin'''<ref name="runv3#1">{{Comic book reference | Writer = [[Terry Moore]] | Penciller =  [[Humberto Ramos]] | Inker = [[Christina Strain]] | Story = Dead Wrong | Title = Runaways | Volume = 3 | Issue = 1 | Date = August 2008 | Publisher = [[Marvel Comics]] }}</ref> is a [[fictional character]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] award-winning series, ''[[Runaways (comics)|Runaways]]''. He was created by author [[Terry Moore]] and artist [[Humberto Ramos]], and debuted in ''Runaways'' #1 ([[2008 in comics|August 2008]]), at the start of the series' third volume. Val first appearead as a worker in a radio station. Although he played a minor role in ''Runaways''<nowiki>'s</nowiki> "[[Dead Wrong (comics)|Dead Wrong]]" story arc, Val was the main villain in the following story arc, "[[List of Runaways story arcs#Rock Zombies|Rock Zombies]]".

==Fictional character biography==
When the [[Runaways (comics)|Runaways]] return to [[Los Angeles]], [[Nico Minoru]] asks [[Chase Stein]] to find a job. When they head to a shopping mall, Chase tries to bag a radio station job with Val Rhymin, his idol. Meanwhile, Val argues with his manager, saying his manager doesn't let him have any freedom. Curiously, it is now when Val's boss has his seizure, and falls over the mall balcony to the floor below. Before he hits, [[Klara Prast]] saves him by making the mall plant-life break his fall.<ref name="summaryv3#1">{{cite web|url=http://runaways.mergingminds.org/showarticle.asp?section=10&fldAuto=439|title=Runaways (third series) #1|first=Fantomex|accessdate=2009-01-23}}</ref>
