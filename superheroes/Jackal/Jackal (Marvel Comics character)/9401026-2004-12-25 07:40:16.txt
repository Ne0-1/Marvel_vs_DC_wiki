The '''Jackal''' ('''Miles Warren''') is a [[Marvel Comics]] [[Universe]] [[supervillain]].

Dr. Miles Warren was once a professor of [[biochemistry]] at [[Empire State University]]. He fell in love with one of his students, [[Gwen Stacy]]. After her death, he became completely insane, developing the personality of the Jackal. He also created a clone of Gwen, and later cloned Peter Parker; years later, this clone would become the [[Scarlet Spider]]. Both the Jackal and the clone of Gwen were manipulated by the [[Green Goblin]] in the [[Clone Saga]].
