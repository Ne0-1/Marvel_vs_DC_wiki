'''Ulysses X. Lugman''', better known as '''the Slug''' was a criminal in the [[Marvel Universe]]. His drug dealing business was once toppled by the [[Nomad (comics)|Nomad]], but he seems to have rebounded, as he was shown in a meeting between the United States major criminals empires in order to divide the [[Kingpin (comics)|Kingpin]]'s former territory.
