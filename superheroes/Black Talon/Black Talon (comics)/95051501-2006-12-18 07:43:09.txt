The '''Black Talon''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[supervillain]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]]. He is a [[voodoo]] priest who can create and control [[zombies]]. He wears a costume which resembles a chicken, and has an upside-down cross painted on his chest. The Black Talon first appeared in Strange Tales #173 in which he battled [[Brother Voodoo]]. The Black Talon has also battled the [[Avengers (comics)|Avengers]], [[West Coast Avengers]], [[She-Hulk]], and [[Deadpool (comics)|Deadpool]] (in which he was viciously mocked for his chicken costume). It was the Black Talon who resurrected [[Wonder Man]] in Avengers #152.
