The '''Dark Riders''', and also known as the '''Riders of the Storm''' are a team of [[comic book]] [[supervillain]]s composed of [[mutant (fictional)|mutants]] and [[Inhumans]], in [[Marvel Comics]]' [[Marvel Universe|universe]], who are devoted to the philosophy of [[survival of the fittest]]. They were created by [[Chris Claremont]], [[Jim Lee]], and [[Whilce Portacio]]; in ''[[X-Factor]]'' Vol. 1 #65 (April 1991).
