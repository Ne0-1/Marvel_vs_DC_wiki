'''John Garrett''' is a fictional character appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]].

==Fictional character biography==
{{expand section|date=February 2015}}
John Garrett was a cybernetically enhanced agent, known for his lack of discipline.<ref>''Elektra: Assassin'' #2 (September 1986)</ref> He later becomes the President of the United States of America by the unwanted help of [[Elektra (comics)|Elektra]], who put his mind into the body of the (fictional) President Ken Wind, in order to save the world.{{issue|date=February 2015}}

==Powers and abilities==
John Garrett was a trained S.H.I.E.L.D. agent.
