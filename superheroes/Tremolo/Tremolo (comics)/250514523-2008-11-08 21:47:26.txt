'''Tremolo''' is a fictional [[Mutant (Marvel comics)|mutant]] character in [[Marvel Comics]]' [[shared universe]], the [[Marvel Universe]].

==Fictional character biography==
Tremolo was a member of female [[Mutant (Marvel comics)|mutant]] terrorists. She was resciuted by [[Cyber (comics)|Cyber]] to commit acts of [[extortion]], and worked for a notorious [[drug cartel]]. A other member of group, [[Shrew (comics)|Shrew]] eventually quit the team and agreed to [[testify]] against the cartel for [[immunity (legal)|immunity]]. Tremolo and other The Belles, upon discovering their former teammate's betrayal, sought out to kill Shrew. The government then placed Shrew under the protection of [[X-Factor (comics)|X-Factor]]. The Belles ambushed X-Factor in a hotel, and in the ensuing battle. She was initially driven off by Wolfsbane, and was leater punched out by Quicksilver and taking them into custody with other defeated Hell's. 

She is currently depowered due to events of [[M-Day]].

==Powers==
Tremolo radiates sound and sonic waves around her body at all times, allowing her to fly, giving off explosive shock blasts, and vibro-shattering anything within range.
