'''Tremolo''' is a fictional [[Mutant (Marvel comics)|mutant]] character in [[Marvel Comics]]' [[shared universe]], the [[Marvel Universe]].

==Fictional character biography==
Tremolo was a member of female [[Mutant (Marvel comics)|mutant]] terrorists. She was resuscitated by [[Cyber (comics)|Cyber]] to commit acts of [[extortion]], and worked for a notorious [[drug cartel]]. A other member of group, [[Shrew (comics)|Shrew]] eventually quit the team and agreed to [[testify]] against the cartel for [[immunity (legal)|immunity]].{{Issue|date=September 2009}} Tremolo and other The Belles, upon discovering their former teammate's betrayal, sought out to kill Shrew. The government then placed Shrew under the protection of [[X-Factor (comics)|X-Factor]].{{Issue|date=September 2009}} The Belles ambushed X-Factor in a hotel, and in the ensuing battle. She was initially driven off by Wolfsbane, and was later punched out by Quicksilver and taking them into custody with other defeated Hell's.{{Issue|date=September 2009}}

She is currently depowered due to events of [[M-Day]].{{Issue|date=September 2009}}

==Powers==
Tremolo radiates sound and sonic waves around her body at all times, allowing her to fly, giving off explosive shock blasts, and vibro-shattering anything within range.
