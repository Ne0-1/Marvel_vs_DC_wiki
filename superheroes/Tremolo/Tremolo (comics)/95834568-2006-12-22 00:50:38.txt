'''Tremolo''' is a fictional [[mutant (fictional)|mutant]] character in the [[Marvel Comics]] Universe.

==Powers==
Tremolo is a mutant that had the ability to radiate sonic waves and fly.  She is currently depowered.

*There is a member of the [[Great Game]] that is also called '''Tremolo''', that first appeared in ''New Thunderbolts'' #2
