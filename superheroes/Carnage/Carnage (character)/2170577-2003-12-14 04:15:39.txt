'''Carnage''' is the name of a [[fictional character|fictional villain]] in the ''[[Spiderman]]'' comicbook. He is a [[serial killer]] who has merged with an [[alien]] symbiote. The symbiote endows him with superhuman strength and the ability to wield sharp appendages, which grow from the costume.  Carnage is 5 times as strong as Spiderman.
