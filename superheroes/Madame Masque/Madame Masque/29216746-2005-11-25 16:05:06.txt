'''Madame Masque''' (Whitney Frost) is a [[fictional character]], an occasional love-interest and villain of [[Iron Man]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]].

The daughter of [[Count Nefaria]], she has a scar on her face she hides behind her distinctive brass full-face mask, and has faked her death several times through the use of [[clone]]s.
