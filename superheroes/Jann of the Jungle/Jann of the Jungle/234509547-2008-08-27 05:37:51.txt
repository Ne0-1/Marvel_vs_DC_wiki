'''Jann of the Jungle''' is a [[fictional character|fictional]] [[comic book]] [[jungle girl (stock character)|jungle girl]] [[protagonist]] from [[Marvel Comics]]' 1950s predecessor [[Atlas Comics (1950s)|Atlas Comics]]. The [[anthology]] title ''[[Jungle Tales]]'' was renamed ''Jann of the Jungle'' starting with issue #8 in November 1955. The series continued to issue #17 in June 1957.

Since then, she has made a handful of appearances, among them an [[alternate history (fiction)|alternate history]] in  ''[[What If (comics)|What If]]'' #9 (June 1978).

Jann of the Jungle should not be confused with the [[Hanna-Barbera]] [[animated television series]] character [[Jana of the Jungle]].
