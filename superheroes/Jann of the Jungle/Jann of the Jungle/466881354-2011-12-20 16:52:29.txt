'''Jann of the Jungle''' is a [[fictional character|fictional]] [[comic book]] [[jungle girl (stock character)|jungle girl]] [[protagonist]] created by writer [[Don Rico]] and artist [[Jay Scott Pike]] in the [[anthology]] title ''[[Jungle Tales]]'' #1 (Sept. 1954), published by [[Marvel Comics]]' 1950s predecessor, [[Atlas Comics (1950s)|Atlas Comics]].<ref>[http://www.comics.org/issue/11556/ ''Jungle Tales'' #1 (Sept. 1954)] at the [[Grand Comics Database]]</ref>

''Jungle Tales'' was renamed ''Jann of the Jungle'' starting with issue #8 (Nov. 1955). The series continued to issue #17 (June 1957).

Since then, the character has made a handful of appearances, among them a non-canonical, [[alternate history (fiction)|alternate history]] story in  ''[[What If (comics)|What If]]'' #9 (June 1978).
