'''Virman Vundabar''' is one of the [[New Gods]], a [[Race (fantasy)|fictional race]] created by [[Jack Kirby]] for [[DC Comics]]. A resident of [[Apokolips]], he is a minion of [[Granny Goodness]], and models his personality and schemes on [[Prussia]]n military appearance and precision. He is a nemesis of [[Mister Miracle]]. His niece, Malice, is a junior member of the [[Female Furies]] who can control a shadow monster named Chessure. He is occasionally (as in the [[Elseworlds|Elesworld]] Superman: The Dark Side) depicted as a double of [[Adolf Hitler]].

== Trivia ==
The name "Vundabar" is a corruption of the German word "wunderbar", which means "wonderful". This name was given to him by [[Granny Goodness]].

== Other Media ==
Vundabar has appeared in an episode of ''[[Justice League Unlimited]]''. In that episode, he was in control of [[Apokolips]] when [[Darkseid]] disappeared and held [[Kalibak]] as a prisoner. He was voiced by [[Arte Johnson]]. He also makes a cameo appearance in the Justice League Unlimited episode ''Alive'', where he tried to take over Apokolips from Granny Goodness and the Female Furies.

== Note ==
His name has varied over the years between ''Vundabar'' and ''Vunderbarr''.  His name in his first appearance was Vundabar.
