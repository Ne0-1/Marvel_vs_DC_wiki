The original Eel first appeared in ''[[Strange Tales]]'' #112 (September 1963). '''Leopold Stryke''' and his brother Jordan, who was the original [[Viper (comics)|Viper]], were members of the first [[Serpent Squad]]. Stryke was also a teammate of [[Plantman]], [[Scarecrow (Marvel Comics)|Scarecrow]], [[Unicorn (comics)|Unicorn]] and [[Porcupine (comics)|Porcupine]] when they worked for [[Count Nefaria]]. He was killed in the [[Madripoor]] Bloodsport Tournament by [[Toad (comics)|Toad]].

The second Eel was a member of the second [[Crimson Cowl]]'s [[Masters of Evil]]. He has recently been apprehended by the [[Thunderbolts (comics)|Thunderbolts]].

==Powers and Abilities==

Both Eels wore special suits that were coated with an extremely slippery [[gel]], and could generate a powerful [[electrical shock]].
