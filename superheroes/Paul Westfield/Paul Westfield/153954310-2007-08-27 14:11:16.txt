'''Paul Westfield''' is a fictional DC Comics character.  He appeared in Superman in numerous occasions and contributed in creating [[Superboy]], and [[Auron (comics)]]. He was first thought to have been Superboy's genetic donor but later it was discovered that Superboy was really a clone of Superman and Lex Luthor. He was killed by Dabney Donovan in the main DC universe and during and accident in [[Hypertime]] all of his alternate versions were erased from existence. He was survived by his daughter [[Amanda Spence]] who was part of group who cloned Superboy and created [[Match (comics)]].  He is considered to be a Bureaucratic member of [[Project Cadmus]].
