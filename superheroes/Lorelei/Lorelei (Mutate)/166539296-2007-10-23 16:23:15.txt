'''Lorelei''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[Mutant (Marvel comics)|mutant]] in the [[Marvel Comics Universe]] whose first appearance was in ''X-Men (1st series) #63''.

==Fictional character biography==
Lorelei is a [[Savage Land]] native until [[Magneto (comics)|Magneto]] turns her into a mutated siren, whose voice can put men into a [[hypnosis|hypnotic trance]] (''X-Men'' #63) and she is placed in his [[Savage Land Mutates]] group. At the end of the comic, the machine that Magneto had used to empower his Savage Land creations is destroyed. As a result, Lorelei loses her siren powers. 

Some time later, she was seen in ''[[Defenders (comics)|Defenders]]'' #15&ndash;16 with her powers somehow restored.  She made a further appearance with the [[Brotherhood of Mutants]] in ''[[Champions (comics)|Champions]]'' #17. 

Years later, Lorelei was in ''[[Captain America]]'' (1st Series) #415&ndash;417.  She appeared on a trading card in the [[Marvel vs. DC card game]], and appeared briefly in the first ''[[New Avengers (comic)|New Avengers]]'' story arc.

==Notes==
Other [[Marvel Comics|Marvel]] characters known as '''Lorelei''' have been:
*The [[Asgardians (comics)|Asgardian]] sister of [[Enchantress (Marvel Comics)|Amora the Enchantress]]. [[Lorelei (Asgardian)|Lorelei]] was a former pawn of [[Pluto (Marvel Comics)|Pluto]] and succeeded in briefly seducing [[Thor (Marvel Comics)|Thor]] by tricking him into drinking golden mead and she first appeared in ''Thor'' vol. 1 #337
*[[Mantis (Marvel Comics)|Mantis]] briefly took the name '''Lorelei''' as revealed in the ''Official Handbook of the Marvel Universe: Avengers 2005''.
*[[Lorelei Travis (comics)|Lorelei Travis]] is also known as '''Lorelei'''.  She first appeared in ''District X'' #1 and is one of the [[198 Files]].

==Powers and abilities==
Lorelei can hypnotically paralyze men by singing.
