'''Bast''' is a [[fictional character]] appearing in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]], based loosely on the [[Bast (goddess)|Bast]] of [[Egyptian mythology]].

The cat-headed Bast and the lion-headed [[Sekhmet (Marvel Comics)|Sekhmet]] are the children of the gods Shu and Tefnut.

The Panther God of [[Wakanda (Marvel)|Wakanda]] is apparently an aspect of Bast.
