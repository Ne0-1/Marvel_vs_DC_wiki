'''Prester John''' is a [[fictional character]] appearing in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]], based loosely on the mythical Christian king [[Prester John]]. 

Prester John first appeared in ''[[Fantastic Four]]'' Vol.1 #54 (September 1966). He has mostly appeared as a villain, opposing [[Thor (Marvel Comics)|Thor]] and [[Deadpool (comics)|Deadpool]] as well as the Fantastic Four. 

Prester John is a centuries-old explorer who once was an ally of King Richard the Lionhearted, and was once ruler of an unknown kingdom in Eastern Asia.  He is the possessor of the [[Evil Eye of Avalon|Evil Eye]], a mystic object of power which was bestowed upon him in the extra-dimensional land of [[Avalon (comics)|Avalon]], and which can project vast amounts of energy.

Currently, Prester John is allied with the would-be [[messiah]] [[Cable (comics)|Cable]], serving as the "Head of Multi-Religious Studies" for the island nation [[Providence (comics)|Providence]].
