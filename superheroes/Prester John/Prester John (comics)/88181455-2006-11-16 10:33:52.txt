'''Prester John''' is a [[fictional character]] appearing in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]], based loosely on the mythical Christian king [[Prester John]]. 

Prester John first appeared in ''[[Fantastic Four]]'' Vol.1 #54 (September 1966). He has mostly appeared as a villain, opposing [[Thor (Marvel Comics)|Thor]] and [[Deadpool (comics)|Deadpool]] as well as the Fantastic Four. Currently, Prester John is allied with the would-be [[messiah]] [[Cable (comics)|Cable]], serving as the "Head of Multi-Religious Studies" for the island nation [[Providence (comics)|Providence]].
