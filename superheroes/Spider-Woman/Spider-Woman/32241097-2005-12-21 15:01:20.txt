'''Spider-Woman''' is the codename adopted by a number of fictional characters, all published by Marvel Comics and based on the [[Spider-Man]] character:

* [[Spider-Woman (Jessica Drew)]], the original and current Spider-Woman
* [[Spider-Woman (Julia Carpenter)]], a former Avengers member who appeared in the 1990s ''Iron Man'' cartoon
* [[Spider-Woman (Mattie Franklin)]], who briefly impersonated Spider-Man before receiving her own short-lived comic series
* [[Spider-Woman (Charlotte Witter)]], the only supervillainess to use the name

Similarly-named characters include:

* [[Spider-Girl]] (May Parker), daughter of Spider-Man in an alternate future
