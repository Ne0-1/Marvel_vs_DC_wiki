'''Lucifer''' is a [[comic book]] [[supervillain]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]]. 

An [[extraterrestrial life|alien]], he first came to Earth as the vanguard of an [[alien invasion]], which was foiled by the young Charles Xavier (later [[Professor X]], leader of the [[X-Men]]). In retaliation, Lucifer dropped an enormous stone block on Xavier, leaving him wheelchair-bound.
Lucifer made several later attempts to conquer Earth, all foiled by the X-Men.
