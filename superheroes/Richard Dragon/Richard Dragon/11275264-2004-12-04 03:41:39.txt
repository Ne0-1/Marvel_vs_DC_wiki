'''''Richard Dragon, [[Kung Fu]] Master''''', is a [[DC Comics]] [[comic book]]. 

In it, the eponymous hero was a [[thief]] who was trained in [[martial arts]] and decides to use his abilities for good. With his comrades [[Lady Shiva]], [[Bronze Tiger]] and [[Batman]], he is one of the top [[martial artist]]s in the [[DC Universe]].

After the character's title was cancelled, the character was a [[supporting character]] in the [[1980s]] series, ''[[The Question]]''.  He trained the title character both in martial arts and in [[eastern philosophy]].

In [[2004]], the character's title was revived.
