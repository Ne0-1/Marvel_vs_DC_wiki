'''Tonaja''' is a [[fictional]]  [[superhero]] published by [[Marvel Comics]]. She first appeared in ''[[Inhuman|Inhumans]]'' #2 (December 1998), and was created by [[Paul Jenkins]] and [[Jae Lee]].

==Fictional character biography==
She is a member of the Inhumans and the lover of Dewoz. She was also part of the delegation sent to Earth, which allowed her to attend human school. Being beautiful to Inhumans, she learns humans don't consider her that.{{Fact|date=February 2007}}
