'''Dino Manelli''' is a [[fictional character]] in the [[Marvel Comics]] Universe. His first appearance was in ''[[Sgt. Fury and his Howling Commandos]]'' vol. 1 #1.

==Fictional character biography==
Before the war, '''Dino Manelli''' was a charismatic actor who was fluent in [[German language|German]] and [[Italian language|Italian]].  Manelli was a member of the original Howling Commandos and fought alongside the team during [[World War II]].  He was briefly replaced by [[Eric Koenig]] when he was sent on a special mission, which was helping to organize another team known as the [[Dirty Dozen (comics)|Dirty Dozen]].  He later returned to the Howlers but was briefly off-duty when he was wounded.  After the war, he retired from acting.  He later assisted [[S.H.I.E.L.D.]] following the Deltite Affair.

==Other media==
===Marvel Ultimate Alliance===
In Marvel Ultimate Alliance, the son of Dino Manelli makes a quick cameo: Dino Manelli Jr. is rescued by your team, and in his journal he speaks of his time joining [[S.H.I.E.L.D.]]. He also has a special conversation with Deadpool (who insults Dino for being stupid and asks for money in return of rescuing him).
