'''Star Thief''' ('''Ditmal Pirval''') is a [[fiction]]al [[Marvel Comics]] [[character (arts)|character]].  

==Fictional character biography==
Star Thief was struck by a bolt of energy from space. This bolt kills his family and gives him extensive energy based [[Superpower (ability)|powers]].  This also motivates him to attempt to end all [[human]] exploration of space.  He was dissuaded from this goal after a violent confrontation with the [[New Warriors]] and the [[Inhumans]].{{issue|date=May 2011}}  In a more thoughtful mood, he lived with and learned from the Inhumans for a time,{{issue|date=May 2011}} but has not been seen during the Inhumans' last few years of activity.
