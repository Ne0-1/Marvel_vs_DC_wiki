'''Aleytys "Lee" Forrester''' is a comic book character created by [[Marvel comics]]. Her first appearance was in ''Uncanny X-Men'' #143. 

== Fictional character biography ==

Forrester works as a ship captain of the fishing trawler, ''Arcadia'', and had a brief romantic relationship with [[Cyclops (comics)|Cyclops]] of the [[X-Men]], a.k.a. [[Scott Summers]]. Lee was targeted by the supernatural being [[D'Spayre]] after Lee's father, Jock, committed suicide. Lee learned that Scott was the mutant superhero Cyclops when Lee and Scott were shipwrecked on an island inhabited by [[Magneto (comics)|Magneto]] in the Bermuda Triangle. Later, after Scott broke off their relationship, she was involved with Magneto for a short time, after she rescues him from drowning. After this, she became interested in [[Cable (comics)|Cable]], but she did not pursue it, finding out that he was the son of her former boyfriend, Cyclops.
