'''Aleytys "Lee" Forrester''' is a comic book character created by [[Marvel comics]]. Her first appearance was in ''Uncanny X-Men'' #143. 

==Fictional character biography==
Forrester is a ship captain of the fishing trawler, ''Arcadia'', and hads a brief romantic relationship with [[Cyclops (comics)|Cyclops]] of the [[X-Men]], a.k.a. [[Scott Summers]], when he takes a leave of absence following the death of [[Phoenix (comics)|Phoenix]]. Lee was targeted by the supernatural being [[D'Spayre]] after Lee's father, Jock, committed suicide over the loss of his wife, but he was defeated by Cyclops, who had accompanied her, and his principal enemy, the [[Man-Thing]].

Lee eventually learns that Scott is the mutant superhero Cyclops when Lee and Scott are shipwrecked on an island inhabited by [[Magneto (comics)|Magneto]] in the Bermuda Triangle. Later, after Scott breaks off their relationship, she becomes involved with Magneto for a short time, after she rescues him from a shark attack. After this, she becomes interested in [[Cable (comics)|Cable]], but she does not pursue it, when she learns that he is the son of her former boyfriend, Cyclops.
