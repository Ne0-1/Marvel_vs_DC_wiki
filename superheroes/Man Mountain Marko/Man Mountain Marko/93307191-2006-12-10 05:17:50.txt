'''Man Mountain Marko''' is the name of a [[Marvel Comics]] supervillain.  Michael "Man Mountain" was a member of [[The Maggia]].  He was introduced as the right hand man of [[Silvermane]] in the 73rd [[Amazing Spiderman]] comic.  He was established as a second rate villain immediately, having only one superpower.  He was allied with Silvermane, [[Caesar Cicero]], [[Eel]], and [[Nightshade]].  his enemies are Spiderman, [[Power Man]], [[Ironfist]], [[Thunderbolt]], and [[Boomerang]].
