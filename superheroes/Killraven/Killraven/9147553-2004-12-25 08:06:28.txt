'''Killraven''', the "Warrior of the Worlds", is a [[fictional character]] in the future of the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe]].  He the leader of the Freemen, a group of [[freedom fighters]] against the [[Martians]] who have conquered the [[Earth]] in their timeline - the same Martians from ''[[The War of the Worlds]]''.  He first appeared in ''[[Amazing Adventures]]'' volume 2 #18.
