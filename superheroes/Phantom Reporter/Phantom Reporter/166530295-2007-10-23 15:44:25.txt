'''The Phantom Reporter''' ('''Dick Jones''') was a Timely Comics Golden Age superhero.  He appeared in ''Daring Mystery Comics #3'', and will reappear in ''[[The Twelve (comic)|The Twelve]]''.<ref>[http://www.comicbookresources.com/news/newsitem.cgi?id=11312 Mystery Men's Dozen: Brevoort Talks "The Twelve"], July 26, 2007, [[Comic Book Resources]]</ref> <ref>[http://forum.newsarama.com/showthread.php?t=125392 12 Days of the Twelve: The Phantom Reporter], August 15, 2007, [[Newsarama]]</ref> He had no known superpowers.

==Fictional character biography==
Dick Jones, The Phantom Reporter's alias, was "ex-All American fullback, ex-collegiate boxing, wrestling, [and] fencing champ."  He actually has three identities: Dick Jones, reporter; Van Engen, millionaire; and Phantom Reporter, costumed crime fighter.
