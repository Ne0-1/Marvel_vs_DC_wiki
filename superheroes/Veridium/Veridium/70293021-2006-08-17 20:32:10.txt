'''Veridium''' is a fictional alien metal in the [[DC Comics]] [[DC Universe|universe]]. In the [[1993]] ''[[Metal Men]]'' minseries, it was used to build a new [[eponym]]ous member of the [[Metal Men]], housing the mind of the Metal Men's creator [[Will Magnus]].  It has properties of energy and heat absorbance.
