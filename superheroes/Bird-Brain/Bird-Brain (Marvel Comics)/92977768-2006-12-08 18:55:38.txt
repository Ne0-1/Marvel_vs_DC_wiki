'''Bird-Brain''' is a [[fictional character]], a [[superhero]] from [[Marvel Comics]].  He first appeared in ''[[New Mutants]]'' #55, and was first fully seen in [[New Mutants]]'' #56.

==Character history==

Bird-Brain was one of a number of "Ani-Mates", creatures created by an insane geneticist named the [[Ani-Mator]], combining the characteristics of human beings and other animals.  The Ani-Mator subjected his creations to a number of cruel and deadly experiments, but Bird-Brain escaped the Ani-Mator's island and befriended the [[New Mutants]].  The New Mutants returned to the island with Bird-Brain to stop the mad scientist's experiments.  The Ani-Mator, attempting to shoot [[Wolfsbane (comics)|Wolfsbane]], instead killed the New Mutant [[Cypher (comics)|Cypher]].  [[Magik (comics)|Magik]] exiled the Ani-Mator to the dimension of [[Limbo (comics)|Limbo]] in retailiation.  Once freed from the Ani-Mator's tyranny, Bird-Brain decided to remain on the island with the other Ani-Mates.
