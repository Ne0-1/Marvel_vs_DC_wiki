'''The Mindless Ones''' are [[fiction]]al [[monster]]s appearing in the [[Marvel Comics]] [[Marvel Universe|universe.]] Their first appearance was in [http://www.docstrange.com/profiles/mindless.asp Strange Tales #127]

They are extra-dimensional creatures summoned via magic to do the bidding of others. (They have no will of their own.)

The appear to have a thick, roughly humanoid shape standing taller than most men and a hide that appears to be made of rock. The have no faces to speak of except a single glowing eye from which they can fire a blast of energy. As befits their appearance they are inhumanly strong and invulnerable. 
They are most often seen in the employ of '''[[Dormammu]]''', although they have also been utilised by others such as '''[[Dr. Doom]]''', '''Dr. Midas''' (In the pages of [[Noh-Varr|Marvel Boy]]) and the Dormammu parody '''Rorkannu''' (in [[Nextwave|Nextwave: Agents of H.A.T.E.]])
