'''The Man-Killer''' is a [[fictional character|fictional]] [[supervillain]] in [[Marvel Comics]]' [[Marvel Universe|universe]], who first appeared in ''[[Marvel Team-Up]]'' volume 1 #8 ([[April]] [[1973]]) by [[Gerry Conway]] and [[Jim Mooney]].

'''Katrina Luisa Van Horne''' was an [[Olympics|Olympic]] [[skiing|skiier]] and is a militant [[feminism|feminist]].  After engaging in an argument with anti-Women's Liberation skier Karl Lubbings, the two took their disagreement to the slopes.  Katrina was an Olympic-level skier, but Lubbings cut her off and both skiers plunged off the mountain.  Katrina was severely injured and disfigured.  She was fitted with a powered [[exoskeleton]], and took the name Man-Killer.  She has worked freelance and as an agent for [[Hydra (comics)|Hydra]].  She has been a member of the [[Masters of Evil]] under the [[Crimson Cowl]].  As '''Amazon''', she was briefly a member of the [[Thunderbolts (comics)|Thunderbolts]] under [[Hawkeye (comics)|Hawkeye]].
